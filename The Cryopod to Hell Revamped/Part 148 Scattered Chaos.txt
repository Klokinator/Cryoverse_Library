Part 148: Scattered Chaos

Smealum blinks his eyes. Grogginess slows his thoughts for a few moments, along with an ache at the base of his neck. He pulls himself into a sitting position and freezes.

In the middle of the clearing, his brother, Shepherd, lays flat on his stomach. A human male with dark skin and black hair slowly traces his fingers over the acolyte's back. A grim expression sits on the man's face, even as he tilts his head up to meet Smealum's gaze.

"You're awake."

The human's voice echoes tree bark scraping against concrete. Dozens of scars line his face, evidence of the many battles he's fought. On the ground beside him sits a perfectly straight staff, one which bends around at the top into an upside-down 'U' shape.

Smealum spares only an instant to glance around the clearing. The three demons who attacked him and Shepherd lie piled up in the corner, a small fire scorching their headless bodies to ash. Blood coats the ground in a few patches here and there, but otherwise, the clearing appears the same as it did before their appearance.

The Animus slowly stands up. He leans against a tree behind him for support, while keeping a cautious eye on the human caring for Shepherd. "Who are you?"

"I am Jepthath, a humble shepherd."

The man stares at Smealum like a grey eagle might a field hare. Not a trace of worry crosses his face as the demon fully regains consciousness.

"A shepherd? The same as...?"

"Aye."

Jepthath offers no further elaboration. Instead, he lowers his eyes to Shepherd's back. Horrible scars line the spots where his wings once existed. Shepherd takes several slow, stuttering gasps, but he doesn't awaken from the trance binding his mind.

Jepthath purses his lips. "Rather than ask my name, wouldn't you like to know about your brother's condition?"

"I would, yes," Smealum replies. He takes a few steps toward Jepthath, then gingerly kneels a few feet away, keeping his distance from the human.

"That Brute tore out Shepherd's wings. They disintegrated into particles of mana after a few minutes, and now, nothing remains. The wings of an angel are what connects them to their control of mana. As such, he has lost all his ability to control and manipulate spiritual energy."

Jepthath keeps his voice low. Considering the state of humans in this era, it surprises me how well-spoken he is. Most others seem to communicate by grunting and pointing their fingers, but Jepthath speaks like a well-educated noble from thousands of years in the future.

Or, perhaps, more like that of an angel or Titan.

Smealum nods. A grim look covers his face. "Will my brother... die?"

"No. He is still immortal. Because of the loss of his wings, his body will cease to release mana over time. While most angels must replace their spiritual energy to retain their immortality, Shepherd will stay as he is right this moment... forever."

Smealum exhales a sigh of relief. "Haah... so... he will survive."

"Survive, yes. Thrive? No. An angel without wings is no longer an angel at all. From what my master tells me, the Archangels refer to Those Without Wings as Nephilim. He will stay their brother, but will forever reside at the bottom of angel society as a serf."

"That's... that's awful," Smealum murmurs. "Not even demons treat their kin in such-"

Jepthath's blue eyes suddenly glow intensely, as he glares in rage at Smealum. "**Speak not of demons in my presence.**"

Smealum freezes in place as the piercing gaze of the human before him sends chills down his back. "W-what? Why? Who are... you?"

Jepthath's eyes continue to glow for a few moments before reverting to their natural form. He glares at Smealum, and a bitter look of resentment appears in his eyes. "Know this, half-breed. I spared you for two reasons. For one, you appeared to be on the side of this angel, defending him against the others of your kind. For two, because you are not a full-blooded demon, I do not feel the immediate need to end your existence. Even so... the hatred in my heart tells me to crush you, just as it does your half-brothers and sisters."

Confusion appears in Smealum's eyes. "Half-breed? What do you mean? Am I not a demon?"

Jepthath narrows his eyes. "You are, and you are not. Perhaps, once, you were a full-blooded member of your species. However, within you, I sense something else; A more primal power. Like that of mother nature, you share energy with the creatures of the Earth and all life across the cosmos. I feel that, were I to kill you, it would be a great tragedy."

"But..." Jepthath continues. "...even so... the hatred I feel for demons... it threatens to consume me. Despite the lack of horns and the blood-red skin they all share, you still reek of their funk."

Jepthath finishes tracing his fingers around the wounds on Shepherd's back, grabs his staff, and abruptly rises to his feet. Smealum mirrors him, if only so the human won't tower over him.

"Why do you hate us?" Smealum asks. "Did... did demons hurt you in some way?"

"Aye."

Jepthath's face contorts into a vile expression of disgust and rage.

"They took... _everything_ from me. Were it not for my master; I might have cast my life away. Because of him, I have a purpose."

"And what might that purpose be?" Smealum asks.

Jepthath stares through Smealum, as if conjuring a hundred memories, each one of which causes him awful, agonizing pain.

The human turns on his heel and starts to walk away, leaving Smealum behind.

"I will _kill_ all the demons."

Smealum doesn't reply.

After a few moments, Jepthath vanishes into the forest cover, leaving the Animus and Nephilim by themselves.

It takes several seconds for Smealum to recover. He slowly lowers his gaze to his brother's limp, comatose body.

"The human did not kill me because I am no longer a demon. When did that become the case? When did I leave behind my brothers and sisters to embark on a new path?"

With a shake of his head, Smealum plops down beside Shepherd and massages his brother's shoulders.

"More importantly... what do I do now?"

Uzziel's eyes grow wide as saucers. She stares at the Devil and the two Emperors flanking him with a mixture of fascination and abject horror. The dark mana flowing from just those three demons dwarfs all of the others swarming into Heaven combined.

Satan motions with his hands. A moment later, water erupts from the grass around him, ripping the life out of the greenery and turning it brown. The grass shrivels and cracks as its life-giving liquid rips out and splashes against Satan's back, then transforms into eight fluid limbs.

The Devil pounces toward Belial, Bael, and Michael. By the time Lucifer and Diablo manage to follow his movements, he's already cleared the gap.

"Nobody hurts my girl!" Satan roars. "You fuckers are dead!"

Michael immediately pivots from Bael to the incoming threat. He jumps back a split-second before Satan's vectors manage to grab him. An instant later, Michael's sword begins dancing from left to right. He summons holy energy to envelop his weapon, allowing him to parry Satan's invisible limbs.

Thousand of sparks erupt around the two combatants as Satan and Michael's limbs and weapons collide with blinding speed. Gabriel motions with his hands and fires a massive blast of holy energy at Satan, but the Devil darts to the left and dodges it, never once stopping his sixteen-limb attack against Michael.

The other Emperors arrive. Diablo charges in after compressing kinetic energy around his fist. He attempts to blindside Michael, only for Camael to get in his way and use her shield to block his fist.

_KABOOM!_

Diablo sends Camael flying. Before she skids across the ground, she uses Hermes' Boots to kick off an invisible platform, then leaps into the sky, dispersing the power of his punch.

"Uzziel!" Raphael says to his sister. "Try to slow the movements of the Emperors! I'll distract them so that our brothers and sisters might land a few decisive strikes!"

"Right!" Uzziel replies. She conjures the elemental power of nature to summon vines from the ground. Unlike when she uses her powers on Earth, the incredible amount of Holy Energy pervading Heaven transforms her ability into a weapon of mass destruction.

Tree roots and vines burst out from underneath Satan and the other Emperors, entangling their legs and slowing their movements while allowing Michael, Gabriel, and Camael to launch a frontal attack.

Satan ignores the vines coiling around his limbs since his water-tentacles and vectors are more than capable of taking out an army by themselves. When Gabriel swings his greatsword at Satan, the Devil merely catches it with three vectors and yanks it out of the Archangel's hands. He tosses the weapon aside, causing it to disperse into particles of light.

Gabriel aims both of his palms at Satan.

_Da-da-da-da-da-da!_

Rapid-fire bolts of holy energy emerge from his palms and fly at Satan. The Devil summons a pillar of stone to block them and focuses on Camael instead. The Archangel of Divination lunges toward him and raises her hammer to smash his skull.

"A frontal attack? Suicide!" Satan laughs. Three water-tentacles spring at her and punch the Archangel with all their strength.

However, the instant Satan's octopus limbs connect, Camael vanishes.

"What? An illusion?!"

Satan whips his head around to see Camael spring out of three different places at once. Each of her copies raises their hammers high and swing down at his flank, but the Devil quickly deflects them with his Vectors and octopus limbs.

However, one of the illusions proves to be the real one. Camael summons her tower shield and bats Satan's Vectors away thanks to its holy-energy infused front-side. He tries to retaliate, but she activates Hermes Boots and charges him three times faster than before.

_WHAM!_

The Archangel of Divination bullrushes Satan's body with the flat of her shield and plows him into the ground. Immediately, Uzziel's vines jump out and wrap around him like a spider binding a fly.

Michael leaps at Satan. "Thy death is nigh, little bloodskin!"

He switches his sword stance and aims to plunge it into Satan's chest, but an energy blast suddenly knocks him out of the air. Lucifer fires from her third eye three times, each shot battering Michael with concentrated mana. Michael flaps his wings, rights himself, and lands on his feet a few paces away.

Satan tears through Uzziel's vines with his Vectors. The Devil quickly jumps and hovers over the terrain, using Samael's ultimate weapon to rip apart anything else the Archangel of Creation throws at him.

"You guys are really pissing me off!" Satan yells at the Archangels. "Let's split these angels up! It's time for phase two, boys!"

Bael, Diablo, Lucifer, and Berith all nod simultaneously. "Right!"

A sudden sense of foreboding bubbles up in the back of Uzziel's mind. At the last second, she dives to the right and shoves Camael aside. One of Berith's shadow-fists jumps out of the ground and smashes Uzziel in the face, striking her instead of her elder sister. Uzziel gasps and tumbles aside, but quickly motions with her hands to suck into the ground.

Unlike the slow movement of Burrowers, Uzziel travels even faster underground than when she flies. Her powers make her one with the soil, and she jumps out from behind Gabriel, using his body as a shield. "Brother! Watch out! The demons are going to-"

She doesn't get to finish her sentence. Michael suddenly yells in alarm. A nearly translucent figure, one crouched low to the ground, pounces at the Archangel of Courage from behind. Agares, now using his Camouflage ability to blend in with his surroundings, catches Michael unawares as he delivers a reptilian punch to the Archangels' spine. Michael gasps and tries to swing his sword around, but a cloud of poison gas descends upon him, blinding and choking him as Vepar attacks him in her gas-body form.

"Michael!" Uzziel shrieks her brother's name. She starts moving toward him, but her eyes lock onto Bael as the big guy charges Camael from the side. 

"Just **try** and hit me with that hammer again, grandma! Bahaha!"

Bael plows into Camael and throws her to the ground. At the same time, the Emperors split apart, each of them targeting different Archangels.

Lucifer and Diablo charge at Gabriel. Satan grabs Belial and splashes healing water on his mate, then both of them move to attack Raphael.

_They're splitting us up!_ Uzziel screams internally. She starts to say something, only to flap her wings and dodge backward as Berith appears in front of her.

_Whiff!_

Berith's claws swing at the spot where Uzziel stood only a moment before. "Bah! Come back here, little tree-bender! You can't run forever!"

Uzziel's heart trembles as her eyes flick to each of the other Archangels. Bael and Camael trade blows. Her hammer crashes against his armored body, while his fists batter her tower-shield and threaten to throw her off her feet.

Raphael summons several ghostly clones of himself to confuse Satan and Belial, but the two mightiest Emperors dispatch a dozen of them in the blink of an eye. 

Diablo rears back for a massive kinetic punch and batters the force-field enveloping the Archangel of Power with all his strength. Gabriel staggers backward a few inches, but otherwise shrugs off the atomic attack. A moment later, Lucifer fires her mental barrage at him, bypassing Gabriel's barriers and hitting him in the brain where his defenses are lowest.

"Gaaugh!"

Gabriel lurches back and grabs his head. Waves of pain bring him to his knees, but the force field surrounding him protects him from another of Diablo's kinetic punches.

Uzziel perceives all of their battles in a single second, then returns her attention to Berith. The Emperor of Backstabbing summons two swords of shadow and charges at Uzziel. She cackles and swings both weapons toward Uzziel's neck like a pair of scissors.

Uzziel summons a magically-imbued walnut to surround herself. 

_Thunk!_

Berith's swords slice into Uzziel's shield, but they halt halfway and jam inside the thick wood.

Uzziel summons thorny brambles from beneath Berith's feet. The holy-energy-infused weeds tear into Berith's legs and quickly climb her body, making the Emperor howl in pain.

"Kyaarr! Get off me, you damned-!!"

Berith releases her swords and melts into her shadow, giving Uzziel a moment of respite. She uses the precious few seconds to spare a glance at the other Archangels. In addition to Bael, two other high-level demons enter the battle: Shax and Murmur. The tall, lanky, wild-haired Shax ducks and dodges as the four Valkyries join alongside Camael to help their sister fight off the Duke of Pain and his cohorts. Shax dances around with style, each movement flowing around like a mixture between a practiced martial artist and a disco dancer.

However, what catches Uzziel's attention most is that while Shax evades Catherine's sword slashes and Hyacinth's wind-arrows, Murmur rides his shoulders, piggyback-style, without falling off. Her child-sized legs wrap around his front, and whenever any attack comes close to his chest or head, she gingerly pokes the air and deflects it harmlessly.

"L-look out. Here it comes. From your left. Now, right..."

Murmur whispers in Shax's ear as she delicately protects him from anything he doesn't dodge. Shax, on the other hand, grins like he's having the time of his life. He drops to his legs and does the splits. "Oh, yeah! Whoo! What a rush! I wanna bust out in song, haha!"

Indeed, he rolls backward, and Murmur pushes her hands against the ground to backflip Shax into the air. The two of them never separate, despite the intensity of his acrobatic movements. Shax lands on his feet and begins strumming as if playing an air guitar.

"♪Four girls, but ain't none of 'em my type! They come a-swingin' but there ain't no time ta' fight!♪"

Hyacinth fires three arrows rapid-fire at Shax, but he moonwalks from side to side, dodging two of them effortlessly while leaving the third to Murmur. She pokes the air and sends it tumbling into the ground.

"Damn this demon! Catherine, help me shut him up! I can't stand that awful singing!"

Sophia spins her bo staff and does a backflip toward Hyacinth. "Aww, I think it's pretty catchy!"

Hyacinth rolls her eyes. "As always, thy taste in music churns my stomach!"

Catherine and Penelope jump toward Shax from his left and right. Catherine slashes at his legs, while Penelope swings her mace down, intent on splattering not only him, but Murmur as well.

Murmur pokes the air to deflect Catherine's blade, but she's too slow to stop Penelope's mace. "Sh-Shax! Look out!"

Shax doesn't hesitate. The instant before Penelope's attack connects, he whips his head toward her and screams at the top of his lungs, "STEP OFF, HUSSIE!"

A powerful soundwave fires from Shax's mouth. Penelope yelps in surprise and goes flying, unable to defend herself from his supersonic scream.

Without missing a beat, Shax pivots to face Catherine and strums his invisible guitar even harder. "♪Ten million ta' one, and we almost fuckin' done! Step off, sista, cuz we've only just begun!♪"

The harder he strums, the more powerful the soundwaves unleashed from his invisible guitar become. Catherine staggers, as an unrelenting force begins pushing her backward with all its strength. Blood trickles from her ears, and her head starts to spin. Shax's words blur together, transforming from English to pure babble as his oppressive soundwaves threaten to shatter not only Catherine's eardrums, but her mind as well.

Suddenly, Murmur yelps. "Look out!"

Diablo comes flying through the air, having been flung aside by Gabriel's greatsword. He hurtles toward Shax and Murmur, the latter of whom can't decide whether or not to deflect him, given he's an ally.

"Uwah!"

Murmur gasps as Diablo's sizable body plows into her and Shax, sending all three of them sprawling across the grass.

"Owie..."

Murmur whimpers and curls into a ball. She shivers for a moment, then raises her eyes to stare as Hyacinth aims her bow at Shax.

"Those two are separated! Take out the male, then the female!"

Tears bubble in Murmur's eyes as Hyacinth starts to release a wind-arrow toward her brother, lying on his back in the grass.

"Nooo! Don't hurt big bro!"

Murmur tries to run to her brother's side, but her legs are too tiny. She watches in disbelief as Hyacinth's arrow travels at the speed of sound. It smashes into Shax, sending a massive explosion rippling through Heaven. The arrow strikes Shax's body, driving him underground. He ends up buried eight-feet deep, hidden from the surface world.

"Eeeyaa!!"

Murmur screams as she runs over to the crater. Her brother lays motionless, but the Valkyries give him no respite. Immediately, all four of them close in on Murmur and Diablo. Diablo groans and staggers to his feet, but when he sees Shax's crater a few paces to his left, his eyes widen.

"Uh, oh."

"One down!" Penelope yells, triumphantly. "Woo-hoo! Now, let's get that little one, and we can all focus on Diab-"

"BIG BRO!! NOOOO!"

Murmur screams bloody murder. Tears well up in her eyes, and she whirls to face Hyacinth. "You fat, ugly, nasty cow! Take THIS!!"

She aims her finger at Hyacinth, then raises it up and down. Hyacinth gasps as telekinetic force grabs her body, lifts her into the sky, and flings her into a pile of rubble below. "Ahh! I can't control my-!"

_WHAM! WHAM! WHAM!_

Over and over, Murmur raises and smashes Hyacinth into the ground. 

"This is for Shax, you nasty chicken!! I'll turn you into a puddle of blood!"

"Hyacinth!" Sophia cries out. She quickly flaps her wings and flies toward Murmur. "Little turd! Let go of my sister- gack!"

Before Sophia can land a decisive blow, Murmur releases Hyacinth, whirls to face the incoming Valkyrie, and flicks the air. A blast of telekinetic force smashes into Sophia and flings her backward, sending the Valkyrie tumbling end over end. She lands face-down, ass-up, her head spinning from the impact.

Suddenly, Catherine and Penelope arrive on the scene. Both of them rush the hooded little demon girl and raise their weapons high. Before they can end her life, Diablo jumps in front of them and kicks his foot into the ground. A ten-times-enhanced shockwave summons a minor earthquake, knocking both Valkyries off their feet. They trip and stumble, then fall to their knees. Catherine flaps her wings and yanks herself to her feet, but Murmur turns around and flicks the air, sending the Valkyrie of Fire tumbling backward, just like Sophia. Before Penelope can raise her mace again, Diablo rears his fist back and smashes it against her golden armor, punting her in the opposite direction.

_WHUMP!_

All four Valkyries grind into the dirt and gasp for breath as they try to recover from the power of the Emperor and his tiny little comrade. Hyacinth moans and tries to shift around, but her strength gives out, and she flops into the dirt lifelessly. Sophia clambers to her feet, but her footing proves shaky. She stumbles back and forth, using her bo staff to hold herself upright. Catherine, the toughest of the Valkyries, manages to regain her footing but thinks better of charging the Emperor of Annihilation alone.

From the crater to her right, Murmur's brother calls out to her. "Ohh... I'm alive, sis. Barely."

"Shax? You're okay?!" Murmur dives to the side of the crater and peers inside. Shax lays on his back, a massive gash torn across the right side of his chest, but otherwise in good condition.

"M-managed to deflect that arrow with a soundwave. Hurts like hell, though."

"You're bleeding everywhere! I'll get Belial and-"

Shax shakes his head. "Nah, nah, it's fine, sis. No biggie."

With a grunt, Shax kicks his feet up and grabs the sheer walls of the coffin-shaped depression. He yanks himself upward and jumps out of the pit, then lands on his knees.

"Get on," Shax says to Murmur, as he aims his thumb toward his back. "We're leaving this shithole. It's too bright here, anyway. Ruining my mojo."

Murmur pulls her hood down to hide her face. "Oh... um... alright, but are you okay, big brother?"

Shax glances down at the gash going across his chest, then presses his arm against it. "Yeah. I'll survive, but only if I stop the bleeding. Come on."

"O-okay."

Murmur climbs onto his shoulders, and Shax darts away, leaving the Valkyries in his dust.

Diablo watches them go. "Sheesh. Bye, then. Don't mind me."

With a start, Diablo turns around to look for Gabriel. He ignores the Valkyries to focus on the figure of the fifteen-foot-tall Archangel attacking his mate. Gabriel slashes at Lucifer with his greatsword, aiming to lop her head off.

"Stand still, demon. I do not wish to cause thee any unnecessary pain," Gabriel says, his voice as calm as a lake during summer. His weapon misses Lucifer by inches as she dives to the ground and lets it swing over her back.

"Not a chance, Archangel! Eat shit and die!"

Lucifer fires a kinetic blast at Gabriel, but it splatters against the seemingly impenetrable force field surrounding his body without causing so much as a ripple. Gabriel flicks his eyes toward Raphael, as the old man battles Satan and Belial. However, no longer is he alone, as several Titans, including Zeus, have joined the battle.

"How many times must we fight, demon dog?!" Zeus bellows. He aims his palm at Belial, and a bolt of lightning slams into her, sending a thunderous shockwave in every direction. 

Dozens of demons nearby cover their ears and howl in pain, but their angelic adversaries prove unaffected. Their helmets, forged by Camael, offer magical protection from sounds above a specific volume. With their demon adversaries stunned, the cherubs and seraphs use weapons and magic to cut through the horde with ruthless efficiency.

Belial lands a few dozen feet away and rolls to a stop. Her limbs spasm for a moment, thanks to Zeus's lightning seizing up her body. She heals herself and jumps to her feet. "That damned Titan. His lightning is so fast that I can't avoid it in time!"

Belial's heart skips a beat as something invisible punches her in the back and sends her tumbling forward. Raphael's illusion magic breaks for a split-second, but he quickly activates it once more and flaps his wings to leap into the sky.

The old man glares at Belial. "That wench's healing magic appears to function identically to Uzziel's. She's a troublesome opponent, especially when fighting alongside Satan. Damn. Even if only the two of them were here, without any other demons, it might take the full power of Heaven's Finest to bury them. But with all these noteworthy upper-ranked demons, as well..."

Raphael glances around. His eyes flick to Michael, flanked by a camouflaged Agares, and the troublesome Vepar. Michael tries to attack Vepar, but she disperses into a cloud of poison. A blur of motion from behind is Michael's only warning of Agares' incoming attack, but the Archangel of Courage easily spins around and knocks his reptilian adversary aside. 

"Heh," Michael chuckles. "Not so strong without the element of surprise, art thou, filthy bloodskin?"

Then, Raphael turns to search for the four Valkyries. Far in the distance, Diablo faces off against the four of them. Uzziel finishes healing her sister Hyacinth and helps her to her feet.

The Archangel of Creation glances around, her nerves on edge. The shadow-walker, Berith, isn't anywhere in sight, but that fact isn't enough to steady her pounding heart.

"I need to leave," Uzziel says, her voice tight. "Even with the assistance of the Titans, Heaven may fall. I will bring reinforcements."

Hyacinth's wings twitch. She clutches Uzziel's shoulder and spares a wary glance toward Diablo, as several Lord-ranked demons saddle up beside him. "Reinforcements? Are not all of our family members here? We haven't time for games, sister."

"We'll be fine," Penelope replies, answering in Uzziel's stead. She casually slings her mace over her shoulder and smirks at the demons lining up. "At least for a little while. Don't take too long, sister."

Uzziel looks at Penelope, then sweeps her gaze across Catherine and Sophia's faces. "Stay safe. The demons are nothing if not crafty. They will deceive thee at every turn."

"Aye," All four Valkyries reply in unison. They turn their backs to Uzziel to face down the demons standing across from them.

"Well? What art thou waiting for, little rats?" Penelope yells, taunting her opponents. "Don't tell me thou art afraid of a few women?!"

The lords beside Diablo howl and shriek, then charge toward the Valkyries.

"No, you idiots!" Diablo roars. "Don't let them goad you- ahh, dammit to Hell!"

Diablo follows from the rear, an annoyed scowl plastering his face. He tails his comrades, and the Valkyries return the gesture.

Uzziel turns away, and her heart pounds in her throat like the beating of a drum.

With a shudder, the Archangel of Creation dives underground. She begins speeding away from the battlefield, leaving her family behind to face the demons alone.

_There is one who has yet to join the fight! I must find him!_
