His current weapon, Dellfingler, dizzily coughed. "W-whoooaaa partner! You okay? You took that hit like a champ!"

Arthur didn't respond, instead opting to stare into the distance.

_The Demon Emperor's mana is depleted. He will not be able to retaliate._ Arthur thought.

His eyes glowed as he transformed Dellfingler into a warhammer of light once more. Bael stirred and shook his head, wincing as he struggled to orient himself. He spotted Arthur standing and gaped. "Whu— how? You... you're alive? But... that punch..."

Arthur didn't respond. Instead, he raised two fingers to his temple.

A sudden realization hit Bael. _The boss! No way he's still standing after using that much power! If Arthur teleports over there, Deebs is a goner! I've gotta grab this goon..._ Bael thought.

He leaped from the pit, every bone in his body screaming and cracking in the process, and grabbed the Crusader. "Oh no, you don't! Worthless fleshb—"

Arthur transmitted to Diablo and flicked Bael to the side effortlessly. The Demon Emperor, flopped on his back, stared emptily at the Hero-King as the human raised his massive warhammer.

_No...!_ Diablo thought.

_BOOM BOOM BOOM BOOM!_

Over and over, Arthur smashed Diablo with his holy warhammer. The Emperor's bones turned to pulp. Diablo barely raised a hand in protest for the first strike, let alone the rest.

"Boss!!!" Bael leaped to his feet and lunged at the Hero, but Arthur pivoted to face the incoming threat. He fired a wave of Holy Magic from his free palm, which didn't hurt Bael, but did violently fling him backward. Arthur then morphed Dellfingler back to his sword form.

"Oh, jeez! Being a different weapon is disorienting! It's like when you forget your car keys, but you have to climb inside a washing machine to get 'em! Great work, though, Arthur! You really showed that bloodskin who's the boss!"

Mephisto sent more undead minions leaping from the ground at Arthur. The Hero-King ignored them and transmitted over to Diablo's other side, raising Dellfingler high.

_Swish, slash, chop!_

Arthur sliced and diced skin and muscle, carving up the Emperor's limp corpse to ensure his death.

Bael staggered to his feet and watched in horror as the Hero turned Diablo into mincemeat.

Tears swelled in his eyes. _Boss. Diablo... no! You were... always there for me. I can't... I can't let this go! I gotta avenge him! I gotta tear that human to pieces!_ Bael thought.

Anger filled the Duke's mind as he formed hand signs into the only magical seal he could use.

His teeth ground together with enough force to chew steel. _I shouldn'a got in the way, Boss. But... I won't let Arthur win. I'll beat this bastard! For you!_ Bael thought.

Bael's flesh and bones rapidly regenerated as a new, primeval power boiled in his body. The power of a Duke. The power of the Ancient Demons. Armor began to form over and around all the skin on his body. His cherry-red body rapidly faded, and charcoal black replaced it.

Cracks appeared in the blackness, and sparks of fire emerged from those cracks. Bael rapidly expanded in size, from almost eight feet tall to well over fifteen. His body doubled in size. His horns morphed and deformed, transforming from upward spikes into forward-facing spears.

Fire exploded in Bael's belly as Arthur turned to face the demon.

"**GRAAAAAAAAUUUURRGGHHH!!!**"

Bael unleashed a mighty roar, releasing the pent-up power of the hottest Hellfire from his stomach. His mouth erupted with the energy of a volcano, creating a bright enough light that he illuminated the land for miles around.

Mephisto peeked from the ground and shuddered.

_The Balrog._

_Legendsss ssspeak of Bael'sss fearsssome power... his featsss during the Great War in Heaven, where he cemented himself as the mossst powerful Duke without quessstion. They sssay only Diablo hasss ever held back Bael'sss unssstoppable battle power._ Mephisto thought.

Mephisto slowly lowered again, transferring under the surface.

_But the Hero-King took Diablo'sss mightiessst attack. Can he be ssso easssily ssstopped, even by Bael'sss ultimate transssformation?_ Mephisto thought.

Across the plains, Beelzebub gazed in horror at the monstrosity bellowing on the battlefield. _Seven Devils. Whatever that thing is... I'm not willing to fight it. I'll keep my distance, for now._ Beelzebub thought.

Bael grabbed his massive flail, now an average-sized weapon for a monster of his size, and screamed furiously at the human below him.

"**PUNY! SMALL! ME CRUSH! ME SMASH!**"
