Part 256: Belial's Demise

With two Shades at the far corner of Belial's makeshift operation room, and one in between them, the Emperor of Passion can only suck in a breath and grit her teeth as her shadowy enemies begin conjuring mystical spell patterns in the darkness. Dreadful killing intent falls upon Belial's figure as the three female Shades square off against their three female opponents.

"Get behind me!" Belial yells, speaking not only to Daisy, but Rosalia too. "Physical attacks won't hurt a Shade!"

"That's right," Greed says, her smile turning sinister. "They won't. You'll have to use your raw mana against us! What a pity. You've been wasting it on useless humans!"

A barrage of shadow-lances fires from both corners of the room. Greed and Malice work together to attack Belial from her left and right, forcing the Second Emperor to summon makeshift mana shields around Rosalia and Daisy. She uses her left hand to form the hand signs necessary for protecting her friends, while her right hand and arm turn into razor-sharp blades capable of batting aside the shadows aimed at herself.

_Ting-ting-ting!_

Belial's bladed arm becomes a whirl of movement. She bats aside the hardened shadows aimed at her body, then moves to attack the lances stabbing into her clumsily thrown-together mana barriers. She slices through a half-dozen aimed at Daisy, then chops apart another half-dozen targeting Rosalia.

Belial's eyes narrow. _The physical body of a Shade is immaterial and ghostly, much like Mephisto. However, when they manifest their powers into reality, I have a small window where I can physically deflect them! Even so... I still can't hurt any of these Shades' physical bodies, only the parts they manifest!_

Belial continues battling with the two Shades, their rapid-fire back-and-forth happening in the span of mere seconds. While Belial has her mind focused on one of Greed's probing attacks, Demise roars violently, transforming her Orc-shaped figure into an eight-foot-tall behemoth! Her body absorbs the darkness around her, and her strength erupts with explosive might!

"**Belial! I am the mightiest Shade! Now, I shall fulfill my Master's will!**"

Belial's heart leaps into her throat. Her eyes become pinpricks as the gigantic shadow-creature charges at her, clearing the ten-foot gap instantly. The Shade smashes aside the hospital bed in the center of the room, blasting it through the wall to the side and clearing a path. Demise summons a tremendous great-axe, one as tall as her entire body, and swings it at Belial's head.

"Shit!"

Belial curses and raises her right blade-arm, stopping Demise's shadow-axe mere inches from her head. The _clang_ of Demise's mighty attack versus Belial's stalwart defense causes a shockwave to erupt, blasting apart the window behind where Demise started. Over and over, Demise raises her weapon and begins hammering on Belial, forcing Belial back against the wall. Her concentration falters, and she howls in pain as Yama's strongest Shade strikes her over and over. All Belial can do is block Demise's attacks to the best of her ability while maintaining her dual mana-barriers to protect Rosalia and Daisy.

However, with both arms occupied, Belial reveals an opening, allowing Greed and Malice to target her weak points. Unable to protect herself, Belial screams in pain as a dozen shadow lances impale her from the right and left, weaving around Demise's giant body like vipers to bite into the Second Emperor's flesh and bone.

"Kaahk!"

Belial nearly bites off her tongue as she desperately holds back the Orc-Shade, while the twelve shadow-lances begin rapidly absorbing her strength, sucking her mana away to empower Greed and Malice's bodies instead.

"Haha! How juicy!" Greed yells, taunting the succubus. "An Emperor's mana tastes incredible! I could suck you dry and I still wouldn't feel full!"

"Aunt S'matha!" Daisy cries in alarm. The child stares at her aunt in horror as she witnesses the awful barbs biting into Belial's right and left sides. "Nooo!!"

Belial's complexion loses some of its color. However, at the same time, a deep rage boils in her eyes, and her fighting spirit soars to the heavens.

"Tch. You want to play dirty?! So be it!"

Suddenly, from Belial's sides, underneath both of her existing arms, two additional arms burst out! Thanks to her body-transformation powers, the Second Emperor sprouts a third and fourth arm, then transforms them into blades and hacks apart the four-foot-long javelins impaling her ribs. Before the Shades can react, she double-palm-strikes Demise and blasts the strongest Shade backward, sending Demise flying out the broken window!

"**Aaargh!**"

Demise howls in surprise, but not pain. Her sturdy body barely takes any damage from Belial's hasty counter-attack. The moment she flies out the window, she explodes into smoke and reforms her body, then charges back inside.

A vicious melee ensues. With one arm conjuring mana to protect Daisy and Rosalia, Belial uses her other three arms to fend off the Shades. However, she quickly realizes she can't use her superior agility in tandem with her strength, causing her actual fighting power to fall!

_If Daisy weren't here, these three Shades would be nothing to me!_ Belial thinks. _But the cowardly bastards know I can't let Phoebe's daughter get hurt, so they're turning Daisy into my weakness! What's my next move?_

Five minutes pass. The furious battle intensifies further as Demise switches between her greataxe, a greatsword, and even a deadly shadow-halberd. Not only do Greed and Malice summon shadow-lances to attack Belial from afar, but they continuously hammer the force-fields around Daisy and Rosalia, weakening Belial's mana reserves.

Belial's forehead runs slick with sweat. The sounds of her battle echoes throughout the darkened halls of the Hospital, yet not once do the lights turn on, nor do any soldiers show up for backup.

_Where the hell is everyone?!_ Belial screams internally. _I thought if I held out, at least a few soldiers would arrive to back me up! Don't tell me these shadow-bastards have already killed everyone else in the hospital?!_

The succubus's thoughts churn rapidly. She grabs Demise by the neck and tries to smash the Shade against a wall, but Demise turns immaterial in her grasp and flits to the side, frustrating Belial further.

_No! It's not likely the Shades killed everyone else in the hospital,_ Belial thinks. _They can't move freely in the light! The lights barely went out for ten seconds before they showed up here! More likely, they placed some enchantment around this room so nobody can hear our battle! Yama has likely prepared for this moment for a while now, so he'd be sure to grasp onto any advantages he could!_

While Belial's mind works rapid-fire, Daisy shrinks down, frightened of the scary shadow-monsters attacking her aunt. Never in her life has the child seen such horrible, vicious violence.

Not in reality, at least.

However, these awful creatures and their bloodthirsty auras do cause Daisy's complexion to turn white as she remembers something similar from only a month before. Back when she flew with her father to Tarus II's moon, on the trip back, she suffered horrible nightmares, terrible visions of demons and angels fighting one another, ripping themselves to pieces.

Those horrible memories come rushing back, making her shiver in fright.

"Daddy... daddy... I'm scared..."

The little girl shivers and covers her eyes, unable to watch. Even so, the sounds of the Shades taunting her aunt, as well as their weapons clashing, reaches her ears.

The longer the battle rages, the more disheartened Belial becomes.

_Damn. Damn! I'm running low on mana! Shades can maintain their energy by swallowing the darkness around them, but I can't! I'm wiped out from days of hospital work! Even with Daisy helping me, I still didn't have much mana to start!_

_What should I do?!_

The blood continues to drain from Belial's face, but she stubbornly holds on, fighting the Shades with all she's got.

Suddenly, after once again grabbing and hurling Demise aside, Belial's eyes flash with determination! She drops the force-fields around Daisy and Rosalia, grabs the little girl, and thrusts her into Rosalia's arms.

"Take the girl! Go to Kar's room! Find any soldiers you can and tell them the Shades are attacking! GO!"

Belial leaves no room for debate. In the split-second she lowers her defenses, she switches from defense to offense. Before Rosalia can react, Belial explodes with rage and releases a massive force-field around the room, caging herself in, along with the Shades. She stops the force-field mere inches from Rosalia's face, causing the tubby demoness to jump back in alarm.

"What? Kar? But-"

"I told you to LEAVE!"

Belial shouts back her order one more time before launching herself at Demise.

_BOOM!_

The succubus no longer holds anything back. She smashes her fist into Demise's body, startling the newly-reformed Shade, and smashing her backwards, against the force-field. This time, Demise doesn't turn into smoke, but instead falls to the ground heavily. She tries to pick herself back up, but Belial jumps on her before she can react!

_Boom, boom, boom!_

Belial punches Demise with all her strength, battering her against the floor over and over. Not once does the strongest Shade turn into smoke to evade her, because as she quickly finds out... she can't!

Greed's sinister smile vanishes. She quickly realizes something has changed, and it likely involves Belial's mana force-field. "Dammit! The succubus trapped us in here!"

"Fight with all you've got!" Malice orders.

As both Shades descend on Belial to try and pull her away from Demise, Rosalia's widened eyes quickly become focused. She clutches Daisy to her chest and runs out of the room.

Finally sensing Rosalia leaving, Belial roars out one last order. "Don't look back! Just run!"

...

Rosalia dashes away from the operating room. Her heart tumbles in her chest as she makes a mad dash down the corridor. However, hardly has she made it twenty feet before her eyes turn as wide as saucers. All around her, countless human bodies lie around the corridors, their insides ripped to pieces, as if by a wild, mad dog.

"Oh devils, oh devils..."

Without hesitation, Rosalia keeps running. She moves from the west wing of the hospital, the one closest to the battlefield and Hero City's limits, toward the eastern wing, where Kar lays.

"Gotta move, gotta move!"

As Rosalia runs, she continues holding Daisy in her arms. The little girl opens her eyes, looks around, and sees all the dead bodies.

"Ahh! They're hurt! Fat lady, stop! We have to help them!"

Rosalia's eyes erupt with rage. Even as she continues running, she gnashes her teeth. "Shut up, brat! This is all your fault! If it weren't for you, the Second Emperor wouldn't be in this state!"

Daisy pales. "M-my fault?"

"That's right!" Rosalia screeches, as she jumps over a headless corpse laying in the hall. "Those shadow-walkers are here for you! Just great!"

The pudgy demoness grinds her molars together, as ugly thoughts swirl in her head.

_Tch! Why am I protecting this stupid little girl anyway?! The only person I should protect is Mister Beelzebub! Not the Hero's spawn! I should throw her to the ground and leave her to die! Yeah!_

In the midst of her anger, Rosalia momentarily forgets Daisy's uncanny ability to read minds. Unseen by Rosalia, who has her eyes set on the darkened hallway ahead, the little girl squints at her face and frowns.

Rosalia's thoughts continue to race. _I'd be doing all the demons a favor. Mister Beelzebub would probably love me again if he knew I did something as awesome as killing the Hero's brat! Yeah! Mister Beelzebub has only forgotten how much he loves me! If I just stop for a moment and wrap my hands around her little throat..._

Before Rosalia's thoughts can go any further, a female voice speaks in her mind, one she's heard before.

**Unacceptable. Murdering the child won't bring you any closer to your goals. More importantly... Master Gressil wouldn't like it if you killed this girl.**

Rosalia's face turns ashen. _Desire? Why are you popping up at a time like this?! I'm busy!_

**Why have I appeared? Is that not obvious?** Desire asks, a note of taunting in her voice. **I'm here to stop you from unleashing a moment of stupidity. Master Gressil doesn't want this girl dead. She's... special. To him, you might even say she's irreplaceable.**

Rosalia scowls. She leaves the west wing and arrives at the central hub, only to find even more dead bodies lying in the corridor. A shiver goes down the plump demoness's back as she continues running toward the eastern wing.

_Why should I care?! My only goal is to return to Mister Beelzebub! I'm here spying for Master Gressil. That's as far as I'll go for the likes of him!_

**Haha. How shallow-minded,** Desire says, her voice seductive. **Master Gressil is not like other demons. He sees things their eyes cannot comprehend. He observes from a distance, watching, waiting. He knows how the lines of fate intersect. If he tells you to keep the child alive, then that is what you must do. Otherwise... you will never see your precious Beelzebub again.**

Hearing Desire's firm, unyielding words, Rosalia shoots a disgusted glance at Daisy, only to notice Daisy silently staring back at her.

"What?" Rosalia asks, annoyed. "What are you looking at, brat?!"

Daisy turns away. Her eyes fall into the darkness behind Rosalia, at the corpses left in her wake. "...Nothing."

Rosalia doesn't notice the odd glint in Daisy's eyes, nor does she realize that the child may have seen through her thoughts. Instead, she replies to Desire inside her head. _Fine! I won't kill her! Are you happy? Sheesh! What use does this brat have to Mister Gressil, anyway? Does he have a boo-boo he needs healed?_

**Hahaha... not quite. The girl is special... and that is all you need to know. Mmm. I should warn you... her eyes can observe phenomena yours never will. For example, the shadow following in your wake.**

Rosalia's heart skips a beat. _A shadow?!_

She continues running, but pauses half a step to glance behind her. All she sees in the darkened hallway behind her is the same pitch-blackness as always, with countless bodies fallen throughout the wards.

Before Rosalia can give voice to her incredulity, Daisy speaks.

"Bad person. There's a bad person following us!"

Rosalia's blood turns ice-cold. She runs even faster, huffing and puffing as her lack of exercise starts to catch up to her. "I don't like this, not one bit! Brat, you'd better not be lying!!"

The end of the eastern wing comes into view. At the end of the hallway, Kar's room shows up, countless dead bodies lined up outside of it.

_Tch! Don't tell me the croc is dead too! If I ran here just to come to a dead end, I'm going to be so angryyy!!_

Rosalia's thoughts flick not only to all the dead bodies she's encountered, but Daisy's words.

_The lights only went out for a few seconds before those shadow-walkers showed up. How could they have killed all these soldiers in such a short time? Wouldn't someone have raised the alarm? Everyone has those armor-suit chest-gadgets, yet they weren't able to protect themselves! How could three shadow-walkers have possibly...?_

The moment Rosalia follows that train of thought, a terrible realization befalls her.

_No! Oh, devils! What if there weren't only three shadow-walkers?! What if there were more?!_

Remembering Daisy's words from mere moments before, Rosalia quickly glances backward. Still, she doesn't see anyone following them.

_I have a bad feeling about this!_

Just before arriving at Kar's room, Daisy jerks in fright. She suddenly points at the crocodile's ward.

"Bad person! Very bad person!"

"What?!"

Rosalia screeches to a halt. She nearly slips on a puddle of blood left by a decapitated Kobold commando, but manages to catch herself from falling.

Her eyes sweep into Kar's room.

There, she sees something utterly horrifying.

A demonic hound, blood dripping from its jowls, hovers over Kar's bed. The crocodile sleeps peacefully, while a shroud of darkness covers his body. 

The Shade, Vicious, stares at the intruders meaningfully. Her mouth curls up into a beastly grin. She chuckles quietly.

"Ehehe... shh! Don't wake the baby...!"

Rosalia shivers in terror. She locks eyes with the horrifying demonic fiend, while it releases an evil aura that nearly makes her legs give out. It's only due to spending a few months with the Second Emperor's even stronger demonic aura that she manages to stay standing.

Daisy squeezes Rosalia's shirt.

"Bad person... bad person..."
