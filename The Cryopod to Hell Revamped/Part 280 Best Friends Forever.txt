Part 280: Best Friends Forever?

_Western Front, before the Hydra's arrival._

Emperor Ose and Archangel Uriel furiously battle one another. Their fists and feet collide with thunderous shockwaves, forcing a wide berth of empty space around themselves. Still morphed into her electrical Ultraform, Ose's attacks rain down relentlessly upon Uriel. The Archangel protects herself with earthen armor while summoning Holy Energy to attack the Emperor of Infiltration.

_This white-haired Emperor has grown more domineering in the last six years,_ Uriel thinks as she swings a holy-energy-spear like a hammer toward Ose's chest. Ose flickers backward into the sky, causing Uriel's attack to miss. _Last time we fought, I did not come up nearly as short as I have today._

Uriel motions with her hand. She tears off dozens of armored plates from a nearby Planet-walker via Barbatos's magnetism, stripping one of its humongous legs bare. Then, she compresses those shards into thin but dense metal needles and sends them to chase after Ose, aiming to skewer the Emperor to death.

Just as the needles rush toward Ose's back, her eyes flash with electrical energy. She swings her palm backward and fires a bolt of lightning into the center of the needle cluster, which explodes, causing the needles to lose their momentum and plummet to the ground.

_Mmm?_ Uriel grunts, startled. _Dost mine eyes deceive me? Ose broke Barbatos's magnetism with that attack. T'was no casual move!_

A moment later, Ose cackles. She continues circling Uriel like a bird of prey, looking for an opening. "Foolish Archangel! Do you think I'll lie down and accept whatever punishment you want to vent upon me?! I've planned well for your reappearance! Artorias himself worked alongside me to plan contingencies. Your magic will accomplish far less than the last time we clashed!"

Uriel doesn't reply. She sends a telepathic command to her four Constructs, expertly commanding them to launch a flurry of attacks on the thundering Emperor soaring through the skies. The Cube spins rapidly, trying to drag Ose downward via gravity manipulation, while the two angel-shaped golems attack her from the front and back. Finally, the Artillery Orb launches fireball barrages at Ose, attempting to hit her with torrents of destructive magic.

_What's going on here?_ Uriel wonders. _Somehow, Ose's lightning cancelled-out Barbatos's magnetism. Is such a thing even possible?_

Using her mastery of four primary elements, Uriel unleashes a series of magical strikes on Ose's flickering figure. She discharges compressed streams of water, fireballs, wind-blades, and even tries to throw huge boulders at Ose. Her eyes glow with radiant light as she follows-up with bolts of holy energy, then tries to grab her ankles with dark magic, too.

All of Uriel's attacks fail. Not only does Ose power through any spells launched by the Archangel's golems, she also manages to slip through the gaps in their offenses time after time. 

Most stunningly, when the varying elemental spells rush at Ose's front and back, she merely laughs.

"Hahaha! Are you trying to tickle me, Archangel? You are a hundred million years too late to threaten this genius!"

Five beams of water crash against Ose's chest. Instead of short-circuiting her, a violent burst of electricity races up one of the water beams and surges toward Uriel's outstretched palm.

"Heavens!" Uriel cries. Before she can retract her mana, the bolt of lightning arcs into her body, courses through her veins, and causes her to cry out in pain. "Aaaargh!"

Meanwile, Ose casually swats away the boulders and wind-blades, then pauses to allow the firebolts to strike her back.

_Whoomph!_

Multiple explosions detonate against Ose's spine, yet her aura only strengthens!

"Haha! Hahahahah!!" Ose laughs. She dodges Uriel's light magic and dissipates her dark magic, then whirls to grin at the Archangel. "I've been waiting for an opponent upon whom I could test my newfound might! Mother gave me this transformation, and so, I shall honor her by unleashing its maximum power!"

Uriel's expression turns grim. She shakes off the numbness in her body and rises to her full height.

"Wretched demoness! Thy magic violates common sense! How can lightning alone interfere with all of the other elements?!"

"Curious?" Ose asks, her smile fading somewhat. "Electricity, heat, and other such forces all link together via the laws of physics. Of course, as an unenlightened chicken, you don't possess a millionth my knowledge. You could never wrap your feeble mind around the intricacies of quantum mechanics!"

Suddenly, Ose raises her right arm overhead. She curves her fingers into a claw-like shape, then swipes down with her arm.

In that moment, her thunder-powers tear a hole in the fabric of reality!

_Riiiiip!_

A shimmering gap appears in the air. A spatial tear revealing Uriel's body materializes in the sky, confounding the Archangel.

"What sorcery is this?"

Uriel narrows and widens her eyes repeatedly, trying to comprehend what her opponent has just done. Before she can reach even the vaguest of conclusions, Ose flickers toward the spatial tear and passes through it!

**THUMP!!**

A fist enveloped by condensed electrical energy strikes Uriel's back, sending her hurtling forward! She falls flat on her face and grinds across the dirt, only managing a quick glance backward, where she momentarily spots an identical spatial tear behind where she previously stood.

_That... that is an ability similar to Warper demons!_ Uriel cries out in her mind. _To leap through gaps in space, only my brother Raphael could accomplish such a feat with ease. I cannot replicate it no matter how hard I try!_

All these realizations pass through her mind in a fraction of a second. She quickly throws her palms against the ground and frontflips forward, revolving through the air to land gracefully on her feet. Even inside Barbatos's mutilated body, his hulking form, Uriel demonstrates incredible feats of agility.

"Die!"

Uriel fires a blast of holy energy at Ose's smirking figure, but the Emperor of Infiltration transforms into a bolt of lightning and flickers to the side, easily evading Uriel's spell.

"You and I aren't on the same level," Ose taunts. "We aren't even playing the same game! You are a mere toddler fumbling with powers beyond your comprehension, while I possess abilities enhanced by my cosmic knowledge!"

Ose conjures lightning into her palms, then leaps skyward.

"The humans have a saying, Uriel! Fear not the man who has practiced ten thousand kicks once, but he who has practiced one kick ten thousand times. You possess a vast array of formidable abilities, but your usage of them is sloppy and pathetic. That is why you will lose!"

Ose fires off both blasts of lightning, then flickers behind Uriel with her leg coiled for a brutal kick.

Uriel scowls.

"And what of he who has practiced ten thousand kicks ten thousand times, bloodskin?"

Ose's leg rushes toward Uriel's back, but Uriel whirls on her heel and spins to face her attacker! With two powerful blasts of lightning flying at her flank and the leg-strength of an Emperor aimed at her chest, her life hangs on a razor's edge.

At that moment, Uriel claps her palms together. Two immense shields of holy energy appear in front of and behind her!

_Thooom!_

Ose's thunder spells and her leg both crash against the barriers of holy energy, splattering against them like bugs striking a windshield. Before Ose can react, Uriel swings her palm forward, causing the holy energy barrier to fall forward and flatten Ose against the ground!

"Aargh!" Ose cries.

"Thou speaketh of practicing thy magic with such gusto!" Uriel taunts, "But I am an ancient monster from the Primordial Era. Even if I have long forgotten the memories Raphael sealed away, my body has not forgotten how to fight!"

Uriel waves her arms apart to dissipate her barrier, then conjures holy energy onto her left arm to grab Ose by the throat. She pins the demoness to the dirt, burning her with the Creator's light!

"I have killed millions, perhaps even billions of enemies!" Uriel roars. 

The Archangel smashes her right fist into Ose's face, pummeling her brutally.

"You, on the other hand, are little more to me than an unruly child! A brat who thinks far too highly of her meager skills!"

Uriel rears her arm back again. She pauses for a split-second to empower her bones with holy energy, then lunges it toward Ose's stomach.

"Brag all day, and brag all night, but know that your words are worth no more to me than empty air!"

Just before Uriel's punch obliterates Ose's torso, the demoness smirks, her bloodied face appearing particularly hideous at that moment. She flicks a single finger toward Uriel's charging fist, then fires a seemingly insignificant arc of electricity toward it.

_Zzt._

Instantly, Uriel's attack loses all of its momentum. The holy energy she built up dissipates like a fart in the wind, making her previously brutal punch turn into a light nudge when it strikes Ose's belly.

The Archangel sucks in an alarmed breath. "What?"

A moment later, the mana in her body rocks violently. Her right hand, previously grasping Ose's neck, momentarily loses its strength, allowing Ose to turn into formless electricity and zip away. By the time Uriel regains control of her body, Ose has already escaped her grip.

The Archangel jumps to her feet. Hatred burns in her eyes.

"Demoness! What... what devilry hath thou unleashed now?!"

Uriel whirls to face Ose. The Emperor of Infiltration stands a short distance away, her face still bloodied and bruised. With a quick crinkling of her nose, Ose magically heals her wounds, restoring her face to pristine condition. This, as part of her transformation's ability to convert electrical energy into a healing factor.

"Can't figure anything out? Aww, you poor little _birdie._ It must be hard for you to understand concepts like mana pathways, elementary quarks, subatomic particles, and other such constants of nature. Even if I tell you, it won't do you any good. You're too _stupid_ to match me."

Uriel grinds her teeth. She starts to take a step forward, but pauses, fear evident in her eyes.

_I haven't a clue how Ose disrupted the flow of my mana. Somehow, her seemingly insignificant bolt of lightning penetrated my body and... and..._

Doubts appear in her gaze. Uriel's constructs ready themselves to attack, but she doesn't give them any orders.

"Verily, thy magic cannot possibly be omnipotent..." Uriel murmurs. "Perhaps, in these past years, thou hath discovered some... secret technique..."

Ose snorts disdainfully. "Secret technique? Stupid bitch. You possess six elemental attributes, wield Barbatos's magnetism, and can even summon multiple holy constructs, yet you cannot defeat me, a lone Emperor with only a single elemental affinity. Ah, such is the gap between a genius and a piss-ant! What matters aren't the number of affinities you control, but how you use them, Uriel. It's no wonder your kind went extinct. You never tried teaching yourselves new tricks."

"Arrogant..." Uriel murmurs.

"Indeed, I am!" Ose laughs. "And I have the right to be. I rose from the bottom of demon society to the very top. The fate of the universe lies within my hands now, and I earned my position! Unlike you, born during an era of rich spiritual energy with a Creator-given body, I had to work to reach my current heights. That's why I'm unstoppable, because I have the determination bred in me from all those years of torment."

Sensing that her attacks won't land against the nimble and spry Emperor, Uriel doesn't make any sudden moves. Instead, she keeps talking to Ose, searching for any hints that will help her conquer this frightening foe.

"If I am truly such a 'piss-ant,' then surely, thou hath nothing to lose by explaining thy magical comprehensions," Uriel growls. "Perhaps thou feareth that I am much nimbler of mind than thou might first think."

Ose's maniacal grin momentarily lessens. An expression of contempt appears on her face as she sneers at the Archangel.

"Oh? Are you trying to trick me, Archangel? To give up my secrets willingly? Perhaps if I tell you how my magic works, you'll be able to defeat me?"

Ose laughs. "Hahaha! Pathetic! You still continue to struggle, even with your corpse looming on the horizon. Fine! I shall inform you of that which you can never understand. Even if you pick up some glimmers of knowledge, you'll never be able to defeat me!"

The Emperor jumps backward, away from Uriel. She backflips twice, then rips open a tear in space beneath herself and dives into it feet-first. As she does, a premonition of danger materializes in Uriel's chest! She jumps to the left, barely evading Ose's attack as she tries to stomp down on her head from above. Another spatial tear hangs in the sky above Uriel's previous position; the place from where Ose emerged.

"You're catching on!" Ose cackles. She initiates a flurry of kicks and punches, driving the Archangel backward. If Uriel counter-attacks, Ose merely flickers away or pounces through a spatial tear to attack the Archangel from another direction.

"How can I tear rips in space?" Ose asks. "Simple! Space is a construct of matter and gravity, and electrical energy binds the two together! I need only destabilize them to create a gap for my body to pass through!"

Ose pounces into the air and starts to motion with her hands to fire two more lightning blasts at Uriel, but Uriel's Cube Construct quickly revolves, tripling the gravity around the Demon Emperor.

"Worthless tricks," Ose grunts. She discharges a bolt of lightning toward her chest, which explodes upon touching her skin and destroys the gravitic space surrounding her. "Listen well, Uriel! Gravity is a fundamental force of nature! To manipulate it, your construct must create 'false matter' which can draw others toward it. I need only break that false matter to shatter its bonds!"

Uriel conjures a massive fireball in her palms, then flings it toward Ose. Ose roars with laughter, then spreads her arms apart to welcome the searing heat as it crashes upon her.

_Booom!_

It explodes, scorching her clothes and skin, yet still failing to injure her. Instead, Ose's aura surges with might.

"Fire is merely a construct of heat and energy! Compared to the power of fusion, such weak flames are nothing to me at all! These pitiful embers serve only to revitalize my body and empower my spirit. Truly, you are quite the benefactor to bequeath me such a gift!"

The more Ose speaks, the stranger Uriel's expression becomes.

_While it is true that I cannot fully understand Ose's explanations, even so, I feel as if they have resonated with me, somehow._

_Further, why does she run her mouth like so? Ose is no fool. She is a horrifying genius, one who as a mere Baron laid waste to humanity's majesty. For her to witlessly babble about her abilities, she has stepped well beyond the boundaries of her typical arrogance._

Uriel's eyes flash with insight. She dodges and deflects several more attacks from her opponent, while a new thought crosses her mind.

_Could it be? Might Ose's transformation be somehow affecting her personality? Such a thing is not outside the realm of possibility._

Uriel quickly recalls the many demons she's fought and observed over the last hundred-plus millennia. Of them, two stick out in her mind, echoing this battle so far.

_When Diablo transformed into the Archdemon, his strength increased explosively, but he lost his sanity. He became like a stupid animal, recklessly attacking friend and foe alike._

_When Bael assumes his Balrog Form, his brainpower likewise plunges into an abyss, causing him to act even less intelligently than usual._

Slowly, Uriel nods to herself. She continues putting up a perfunctory defense against Ose's onslaught, but behind the scenes, her mind calculates in secret.

"Water magic? Surely, that must be my weakness!" Ose continues to taunt. She claps her hands, firing a blast of thunder at Uriel and momentarily destabilizing her footing. "But even then, such an assumption is incorrect! 'Water' is nothing more than mashed-together hydrogen and oxygen molecules, both of which create the lifeblood of stars! They are the keys to creating fusion energy, so how could they ever hurt me?!"

As Ose continues to talk, Uriel's heart sinks.

_Indeed. Ose's personality seems to have grown far more cocky and arrogant than usual. Even so, I cannot see myself taking advantage of such a weakness. Her words ring true; she has learned how to shield herself from the majority of my attacks. Perhaps, only by seeking outside assistance will I be able to slay her._

Unwittingly, Uriel's eyes flick toward a small, shallow pit half a mile in the distance. Dozens of Demon Lords guard it, while the rest flow around it.

Inside, a single body lays, unmoving.

Belial, the Emperor of Passion.

Uriel returns her gaze to Ose.

_No. No! I will never seek assistance from Satan's Whore! That wench impaled me by assuming my brother's visage. Were it not for her act of deception, I would never have fallen, and her infernal husband would never have made it inside of Heaven! Because of her, Satan successfully exterminated ninety percent of my species! Angelkind died because of Belial! I will **never** forgive her!!_

Uriel's internal thoughts roar with fury, but at the same time, a second voice, one of reason, also speaks in her head.

_Tch. What is the point of maintaining old rivalries? Even if the Devil's Whore was the one who undertook vile actions to exterminate our race, she could still be a valuable ally in this battle. If I were to heal her, I could unleash her formidable strength against Ose. We could pin demonkind's most threatening Emperor in place, then rip out her intestines!_

A gold glimmer shines in Uriel's eyes.

_It won't be too late afterward. I can still slay Belial once the right moment reveals itself. I can still pay Satan's Whore back for all the atrocities she's enabled. Perhaps I'll even capture and torture her for the rest of eternity. Only then will I finally vent my hatred._

The Archangel blocks another of Ose's thunder-blasts. Her thoughts become resolute.

_I have simply too many demons to slay. Their numbers, unending. Ose and Belial are far from the only Emperors who must pay. Fenrir. Kristoff. Glinch. Fae. Auger. Yardrat. Gorn. Yama._

_These few are merely the tip of the iceberg. I must rip them all to pieces, as well as their subordinates, their lovers, their families, and any others who deem them allies._

A complicated expression appears on Uriel's face.

_To slay them all... 'tis an impossible task, one which I will surely fail to accomplish. Nevertheless, I must pursue it as my goal. I must avenge my people and wipe out those who genocided us from existence. Only then will I have the face to visit my brothers and sisters in the Great Beyond._

Uriel fortifies her mind. She continuously sneaks peeks at Belial while verbally sparring with Ose. She builds up her momentum, plays defensively to the best of her abilities, and doesn't overextend, knowing it will only waste her energy.

Even so, Ose's transformation doesn't wear off, nor dissipate. She maintains it during the whole battle, occasionally pausing to siphon energy from the humans' mecha and weapons to heal herself. She taunts Uriel, deriding the Archangel as stupid and incompetent, too weak to even flee.

All the while, Uriel waits for her chance.

Finally, after an hour of vicious and brutal combat, a distant noise erupts from Hero City's confines.

"**SKREEEEEEE!!**"

Ose and Uriel both whip their heads eastward. There, they see a shocking sight.

"A Hydra?!" Ose gasps. "It's... it's just like the one that attacked a month ago! Why has it reappeared?"

Uriel's eyes widen in surprise. "T-Titans?! Why are there Titans inside Hero City? Could this be one of Mephisto's secret weapons?! That confounded graverobber!"

Even as shock pulses through Uriel's heart and her outward expression echoes that fact, her mind remains calm.

_Chance! This is what I have waited for!_

With Ose momentarily distracted, Uriel sneakily burrows underground, falling into the planet's depths soundlessly.

She sucks Belial's body underground, relying on the Hydra's appearance to hide her actions. Even the demons assigned to guard Belial's body don't notice her disappearance, so violent is their reaction to the happenings in Hero City.

As Uriel pulls Belial's body into the planet's bowels, she gazes at her most hated enemy with venomous eyes. Momentarily unable to control herself, she wraps her hands around Belial's neck.

"Worthless wretch. I could slay thee with a thought. The only reason I shall allow thee to live is because thou provideth a slight value to me."

Her hands tremble as she traces her thumb against the contours of Belial's throat.

"One squeeze. Just _one_ twist, and thy life would end here. Oh, how long I have awaited such an opportunity."

Uriel's expression becomes bitter as she releases her grip on Belial's neck. Without any fanfare, she conjures water and presses it against Belial's wounds, healing the horrific injuries she's suffered.

"Thy awakening shall only occur for a brief while, Satan's Whore. After thou fulfilleth my needs, I shall slayeth thee with my own hands. Thou shalt joineth Satan in the Great Beyond."

By the time Ose realizes her opponent has disappeared, she's too late. Uriel finishes healing Belial's wounds, then she injects mana into the Emperor's body to awaken her.

"Serve me well... Belial. Then thou may continue living a bit longer."
