A look of worry spread across his face. He glanced at the human male. "Turtle, are you absolutely certain?"

"Mhm. A hundred percent." The boy leaned back casually and scratched his head. He seemed perplexed, likely about the riddle. This should be interesting. Perhaps his guess would be more creative than others she'd heard.

The crocodile croaked nervously. "Hurgh. Very well, then. The answer to your riddle is... Man."

Bahamut's body froze. A thousand needles of shock lanced into her stomach, causing gaseous pain to erupt in her chest.

_What._
