_Satan. The former First Emperor of demonkind._

Jason pulled himself to his feet shakily. His spherical vision gave him a good look at the Devil as he approached. Huge, seven-inch horns stuck out of his head. A suit similar to Beelzebub's, but unbuttoned and missing a tie, gave him a much more casual and relaxed appearance than the former. His slicked-back hair and general demeanor made him look like an Italian mobster. The suspenders and goatee, however, gave him a sleazier aura. Jason had always wondered if the Devil was a cross between a lawyer and a used-car salesman, but he hadn't expected to be right.

Satan was also far shorter than other demons Jason had met, at well under six feet. Hell, he was only a few inches taller than Amelia in her adult body.

_This_ guy is the most powerful demon who has ever walked the mortal plane? That doesn't seem right at all. Jason thought.

The former First Emperor strode toward Jason, pulled a cigar out of his pocket, and lit it with a tiny flame from his thumb. After inhaling, he waved his thumb to put out the fire, and his eyes scanned Jason up and down. "Oh? You're a tall one. Enhancement magic, I'd wager. The babyface gives it away."

Jason glanced past him at Amelia. She peeked through her fingers at him, then quickly wrapped her arms around her legs, pulling them up to hide her face.

_Amelia summoned Satan to fight me. Is that because she hasn't the heart to kill me? I thought she hated me. I assaulted her and nearly... I nearly..._ Jason thought.

_Dammit. How could she hesitate? Why? I don't deserve her mercy, not at a time like this._

Satan stopped, ten feet separating them. He looked up at Jason. "Nothing to say? Just gonna stand there like a piss-ant?"

A female voice appeared in the back of Jason's mind. He recognized it as the echo of Joan. Sometimes the Sword from Heaven made Joan's voice appear in his mind.

_Demon. Kill demon. Enemy of God. Kill demon._ Joan's voice whispered.

A different male voice spoke. _Fight. Kill. Kill. Kill._

A third voice wailed in pain. _Please, god, don't let him live! It hurts! My body hurts!_

The same voices had been screaming at Jason only minutes ago, telling him to hurt Amelia. He had listened to them then... but maybe he shouldn't now—

_FIGHT. KILL DEMON. FIGHT._

_Damn, they're getting louder again!_ Jason thought.

Shaking the voices from his head, he used telekinesis to yank Catherine back to him. He didn't want to fight Amelia, but it wasn't her standing in front of him; it was a Demon Emperor.

"Satan, is it? You're shorter than I expected."

He stuck his left hand in his suit pocket. "Yeah? I didn't expect anything from you, and I'm still disappointed."

"Sorry I let you down. Are we going to fight, or what?"

"No need to rush to your death, idiot." Satan nodded at Catherine. "Nice sword. Know how to use it?"

"I've trained," Jason replied while thinking to himself, _kinda._

Satan inhaled and glanced down at a stupidly colorful watch on his right wrist, one covered in gems and other tacky bling. As he looked at it, Jason frowned.

_Wait, that's not a watch... it's a fucking wrist-mounted sundial._ Jason thought.

Satan evaluated it for three seconds, cocking it around as the sun dipped below the horizon. "Never mind; time's running short. Look, I'm a nice guy. If you can land a hit on me in the next five minutes, I won't break every bone in your body before I kill you. I'll handicap myself too. I won't use my arms or my legs to attack or defend. Sound fair?"

Jason's eyes narrowed. "What? Are you fucking with me?"

A shrug. "Depends. By the way... that whole dark energy thing? Tacky. You're overcompensating. Like I always say, if your name ain't Valac, put that edgy evil look back."

Jason's mind argued with itself. _Is this guy serious? He's weird! But then, maybe he's unthinkably powerful. Or, he could be bluffing! I defeated Amelia with ease. She beat him, so he must be weaker than her, right?_

_No, dumbass, it's never that easy. You don't know how their battle went. Maybe he was already weakened. Take it slow. Assess him cautiously, then go for a killing blow!_

Satan, his eyes still closed, sighed. "Those five minutes started ticking, kid. If you don't hit me by the time this cigar's gone, you're gonna regret it."

_Fuck it, attack!_ Jason thought.

Jason lunged forward, using his telekinesis to shove himself to the side, before jumping back at the last second, sword held defensively at the ready.

Satan stood still. He opened his eyes. "The hell was that? Are you dancing? Get a move on, boy!"

_He didn't flinch. It's like he knew I was going to play it safe for the first round._ Jason thought as a bead of sweat dripped down his forehead.

Not bothering to answer, Jason jumped at him again, this time rearing his sword back to slash at him.

Jason's blade swung at Satan's backside.

_WHAM!_

It stopped a millimeter from his skin, as if striking a wall of iron.

"What?!" Jason quickly pulled back again, scanning him to look for damage. There was none. "I hit you!"

Satan rechecked his sundial. "Afraid not. Four and a half minutes left, chum."

"The sun's already dipping past the horizon! A sundial can't tell time if there's no sun!"

Satan shook his head. "Not with that attitude. My beloved Bertha is never wrong. Better hurry up."

Jason's teeth ground together. _If Satan isn't going to attack, and I get four minutes of him only playing defense, I might as well use my advantage._

"**Speed. Speed. Strength. Strength. Perception. Stabilize.**"

Jason glanced down at his boots. _The Boots of Hermes allow the wearer to teleport rapidly at short range, and they offer enhanced speed, but they're difficult to control. Orion's Belt already augments my strength, and Achilles' armor protects me from damage, so I should be able to pierce the Devil's defense with ease!_

This time, Jason didn't hold back. In an instant, he crossed the small gap separating them, delivering a flurry of stabs and jabs, each one striking an invisible barrier surrounding Satan's body. He began teleporting around him, slashing, stabbing, and sending rocks and pebbles flying at his body with his telekinesis.

The flurry continued unabated for what felt like an eternity. Suddenly, Jason jumped away as Satan pulled his left hand out of his pocket. He raised it to his mouth and... covered a yawn. "Aaahhn... Mmm. Three minutes. Don't go easy on me now, y'hear?"

Jason's breath came in short gasps, though he stifled the noise, not wanting to look pathetic in front of the Devil himself. _I gave it my all. Something invisible surrounds Satan's body, and I can't get past it. A barrier?_

Satan glanced at Amelia, then back to Jason. "Look, I know it's poor form to give an opponent tips during a battle, but I feel a bit bad for you. Tell you what, kid. You should probably avoid using melee attacks. Only a few people have ever pierced my Ultimate Defense with a weapon, and their lives ended quite horribly." He stuck his left hand back in his pocket. "I'm trying to help you out, boyo. Oh, and don't forget the timer."

_This little fucker,_ Jason thought to himself. _I can't stand his smug grin!_

The echo of Joan's voice whispered in Jason's mind. _Don't hold back. Use my full power. Catherine knows the Devil's weakness._

Jason's eyes drifted to the sword. He had yet to use its secret abilities. He didn't want to break his mind again... but... against Satan, holding back would be a bad choice.

He set his jaw and locked his mental vision onto the Devil. _Catherine. Give me enough strength to crush this smug asshole!_

A shadowy image of Joan's face appeared in his mind. She smiled, and her grin grew sinister. _Use it._

Suddenly, his body sank underground. The sword activated, giving him Joan's power of earth manipulation. He burrowed rapidly, erupting from under Satan, and the sword twitched with anticipation, dragging him around behind it. _One Thousand Cuts,_ it yelled into his mind.

The weapon slashed and sliced rapidly, each attack swiftly blocked by Satan, attacking his legs, arms, chest, every part of him all at once. However, Satan took two steps back and frowned. "Hm?"

Jason's arms bulged with power as Catherine summoned strength into his body. While she cut, he lunged his unencumbered arm out and grabbed Satan's left leg. To his surprise, he managed to pierce through whatever was protecting him. Satan's eyes bolted down. "What? How?!"

Jason teleported fifty feet into the sky, then used his momentum to hurl the Demon Emperor down. His suddenly freed hand summoned a magical spear of light, one that was nearly four feet long. Before Satan could hit the ground, Jason hurled the holy weapon at him. It raced forward at the speed of sound and created a sonic boom when it impacted his body.

_BATOOOM!_

Jason teleported to the ground with the power of Hermes' Boots. Unlike before, his breath came steadily. The dust around Satan settled and...

_Shit_.

Satan stood in a shallow crater with his lit cigar resting lazily in his mouth.

"Not bad. The grab caught me off-guard, but then, I am a little rusty. A hundred thousand years locked inside the Black Witch's vacant mind will do that to a fella."

"I didn't hurt you?" Jason asked. "But... the light-spear."

Satan grunted. "Nice touch, that one. The thing is, you're supposed to use a flashy attack like that to finish a target with style, not as a desperate attempt to hurt them. Doesn't look very badass. Two minutes left, by the way."

_MOTHER FUCKER!_ Jason's teeth ground together as he tried to assess the situation. _If only I had the Crown!_

Satan chuckled. "Oh, incidentally, I've been meaning to say... love the boots. Hermes. Great guy. I killed him. Long story."

"You did?"

A nod. "Yup. I didn't want to, but I was young and needed power. He wasn't an Archangel, though I killed a few of those during my heyday as well. Hermes was one of the better messenger angels, and that was back before the telephone and email and a bunch of other human inventions." Satan tapped his sundial. "The good old days. Dunno why I'm bringing that stuff up now. Must be my nostalgia gettin' the better of me. Anyway, one-point-five minutes left, lad."

_I can't keep letting Satan stall for time!_ Jason's hands started to shake as he considered his options. _I can't kill him with a single word. Maybe I should summon a black hole. However, that would cost a lot of energy, which I don't have. I can't make a gun without the Crown, nor can I make anything else that's mechanically complex..._

Catherine whispered to Jason again. _I defeated the Black Witch. Use the secret power. Steal his energy for yourself._

Secret power?

Something clicked in Jason's head. Focusing his mind, he formed a giant invisible hand out of telekinesis. The psychic palm reached out and grabbed Satan, pinning him in place. Judging by the surprise on his face, he hadn't expected the move.

Jason lunged forward and activated _Blade of a Thousand Cuts_. As before, Satan quickly blocked all of Catherine's attacks, but this time, with him pinned in place, Jason released the sword, teleported behind the Devil, and grabbed him by the back of the neck.

"**Drain!**"

Immediately, Satan's demonic energy rushed into Jason's body. The Demon Emperor gasped for breath. The cigar dropped from his mouth, and a rush of mana swelled in Jason's chest. Before he could take a swing at Jason, Jason teleported back, grabbed Catherine before she hit the ground, and put all his power in an upward strike. The sheer force of his attack cleaved the Demon Emperor in two, but he quickly followed up with a flurry of other attacks, chopping, cutting, and slicing him into a million pieces.

Satan fell to the ground and became a puddle of flesh.

Jason gasped wildly as his vision wavered.

_I did it! Holy fucking shit! I can't... I can't believe it!_ Jason thought.

"Amelia! Did you see that?"

Jason turned to look at the place she was sitting.

She wasn't there anymore. Only a hole in the ground and her two severed arms remained.

Suddenly, the wind began to hiss around the crimson puddle which used to be the Devil. Blood-colored energy poured from Satan's corpse, dissolved into mist in the air, and the Emperor regenerated atop his remains. His bones appeared first. Then, his muscles and skin.

However, unlike before, when he emerged from the bloody mist, all of his clothes were missing.

Jason gaped in disbelief at the First Emperor. Never, not even in his worst nightmares, had he expected Satan could return from the dead, and certainly not without a scratch on his skin!

Satan stared down at his exposed self, then gingerly bent over to pick up his sundial before tying it back onto his wrist. "I didn't expect you to mix things up. You're more inventive than I gave you credit for."

"I killed you! How did you survive?!"

He swept his hair back with one hand and struck a pose with the other. "Oh, that? Easy. You see, I've formed contracts with many souls, and each one allows me to regenerate from death exactly once. Unfortunately for you, I have ten thousand, four hundred and sixteen souls banked up."

The blood drained from Jason's face. "No... you're lying. You must be."

He leaned forward with a smile. "I hope you've got more of that inventiveness left. Less than a minute left to make me regenerate ten thousand times! Hop to it, pal!"

All of Jason's confidence vanished. _Satan's unstoppable. He's invincible. It took everything I had to beat him once. How am I supposed to do it ten thousand times?!_ Jason thought.

Jason's eyes jumped to where Amelia had sat. _Why did she leave? Where did Amelia go? What's that hole in the ground?!_

............................................

_Not long before._

Amelia had since shifted to wrapping her arms around her legs and burying her face in her knees. "I don't wanna. I've had enough of this day. Once Satan's done, I'll enslave Jason. That's all there is to it. Okay?"

Blaarjiim crossed his arms in her mind's eye. "Come now. Don't be sad. It's obvious you care for him. Soon he'll be with you forever. You can summon him whenever you want. You can make him do _anything_ for you. Think of the fun times you'll—"

"Blaarjiim. Please. Don't." Amelia cut him off before he could continue.

_I don't want that kind of relationship with Jason. I don't want to think about it. Using him like he nearly used me would be horrible. Sickening._ Amelia thought.

_Once he's under my control, he won't disobey my orders. Even if he wants to, he won't. But that's awful! I don't mind using my power on the others... they all hurt me in different ways. Jason is different, though! He's different!_

The ground shook, and she forced herself not to peek. Blaarjiim turned away. "Ooh, I didn't see that coming! He summoned one of Joan's light-spears!"

"Don't tell me, Blaarjiim. I don't want to listen."

Amelia's closest friend in the whole universe sighed and looked at her. "I hate seeing you in this state, my queen. I've seen you weep before, but never to such a degree. I won't enjoy seeing the mortal get torn to shreds at this rate."

"You poor thing," Amelia muttered bitterly. "I'm sorry I ruined _your_ day."

Finally, Blaarjiim became more self-conscious. He materialized and lightly touched the back of her skull. He tried to reach out and caress her hair, but his body had no substance. It was all an illusion in her mind.

"I apologize. I should not prod you so. Why do you have feelings for this mortal? He descends from your greatest enemies, after all."

"Dunno," Amelia mumbled. "It's not like I can help the way I feel."

Blaarjiim lowered his voice to a whisper. "Once you enslave Jason, he will never hurt you again. If you still hold feelings for him, you will both be able to trust each other in the same way as you do me. Won't that be nice? Please cheer up. Smile."

Amelia shook her head. "I can't. I don't want to. I—"

The ground shifted beneath her again, and she flinched, knowing Jason and Satan were clashing.

Blaarjiim's eyes widened. "My queen! Under you! It's—"

Something erupted from the ground underneath her. Too late, she started pulling her head back from her legs, and a hand wrapped around her mouth.

"Mgmm?!" She tried to say something, but the hand silenced her. Then, ten more appeared and grabbed all of her limbs. They wrapped around her, and a second later pulled with all their strength, dragging her underground.

_Oh god! What the hell?! Get off me!_ Amelia thought.

Blaarjiim yelled in alarm as the surface vanished, and the darkness of the underground world took her. Tens of tons of dirt and stone enveloped her body and crushed her, making it impossible to breathe or move. Pitch black darkness surrounded her. She couldn't even whimper or cry out as they started squeezing her throat.

"They're Ghouls! Undead monsters! Fight! Break free!" Blaarjiim yelled frantically, and Amelia struggled to move, but their strength was incredible!

The further the undead creatures dragged her underground, the more unbearable the weight of the darkness became. No matter how she struggled, she couldn't break free.

Suddenly, blinding pain erupted through every part of her body. She opened her mouth to scream, causing dirt to surge into her mouth, gagging and panicking her.

_Shik, Shik, Shik!_

Something horrible started stabbing her stomach, chest, and back. A voice cackled as the ghouls dragged her further and further, their hands grabbing and groping every inch of her body.

"Yesss, thisss isss my moment to ssshine!" A phantom face appeared before her, as a ghostly figure continued stabbing her over and over.

_I can't break free! Jason! Jason!! HELP!!!_ Amelia thought desperately.

Blaarjiim screamed into her mind. "Fight! Don't you dare die to a low-level Duke! Use your power! Unleash it!"

The horrible pain continued for several minutes. The Demon Duke of Mist, Mephisto, hovered in front of her, rubbing his hands with glee as his awful monsters bit and chewed her all over.

"Yesss, thisss isss my moment to ssshine! I will hold and torture you here for the ressst of your daysss!"

He continued stabbing, but the pain wasn't as unbearable as it had been when he first grabbed her. Blaarjiim's energy entered her body. The fear rushed away. Even while the pain blinded and sickened her, she clenched her fists.

_The hell you will! I don't need Jason to save me! I'll kill you myself!!_ Amelia thought fiercely.

"Bleaagghh!" The dirt blasted from her mouth as rage swelled within her.

_MEPHISTO! I'LL TEAR YOU TO PIECES!_
