Part 279: Blinker's Gain, Blinker's Loss

**[Recommended Listening](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f8r-hMQzRXk)**

Blinker's psionic barrier explodes outward, blasting away Zamiel, Mephisto, and more than a hundred of Mephisto's Wraiths. All of Blinker's enemies cry out in surprise and alarm, with Zamiel the most surprised. Before he can react, Blinker rockets into the air and flies at him with her fist reared back.

_THUNK!_

Blinker smashes Zamiel's stomach, blasting the wind out of him and sending him violently crashing into the dirt below. Zamiel bounces off the ground once, but Blinker falls upon him again, this time with ten afterimages of her fist materializing in that instant.

_THUMP-THUMP-THUMP-THUMP!_

She brutally pummels Zamiel, one hit after another, pulling back only at the last second to retreat to her husband's body. Zamiel drags along the dirt, tearing out a massive crevasse in the valley. For a few moments, his consciousness wavers between life and death. It's only his Emperor-level physique that saves him from perishing under the Monster Queen's onslaught.

_Huk... what the... devil?!_

Zamiel struggles to catch his breath. Multiple internal injuries, including ruptured organs, plague his body. He opens his eyes just in time to see Blinker levitating Kar's corpse into the air. Tears fill the fairy's eyes as she sends her husband away, teleporting him somewhere unknown. 

_How could... how could the Monster Queen become so powerful?_ Zamiel gasps.

After a few seconds, his eyes flicker with recognition.

_She must have absorbed the Sphinx's power! I never imagined she would be so brutal as to finish off her own husband! No, wait, that makes no sense. She probably solved his riddle instead. Shit! That... that greedy little..._

Zamiel motions with his hands. Thanks to his vampiric abilities, he manages to repair his internal organs by using his own blood. However, his vitality diminishes significantly, making the color drain from his skin.

_Hah! So what if she took her husband's power?! That only means I have to kill her to reap the Sphinx's magic. With Mephisto and me working together, she's as good as dead!_

Zamiel jumps to his feet. Behind Blinker, Mephisto's ghosts reappear, along with the Duke of Mist himself.

"Ksss! Damned woman!" Mephisto cries. "Ssso what if you took the Sssphinx'sss power?! My minionsss and I are more than enough to ssslay the likesss of you!"

Blinker's expression hovers on the edge of insanity. Her eyes, puffy, red, and swollen, look somewhat demonic.

"You murdered him!" She roars. "You killed Kar! I won't let any of you... leave here ALIVE!"

_Whoomph!_

An aura of inconceivable power erupts from Blinker's body. Having previously increased her size to that of a seven-foot-tall human, she appears about the same height as Zamiel, significantly taller than the hunched-over figure of Mephisto. Thick, intimidating muscles sprout along her body. Her biceps and glutes flex, revealing fine, neatly carved lines, like those belonging to the mightiest Titans.

Blinker points a finger at Zamiel.

"You! You must die FIRST!"

Not one hint of mercy rests within Blinker's eyes. Only rage, anger, and anguish present themselves.

Instantly, Blinker rockets toward Zamiel, roaring like a Banshee. The Duke's veins momentarily turn to ice out of sheer terror.

_Holy crap! That's one scary fairy!_

Zamiel quickly congeals his mana into Manifested Qi, a type of weapon energy capable of taking many different forms. Unlike before, when he made it assume the properties of a sword, he transforms the purple, viscous energy into something resembling a giant, transparent tower shield. 

Blinker crashes into the shield at full speed, the sheer power behind her attack partially passing through the Qi to strike Zamiel's body.

"Argh!" Zamiel howls. He stomps his feet into the dirt and furrows his brow, trying desperately to hold on, before the pressure from Blinker's attack instantly disappears. Caught off-guard, he stumbles forward, then jerks his eyes to the side, where he sees afterimages of Blinker's body circling around to his flank.

"Crap!"

Zamiel spins his shield around to form a hasty barrier behind himself. Much weaker than the first one, he doesn't even get to solidify his footing before Blinker kicks against it and throws him off his feet.

The Duke of Weaponry goes flying for the second time in thirty seconds. He bounces off the terrain and forcibly shifts his momentum by turning his Qi into a mattress-like cushion to slow his flight. When he flips himself around and sees Blinker coming at him again, his expression turns grim.

_I was wrong! The Monster Queen is way stronger than before! Her power is ludicrous! It's like facing two Sphinxes at the same time!_

Blinker, formerly a fairy with a weak physique but mighty and versatile magic, receives no benefit to her magical attributes as a result of consuming the Sphinx's aura. Instead, her physical body elevates to the same level as Kar's, giving her an extremely well-rounded set of abilities.

With a top tier body, and top tier fairy magic, Blinker becomes terrifying beyond belief.

Just before Blinker reaches Zamiel for another follow-up, she stops mid-movement and retreats, narrowly avoiding a hail of fists from Mephisto's Wraiths. These astral enemies bellow and shriek, diving at Blinker from all angles to surround and bully her.

Blinker's expression become livid. "These... these _toys_ stopped me once. But no more! BREAK!"

She swipes out her palms and tears at the air. Instantly, a pair of wraiths explode into astral essence, diffusing into the wind. Unlike before, when she fought them somewhat half-heartedly, thinking she and her husband would eventually win out and overpower Mephisto's goons, Blinker no longer allows herself an inch of compromise.

Her expression speaks a million words of regret, all of them aimed at herself.

_Kar died because I assumed he couldn't lose. I took him too lightly. I took everything too lightly._

_Now he's gone... and his blood is on my hands._

Without holding back, Blinker rapidly rakes the air. She tears twenty Wraiths apart in an instant, ripping their souls into shreds with her telekinetic magic. 

During the few seconds Blinker becomes occupied with killing the Wraiths, Mephisto draws near. He lurks beneath the planet's soil where she can't see. Blinker hovers only twenty feet off the ground, a distance too close for comfort when dealing with Dukes and Emperors.

In a flash, Mephisto attacks! He pounces at her from below and plunges a knife into her back during a moment of distraction, causing Blinker to howl in agony. Mephisto's Pain Inducement sends lightning pouring into her figure, momentarily paralyzing her. He stabs her three times more, aiming for her shoulders and heart.

"Kekeke! Never underessstimate the- eh?"

To Mephisto's shock, Blinker's figure fades away mid-stab, causing 'sweat' to drench his bones.

"An illusssion?!"

Mephisto whirls around in horror, just in time to see Blinker pop out of seemingly midair, her body previously hidden inside a crack in space. Unable to react in time, the Duke of Mist can only watch helplessly as a psionic-energy-coated fist flies at his face.

_THUMP!!_

Blinker savagely pounds Mephisto into the dirt. Struck dumb by her attack, his body momentarily loses its ethereal properties, causing him to physically strike the dirt and bounce off it.

"Kuh! Y-you damned trickssster fairy!"

Having not expected Blinker to retain her sanity, Mephisto ends up caught off-guard by her subterfuge.

The Monster Queen's eyes boil with emotions far beyond the realm of mere 'hatred.'

"You two. You're still thinking about how you'll eventually kill me. You continue to lust after the Sphinx's power. Stupid... **bastards**! Forget killing me. You won't leave here alive. Even if you do, I'll hunt you for the rest of eternity. I'll chase you to the ends of the galaxy. You will never live another waking day without fearing my arrival."

Blinker raises her fist. It trembles with a rage so pure that goosebumps start appearing on Zamiel's body.

"Kar wasn't only my husband. He was my one true love. We shared the warmth of every waking day. We looked forward to an eternity spent comforting each other. Now... I have nothing. **Nothing**!"

Slowly, Blinker uncurls her fist. She lowers her palm to her belly while glaring daggers at Zamiel.

"Did you know, _little Duke_? I never got to give my husband the _good news_. He died without knowing about the children he gave me. You took our happiest moment away from each other... the pinnacle of our union. Death is a luxury too wonderful for you. Not even an eternity of torture would suffice for what you've stolen."

An ominous feeling washes over Zamiel's skin. Seeing the horrific expression directed at him from the Monster Queen, the Duke of Weaponry trembles ceaselessly, unwilling to imagine just what horrors await him if that terrifying lunatic gets her hands on him.

"B-boss? Boss! What do we do?!"

Unknowingly, his tone toward Mephisto becomes deferential once again. Having relied on Mephisto's guidance his whole life, and having been raised from imp-hood to his current level of power by the Duke of Mist, Zamiel always, without fail, turns to his 'Boss' during similar moments of crisis.

Mephisto glowers at the Monster Queen. Without a word, he melts into the ground, leaving Zamiel behind.

"Boss? BOSS?!"

Zamiel panics. He turns to look at the fairy, only to see her figure shimmer and vanish.

From inches behind Zamiel's head, a female voice whispers.

"Where are you looking... little Duke?"

Zamiel screams. "Ahhhhh!!!"

He jumps to his feet and throws a random punch in the voice's direction, only to strike the empty air. A slight motion in the wind catches Zamiel's attention. He turns to see Blinker's leg spin-kicking his face.

_THUNK!_

Blinker savagely introduces her heel to the bridge of Zamiel's nose, horrifying him with not only the strength of her attack, but his complete inability to defend against her illusory powers. Unlike before, when Blinker fought him and Duriel using only her illusion magic, her body has become formidable enough that she can battle him physically and magically at the same time.

Zamiel hurtles to the west. He tries to summon a barrier of Qi to protect himself, but Blinker teleports into his path and swings her fist at his incoming head.

_WHUMP!_

The Duke's thoughts go dark. He crashes headfirst into a boulder, pummeling it into powder. The impact knocks him unconscious for several seconds, and he wakes up to find himself flying upward in an arc, with the Monster Queen holding onto his ankle. She swings him overhead like a sledgehammer, then pounds him against the dirt.

Over and over, the Monster Queen brutalizes Zamiel. Unable to catch a break for even a second, he flops limply in her grasp, his consciousness fading in for mere seconds at a time, just before crashing into something and battering him comatose once more.

...

After five full minutes of savagery, Blinker stops. She kneels down, grabs the back of Zamiel's neck, and pins his unmoving body to the dirt.

"Where are you, Mephisto? Come out."

Her words evaporate into the wind. The valley where countless demons once stomped lies eerily still, with a horde of them having stopped their advance to the east, too terrified by the Monster Queen's rampage to advance. The rest lay dead at the human army's feet to the west.

Mephisto doesn't appear. Instead, his voice transmits via the wind.

"Kekeke. You are fearsssome, Monssster Queen. I mussst admit... a mere Duke like me isss no match for the likesss of you."

Blinker places her free palm atop Zamiel's scalp.

"Show yourself, or I'll blast your minion's brains to mush."

"Do asss you wisssh..." Mephisto whispers, still not revealing himself. "My power isss far too insssignificant to defeat a warrior sssuch asss you. Plusss, we both know there isss no way you will give my minion to me."

Mephisto pauses for a moment, as if to let his words hang in the air.

Then, he continues.

"Monssster Queen. Where, persssay, did you hide the Monssster King'sss body? I cannot ssseem to find it."

Blinker narrows her eyes. "I know your tricks well, Mephisto. You won't touch my husband. Not now. Not ever. I will keep him at my side until the date of his cremation. Don't even think of finding him. I've hidden Kar somewhere the likes of you can never reach."

"Oh? Isss that ssso? Tsssk, how unfortunate..."

Mephisto's voice fades away, leaving Blinker puzzled.

_I hid Kar's body inside a folded space. Mephisto might be ethereal, but he isn't a spatial manipulator. He won't find Kar, no matter how hard he tries._

Blinker waits for several seconds, but Mephisto still doesn't speak, seemingly having disappeared into the nether.

Unsatisfied, Blinker shoots a hateful glare at the unmoving figure at her feet.

"I would love to torture you until you lose all your sanity. Sadly, if I do that, you might break free someday. Kar would never forgive me if I allowed that. I'll just have to ensure that Mephisto can't use you for his evil deeds ever again."

Blinker's eyes lose some of their overwhelming hatred. The Fairy Queen exhales, then wraps Zamiel's body with psionic energy. His figure trembles uncontrollably as she infiltrates all of his vital organs and bones, reaching deep down inside of him to grab as many different points as she can. 

A moment later, Blinker jumps to her feet and yanks her psionic energy apart. Zamiel's body explodes like a bomb, spraying rivers of gore in all directions. His brains and bones alike coat Tarus II's soil, making it impossible for Mephisto to resurrect him. After all, Necromancers might possess incredible means, but without a body, they won't have anything to revive.

Still unsatisfied, Blinker stares bitterly at Zamiel's exploded corpse. 

Memories surface in her mind.

She recalls the first time she met Kar in the Labyrinth's Core.

Memories of his face appear in her mind. Their shared bond which transcended species.

She thinks back on how she first started out as only his little fairy assistant, and how their relationship swiftly progressed when few were around to see. With Kar's ability to shrink, and her ability to grow, they shared many quiet moments together, reminiscing about the past.

Blinker's expression darkens. She remembers the first time Kar told her about what happened to the Crocodiles. She remembers the sad, helpless look in his eyes as he broke down, telling her about Pyra's death.

The Battle Brothers. They slaughtered his people.

Kar lived for one reason, and one reason only: To kill the monsters who murdered everyone he loved.

Blinker's lower lip quivers. She presses a bloody fist against her chest and chokes back a sob.

"We got 'em, gator-man. We got the Battle Brothers."

Tears streak down Blinker's face, followed soon by a look of deliberation.

"There's still one more to kill, Kar. I won't let you down. I'll kill Mephisto. For you. He was the true architect of the Crocodiles' demise. If our children are to live safely in this new world... I'll have to slaughter the one who brought about their ancestors' end."

With her willpower strengthened, Blinker slowly nods.

She raises her head, only for her heart to skip a beat.

Half a mile away, mired in a fog shrouding the valley, a familiar figure stands.

Kar, the Monster King.

Blinker's blood turns to ice.

"What? No. No! How... how did...?!"

Mephisto rises from the ground. A sinister grin spreads across his face as he chuckles evilly. He raises a palm to show a glowing orb of mana-like energy struggling to escape his bony fingers.

"Kekeke. Sssilly woman. My meansss are beyond your imagination. I thank you for the Monssster King'sss corpssse!"

Blinker's eyes widen in horror. "You... you son of a BITCH! Give Kar back to me! Filthy graverobber!!"

She lunges toward Mephisto, but with such a huge distance separating them, Mephisto easily motions with his hands, sucking himself and Kar's body into the ground.

"NOOOOOO!!" Blinker shrieks. She smashes into the soil and begins ripping it to shreds, tearing out tons of dirt and rock furiously, flinging it to the side without a care in the world. Unlike Kar himself, who possessed Earth Manipulation, Blinker wields no such power. She tries desperately to dig to Kar's body, but ultimately fails due to Mephisto's inconceivable means.

Soon after, a voice drifts upon the wind.

"You almossst had me. Sssadly, you forgot one key detail, Monssster Queen. You forgot about... Sssoul Resssonance..."

Blinker pauses her frenzied digging. She gazes at the ripped-apart dirt, unable to fully understand Mephisto's words.

"Soul Resonance?!"

"Ehehehe! That isss right. Kar'sss body died. His sssoul passsed to me. I usssed hisss sssoul to locate hisss corpssse. Not ssso difficult to underssstand, isss it?"

Mephisto pauses, allowing his words to ruminate in Blinker's psyche before continuing.

"And asss for hiding hisss body in a folded ssspaccce... _clever_! Too bad I am the King of the Dead. I have many ssspatial manipulatorsss buried within my tombsss. Well, I've thanked you enough for your generousss contribution. Fare thee well, Monssster Queen. I've reaped many rewardsss from thisss battle. Ehehehehe!!"

Blinker's thoughts turn blank. "Give him back! GIVE HIM BAAACK! I'll chase you forever, Mephisto! You'll never be safe! Do you hear me?! YOU'LL NEVER BE SAFE!!"

Mephisto doesn't reply. He fades into the distance, no longer caring about what happens next on Tarus II.

Ultimately, he has traded two Baron minions for one Emperor.

As far as gains go, none hold a candle to him.

Blinker flops into her burrowed pit. She sobs uncontrollably, rage and grief flooding her mind.

"Kaa-aaa-aar! Nooo... Uhuuu... Kar, I'm sorry! I'm so sorry!!"

No longer able to restrain herself, Blinker leaps out of the pit. Her gaze flashes toward the demon army holding their line to the east. A vicious expression crosses her face as she blasts toward them at supersonic speeds.

"All demons must DIE!!"

...

_Meanwhile. Among the demonic army to the east._

A pair of Demon Lords watch the events in the valley uneasily.

"Uh. Should we charge in again?" The fatter, uglier Lord asks. "Maybe we can slip past while the Monster Queen is fighting Zamiel."

His skinnier brother rolls his eyes. "No way, Skitch. You kidding? We already lost a bunch of Barons earlier. We'll just hold here and wait for reinforcements."

"Sure, okay..." Skitch mumbles. "Just, uh... seems like we should be doing our part, y'know?"

"Yeah, yeah. Goodie-goodie butt-kisser..." The skinny Lord grumbles. "Ose's over on the Western Front. She's an Emperor. Who do we got? A couple of weak-ass Dukes against a Monster Emperor. I'll just sit here and keep my tush nice and safe where the humans can't hit us."

Skitch winces as Zamiel's body gets blasted apart by Blinker's magic.

"Ow. Okay, that looked like it hurt."

"Duh. You think getting your body 'sploded is fun?"

"I'm just saying..." Skitch mumbles. "Why you gotta be so mean, Vex?"

"Sorry..." Vex mutters. "It's just... this whole thing's got me on edge. Me and my guy are going through a bit of a rough patch. You remember Fiolo, right?"

Skitch nods. "Uh huh. I heard you got yourself an incubus boyfriend."

"Sure did..." Vex mutters, smirking proudly. A moment later, his expression falls. "Problem is, the guy's a damn sex fiend. Can't get done with one session before he's ready for rounds two, three, and four. Says he wants to bring in some other partners to liven things up."

"Mmm. Seems fine to me," Skitch grunts. "What's the problem?"

"Eh, I dunno. Guess I'm just a one-demon-at-a-time kinda guy. I get a little jealous sharing my fella with others. Shucks. Perhaps there's somethin' wrong with me."

"I hear fleshbags are like that..." Skitch says, patting his friend's shoulder. "They get all possessive about their mates. It's good you're venting about this stuff. Not smart to hold it in. Bad for your health."

Vex scratches his head awkwardly. "Aw, geez. You think so? Maybe I oughta try and meet some of the guys Fiolo's into. Make it a three-man party, have some fun."

"Or four," Skitch says, flashing a thumbs up. "You know what they say. The more, the merrier."

"Yeah! That's true!" Vex chuckles. "I guess I'm just feeling a bit down 'cause I don't think I can keep up with him. What if he leaves me?"

"You hafta' open up and talk to your boyfriend," Skitch says, nodding sagely. "Only by expressing yourself freely can ya improve yourself. Nothing worse than secrets in a relationship."

"Yeah! You're right!" Vex says, rubbing his face in embarrassment. "Hey, Skitch, thanks for listening, man. Never thought I'd have such a good chat on a day like this. Really learning a lot about myself."

"Nobody's perfect, Vex. We all have bad days."

Both demons smile at each other warmly. Then, Skitch raises his head to the sky.

"Hm. By the way, Vex?"

"Yeah?"

"Ain't that the Monster Queen up there? What's she doing, flying down at us all super-fast for?"

Vex's smile vanishes. A look of worry replaces it.

"Uh... I _really_ hope she just wants to say _hi_."

A moment later, Blinker extends her palm. A blast of psionic energy crashes into the demon army, crushing ten thousand demons into meat paste.

Sadly, Vex meets his end, never getting to go home and reveal his true feelings to Fiolo. In the end, he perishes alongside his best friend.
