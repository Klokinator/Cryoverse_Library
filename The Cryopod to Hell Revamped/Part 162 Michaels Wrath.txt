Part 162: Michael's Wrath

Ivaldi continues pummelling Uzziel with ferocious, unending blows. Over and over, blasts of holy energy collide with the power of Nature, detonating explosively in the air and creating chaotic windstorms, tornadoes, and hurricanes throughout Heaven's battlefield.

"All soul manipulators must DIE!" Ivaldi bellows. "Not only the angels, but you disgusting, bastard Titanspawn as well!"

Uzziel's face remains impassive as she summons a wall of vines to block one of Ivaldi's punches. "I'm sorry you feel that way."

Hercules manipulates Uzziel from a distance. However, his thoughts cut in half as he forces himself to multitask.

_I cannot allow the Devil to flee from Heaven. If he escapes, he will go into hiding, and I may never find him again. Once he amasses more power, he will resurface and pose a significant threat to my rule. There is only one pawn who might stand a chance against the united strength of three Emperors. Luckily for me, the worthless wretch has yet to perish._

As Uzziel deflects Ivaldi's attacks one after the other, she twists her foot slightly and sends nature energy pulsing into Heaven's Soil. Several mana tendrils surge through the dirt and rush half a mile away to where one angel's body lays among half a dozen others.

Michael, the Archangel of Courage, rests in a puddle of blood. Thanks to several broken ribs, the Archangel's breathing comes in ragged gasps.

_No... strength... left in my body. Can't see anything. Darkness in all directions. Is this the end? Is this how I will perish? Defeated by... a single... bloodskin...?_

Having fallen to a fluke punch from Diablo, his former prisoner, Michael's pride crumbles to ash. He gazes at the sky, feeling nothing but disbelief at how his supposed 'unstoppable' power allowed him to fare against the Emperor of Annihilation.

Several thin, wispy vines stretch out of the ground. They curl around Michael, reaching into the chinks and gaps of his armor before resting against his skin. One vine wraps lightly around his throat, then glides up his cheek to touch his forehead.

As Michael barely grasps onto life at Death's Door, Hercules's voice materializes in his mind.

_Hahaha. Look at you now, Michael, a being everyone calls the 'mightiest Archangel'; a battle-fanatic whose bloodlust puts all of his family to shame. You lay here like a beaten dog, ready for death. Is this all your effort was worth? You wasted time toying with your food, only for it to choke you in the end. Disgusting._

Michael coughs weakly.

"Kuh..."

_Don't... patronize me... Hercules. I know why thou hath come... my power... thou dost plan... to heal me... and use it against the demons..._

_Of course,_ Hercules replies. _I'm glad you're quick on the uptake. Using Uzziel's power, I can heal you in seconds. However, I won't do so unless you pledge your loyalty to me._

Michael closes his eyes.

_I'll... never bow... to a soul-manipulating... Titan bastard._

_Oh? Never, you say? That's a strong statement. Let's put it to the test. You might be half-dead, but I'm sure, even now, you can sense the power flowing out of Uzziel. As a supposed traveler of the Virtuous path, you must have strong feelings for your family members. It would be a shame if I were to use Uzziel's power to give the demons a total victory today, wouldn't it?_

Michael shudders. _Hng. Do not... threaten me... thou dost desireth a victory. Thy hatred for the demons-_

_Is nothing at all,_ Hercules replies, finishing Michael's sentence for him. _Do you think I care who wins this war, anymore? I have only two goals, little birdie. The first is to defeat demonkind and establish an order of mighty warriors to keep peace across the world. However, if that should fail, my second goal is petty revenge. If I cannot rule everything, why should I allow those who scorned me to walk among the living? Better to let your kind perish in the eternal flames of judgment than live to mock me._

_Impossible. Thou woulds't never... allow the demons... to win..._

Hercules chortles. _Hah. Try me. I'm giving you an easy solution, Michael. Join me willingly, and I'll allow you to take revenge against those who left you for dead. Decline, and all of your brothers and sisters will suffocate under my thumb. I am not a cruel person. Once the demons perish, and once I establish my era of peace, the world will have room for the smiles and laughter of those you care about. Even those like... Catherine._

Michael's face twitches. _Catherine? Why bring her up? She is nothing to me._

_I wasn't born yesterday. Even now, in your final moments, I sense the feelings resting atop your mind. Images of your beloved sister float around like a log in the sea. You care deeply for her. Imagine how heartbroken she will be when she finds out her brother fell in a most humiliating manner — defeated in a single punch by a bloodskin! Oh, the horror._

Michael clenches his jaw. _Leave Catherine out of this._

_Hahaha. Do not believe for a moment that you may order me around, Archangel. You are putty in my hands. I need you now, yes, but I have another option on the table; one which will prove quite fatal for the others of your kind. You, however, have none._

Michael says nothing. His eyes flutter open, but still, he sees only infinite darkness.

Hercules' inner voice softens. _Worry not. I am a man of my word. Uzziel begged me to help, and so, I did. Your battle instincts have dulled. With my hand guiding you, you will become a weapon capable of cutting through any demon. You have grown soft ever since Raphael's mind-wipes, but when I am through with you, you will understand once again what it means to possess true, unfettered power._

Hercules' voice coalesces into a faint apparition in Michael's mind's eye. The Titan holds out a hand and waits.

_The clock is ticking, Michael. Your people need you. **Catherine** needs you. What is your answer?_

Michael swallows a chunk of hardened blood.

_I... curseth... thee..._

---

A few minutes later, the Archangel of Courage soars through the sky. With his body fully healed and his soul firmly in Hercules' control, he emits dangerous killing intent as Hercules uses him to pursue the three fleeing Demon Emperors.

_Liar._

Michael's soul trembles angrily.

_My sister did not joineth thou willingly. Like a coward, thou used thy soul manipulation most deceptively! Treacherous bastard!_

With his attention divided between Uzziel's battle against Ivaldi and Michael's ambush of the Demon Emperors, Hercules doesn't have time to trade words with the Archangel of Courage.

"Quiet. I'm busy."

_Thy words are poison. Thy goals are genocidal. I will not stand idly and let-_

"Didn't you hear me the first time? I know what will shut you up."

Hercules growls at the Archangel of Courage. After a moment, he adds a little extra force into his willpower and suppresses Michael's soul with all his strength. It takes several seconds, but soon, images of various violent acts begin appearing in Michael's mind. Scenes of angels being murdered and ripped apart by demons consume his thoughts, forcefully turning his attention away from Hercules' betrayal and toward the demons.

One moment, in particular, catches Michael's attention.

Michael's eyes widen. _What... what are these images?_

Hercules laughs. "Haha. Don't you know? My minions can share their vision. What you're seeing is a display of the demons ravishing your sister. They captured her not long ago. As you can see, they're doing whatever they please to her helpless form."

Michael's eyes tremble as a scene of horror plays out in his mind. Catherine screams as the demons hold her down. They rip off her armor and drool as lust-filled looks appear in their eyes.

_No... Catherine?! What are they doing to her?!_

"Oh, don't play dumb, Michael. The demons are doing what they do best. Now that they have captured Catherine, they'll torture her and perform all manner of vile acts, then kill her and eat what's left."

_No! NO!! Not Catherine! I have to stop them!!_

Hercules nods. An evil grin spreads across his face. "Aye. Don't disappoint me, Michael."

_Catherine! CATHERINE!!_

Rage explodes in Michael's mind. Little does the Archangel realize it, but the visions Hercules shared with him were fake, made to confuse him, and boil his blood.

Hercules aims Michael's eyes to the ground. There, about half a mile away, Satan and his two fellow Emperors tear through a line of undead cherubs. Several low-ranked demon Lords support them, but Diablo handles most of the killing while Satan aims for the cherub's heads.

"I'll rip him apart! I'll kill that bloodskin if it's the last thing I do!"

Michael flaps his wings. He shoots from a standstill position to over a hundred miles an hour as he divebombs toward Satan from above. Michael draws his sword and aims it at Satan's heart.

"SATAAAN!!"

Michael's screaming turns delirious. His eyes become bloodshot as they bulge in his face. Hercules' emotional nudges dial Michael's rage to the max as he imagines the demons doing unspeakably vile acts to his sister.

Satan hears Michael's warcry. He jerks his head to the sky and gasps. "Oh, shit!"

The Devil doesn't have time to react. He raises his arms at the last second, only for the hawk-like Archangel to smash him into a bloody pulp.

_BOOM!_

Michael crashes into Satan like a runaway tank hitting a sandcastle. 

Belial screams. "Ahhh!! Satan!!"

Diablo scowls. "Spawn of a broodmother. He sprayed the Devil all over the damn place."

Satan's bloody remains coat the ground in every direction for over twenty feet. However, Michael jumps to his feet, unhurt. He swivels his head toward Diablo and screams at the top of his lungs, "Where is she?!"

Diablo flinches as the Archangel charges him. "Who the hell-?!"

Unable to wrap his mind around the Archangel's rage, Diablo can only go on the defensive. He puts every bit of strength into his kinetic barrier, summoning it over and over again as the Archangel of Courage attacks him with a hundred sword strikes every second. Michael's arm becomes a thousand buzzsaws pressing against Diablo's kinetic barrier, and within a few seconds, he tears through and gashes Diablo's chest, arms, and neck to pieces.

"Gurk!"

Diablo gurgles in his throat as blood escapes from all of his pores. The moment before he dies, a bone-hammer smashes into Michael's back, blindsides the Archangel, and sends him careening to the side. Belial jumps over to Diablo and immediately heals him, pulling him back from the brink of death.

"Are you okay?!" She asks.

"Y-yeah..."

Diablo staggers to his feet as phantom pain wracks every inch of his body. Despite Belial fully healing him, the sensation of Michael's sword leaves a lasting impression, making Diablo doubt his eyes.

_I nearly died! Michael is way more vicious than before!_

Diablo and Belial take up defensive stances. By the time Michael jumps to his feet and flaps his wings to rush them, both Emperors are ready.

_Swish-swish, clang clang!_

Michael's sword furiously pounds Diablo's barrier, but Belial diverts many of his attacks to herself by using her superior speed and ability. She creates multiple bone-blades to counter his swordsmanship, which has grown far more ferocious than during their previous fights.

"Why is Michael so pissed?!" Belial asks. She jerks back in fright as she finally notices Michael's glowing eyes. "Ah! Hercules must be-"

"I know!" Diablo says, cutting her off. He tries to activate a three-times-multiplied kinetic punch, but Michael's flurry of attacks don't give him a moment of breathing room. "You saw what Hercules did to Satan! He makes his goons fight at their peak! We can't handle this Archangel alone!"

Satan speaks up from behind them. "Don't worry. You won't have to."

Having regenerated his body, Satan's bloodlust ignites as he and Hades begin planning to destroy the Archangel of Courage.

_Michael never displays this much emotion,_ Hades says. _I don't know what Hercules did, but he must have angered my brother like never before._

"I'll tell you what he did; he pissed in Mikey's tea, and now I'M pissed. Let's clobber him."

Satan motions with his hands. He summons thirty souls from deep within himself and burns them to ash. His mana capacity and magical power multiply by a factor of thirty, providing him a giant leap in his battle aptitude.

"Hey, Deebs! This one's for you, pal!"

Satan aims his hand at Diablo. A jet of water fires out and encircles Diablo. It transforms into a spiraling water barrier, one that protects Diablo from Michael's attacks. The Archangel strikes his swirling shield repeatedly, but the motion of the water deflects Michael's attacks to the right and left, preventing him from dealing substantial damage to Diablo.

Next, Satan aims another palm at Belial. Heaven shakes as dozens of pounds of soil jump up and wrap around her. The dirt and rock harden into a reinforced earthen armor, protecting her while affording her a decent degree of movement.

Michael screams in rage. His voice turns hoarse as he realizes what Satan is doing. "EVIL BLOODSKINS! I shall burn thee to ASH!"

Michael's glowing eyes double in luminosity. He fires a blast of holy energy at Satan, catching the Devil off-guard.

"What the-?!"

Satan jumps to the right. The laser sweeps through the spot where, an instant before, his neck stood. Satan dives to the ground and yelps as Michael not only continues following Satan with his eyes, but intensifies his attacks against Belial and Diablo. Several low-ranked demons perish as the beam cuts them in half. The others flee for their lives.

_Slash-slash-slash!_

Michael swipes his sword across Belial's earthen armor with such fury that it starts to disintegrate. With her movement slowed by the hardened mud on her limbs, Belial can't keep up with Michael's speed. She retreats while he pushes his advantage.

"Today, I will kill ALL of thy wretched kin!!"

Michael's sword shines as he imbues it with holy energy. A moment later, his sword starts rapidly sending waves of mana firing when he swings it. As Belial retreats, she gasps as fifty crescent-shaped magic blades shoot at her. 

"No!"

Belial tries to block them, but the energy waves tear through her bladed arms as if they don't exist. Belial's armor shatters, and deep gashes slice across her body, spraying blood in every direction. Both of her arms and her left leg sever from her body. She staggers back and flops to the ground as incredible pain tears through her entire frame.

"Guhuk..."

Michael's eyes continue firing holy energy at Satan. Diablo finally finishes building up enough kinetic energy to unleash a twenty-times-enhanced kinetic punch, and as Satan's barrier finally falls apart, Diablo swings his fist.

"Eat SHIT!"

Diablo aims for Michael's chest. Ten feet separates the two warriors, but Diablo doesn't try to close the distance. Instead, he punches the air, sending a blast of superheated wind from his fist. It pounds Michael like a hurricane and flings him off his feet. Finally, with his footing lost, Michael goes flying. He twists and spins through the air before crashing on his back over five hundred feet away.

Satan immediately comes to a stop as Michael's laser stops chasing after him. "Belial! No!"

Ignoring a possible counter-attack, Satan rushes over to Belial. She lays on the ground, covered in blood. Three of her limbs lay tossed around here and there, while blood pours from her stumps. Her eyes flick from left to right as her brain tries to cope with the pain.

"Hnn- nuhh..."

Satan splashes water on her wounds and uses Poseidon's power to heal her wounds and regenerate her missing limbs. "Stay with me, girl! Come on, baby! Don't die!"

Even as Belial's wounds close up, her mind fails to reconnect with reality. She writhes around on the ground and clutches her regenerating arm and legs. "Nnn... hurts... hurts..."

Satan nods. "I know it does, baby. Come on; we've gotta get out of here before Michael-"

"He's back!" Diablo yells. Satan jerks his head toward Diablo and scowls as the Archangel returns to whaling on the Emperor of Annihilation.

Michael screams wildly as he thrashes Diablo's failing kinetic shields. "I WON'T LET YOU TOUCH HER! SHE'S MINE!!"

Sweat drips from every pore in Diablo's body. He continually reinforces his kinetic shields as fast as he can, but they fare poorly against sustained rapid-fire attacks.

Satan bites his lip. _Michael is too fast. I can't keep up with him at all. If I slip up for even a second, he'll kill the other two!_

Suddenly, from behind Michael, the ground explodes. A gigantic Hellhound, fifty-percent bulkier than others of its kind, leaps out of a burrowed hole and pounces on Michael.

"FILTHY ARK-AN-JELL! NOBODY HURTSH MY MAN!"

Lucifer, the Emperor of Providence, catches Michael completely off-guard. Her wolven mouth, resembling a wood-chipper, snaps onto Michael's wings and bites with the strength of a crocodile.

_SKRRIIK!_

Lucifer tears several meaty chunks out of Michael's wings. Her claws rip across his back and legs, spraying blood in every direction.

"Aaaugh!!"

Michael shrieks in pain and jumps forward to try and shake the vicious monster ripping him to pieces, but he can't escape. Lucifer howls at him with her wolf-voice and pounces again.

However, before Lucifer can land the killing blow, Michael falls forward and sucks inside Heaven's Soil! The Hellhound Emperor lands on the grass and claws at it furiously as she tries to dig him out.

"COME BACK, FILTHY AN-JELL! COW-WERD!"

She rips the grass to shreds, but despite her best efforts, Michael escapes.

Ten seconds later, Michael bubbles out of Heaven's Soil a few hundred feet away. His body radiates power thanks to Uzziel's healing energy.

"That was a close one," Michael says, his rage gone. His voice turns monotone as he examines at his arms and legs. "You almost killed my second strongest pawn. It seems that when Michael stops toying with his food, he also loses sight of his surroundings. Imbecile."

Diablo and Lucifer raise their heads to glower at the Archangel. 

"Hercules..." Diablo mutters. "What's the matter? Is Michael too out of control for you?"

"The less effort I have to put into manipulating Michael, the more focus I can put on Uzziel," Hercules replies. "Splitting my mind between two Archangels is mentally draining. However, since I've temporarily immobilized Ivaldi, I can put the four of you down myself."

Satan helps Belial to her feet. The succubus shakily leans against her husband and shudders as Hercules' cold gaze falls on her. "L-love? What do we do?"

The Devil wraps one arm around Belial's waist and balls his free hand into a fist. "Easy. We kill the bastard. Nothin' to it."

Michael doesn't move. His glowing eyes evaluate the four Demon Emperors with the coldness of an eagle gazing upon a field hare.

_Even Michael has his limits._

Hercules sighs inwardly.

_I'll have to tap into his memories. Perhaps my newest pawn has a few tricks up his sleeve he's forgotten. I hope I don't regret this._

Having suffered immensely under Lucifer's ambush, Michael's soul slumbers restlessly as Hercules uses his strength to subdue the Archangel.

_It's a good thing I let the Demon Emperors weaken Michael's psyche, first. I'd be having one hell of a time trying to control him, otherwise. Uzziel is already a pain in my neck. She might seem sweet and innocent, but she lacks Michael's emotional weaknesses._

Hercules's main body takes a deep breath. He focuses his mind and probes deeply into Michael's memories.

...

Falling, falling, falling.

Hercules plunges into a magical black hole, one identical to Uzziel's, yet different in innumerable ways. As he falls, the power of Raphael's memory seal tugs and pulls at him from a million different directions. Hercules winces as he senses his soul ripping apart, but within seconds, he arrives at the center of the seal.

_Michael. What secrets are you hiding?_

Having experienced the memory bombardment once already, Hercules braces himself in advance. A moment later, the onslaught begins.

_Aaaaauuugh!!!_

Molten memories drip from the endless void and land on Hercules' brain. His main body shivers and shudders as he forces himself to endure the excruciating torment.

Half a minute later, Hercules exhales. His soul breaks through the barriers protecting Michael's mind, allowing him to access the Archangel's memories.

_Ancestors, protect me. Michael's recollections are just as numerous as Uzziel's... but the emotional trauma is a thousand times worse! Gods! How could Raphael allow this monster to live?!_

Hercules pants like a dog. 

_Uzziel slaughtered billions, but her actions were like those of a child crushing bugs. Michael's genocide was personal and based on blood-grudges! My god, perhaps Raphael had the right idea in trying to erase the memories of his fellow angels! If I had a choice, I wouldn't have let any of them live, but there's no way he could kill these monsters on his own!_

As Hercules searches Michael's memories, he finds not only the things he was looking for, but things he wasn't, as well.

_The Angels are all monsters. Demonkind, evil though they be, are nothing compared to the butchers and battle-maniacs comprising angelkind's forces. When I become the Infinity King, I will not allow a single one of them to survive._

_I must kill every last living and undead angel._

...

Satan, Belial, Diablo, and Lucifer all stand together and watch as Michael closes his eyes and trembles for several long, painful seconds.

"Hish guard ish down! KILL HIM!" Lucifer roars. She takes a step forward, but Diablo grabs her.

"No! Don't! It's a trick! Hercules would never give us such an easy opening. If we attack, we'll fall into his trap."

Lucifer growls at Diablo, but her animal mind pauses to consider his words.

"Tch... we will shee..."

Diablo returns his gaze to Michael. Despite his misgivings, the Emperor of Annihilation secretly ponders if his intuition is wrong.

_I might be thinking too highly of Hercules. If Michael is vulnerable, we have to take a chance and attack!_

Belial shakes off the last bit of dizziness wracking her brain. She turns to Satan and shoots him a questioning gaze. "Where's Nerissa's body?"

Satan smiles. "Somewhere safe. Don't worry. I have a few Warper souls in me. I stashed her where not even Valac can find her."

"Alright. Are you ready? We'll need all of our power to defeat Michael."

Satan smirks. "I ain't afraid of some winged moron. Mikey caught us off-guard a few times, but he ain't shit. What can one Archangel do against the four strongest demons?"

Michael opens his eyes. He speaks in Hercules' voice, and his words carry an ominous tone. "More than you can imagine."

Hercules flicks his gaze to Heaven's Soil. He sends a quick command to Uzziel, and a vine leaps from the ground and attaches to Michael's right arm's stump. Within seconds, it morphs and shifts until it becomes a pseudo-limb capable of emulating the strength and dexterity of a flesh and blood arm.

Hercules flexes Michael's newly created vine-based arm. After satisfying himself with its capability, he rapidly performs a series of hand-signs.

Diablo tenses up. "Watch out! Hercules is about to pull something!"

_FLASH!_

Michael's entire body explodes with brilliant light. Holy energy erupts from every inch of his skin, turning him into a dazzling star on the battlefield. The four demon Emperors all recoil in horror as the sudden burst of light destroys their retinas.

"Aaugh!!" Belial screams. She pulls away from Satan and falls to the ground. Despite pressing her hands to her eyes, Michael's holy energy bombards her skin and cooks her alive.

Satan and Diablo recoil in horror, unable to shield themselves from the radiant mana erupting from Michael's body. Satan turns his head away and swoons as his mind falls numb. "Shit! Shit! Fucking hell!!"

Only Lucifer remains standing. Thanks to her half-hellhound form, her monstrous body becomes resistant to the effects of Michael's holy energy. However, the bright light still makes her fifty Hellhound eyes flinch.

"I KNEW YOU'D TRY SHUMTHING! TIME TO DIE, MICHAEL!!"

Closing her eyes, the Hellhound charges Michael and pounces at him with the intent to kill.

Two massive wolf legs spread out their claws as Lucifer lunges for Michael's chest. Hercules doesn't flinch as the giant beast jumps at him.

"Too little, too late."

Michael grabs his sword and instantly dices Lucifer apart.

_Slash-slash-slash, SHIK!_

After cutting through Lucifer's massive front legs, Hercules uses Michael's sword to impale Lucifer through her animal heart. The Hellhound stops in midair and gasps.

"GAHK! H...how...?!"

With a shrug, Hercules flicks his sword down. He flings Lucifer's diced body to the ground, where she lands amidst her severed limbs in a rapidly spreading puddle of blood.

Diablo, still blinded, senses something amiss. He forces himself to look at Michael, only to gasp as he spots the image of Lucifer's limp corpse lying defeated in the grass.

"Lucy!! NOOOO!!"

Satan and Belial hear Diablo, but even while trying to heal their wounds, their eyes don't function well enough to let them see what happened. Diablo charges at Michael and raises his fist. 

"**YOU'RE DEAD MEAT!**"

Even with his eyes barely functioning, Diablo charges toward Michael like an enraged bull. The closer he gets, the more intense the dynamism of the holy energy radiating from the Archangel becomes. Soon, Diablo's movements turn sluggish. A sensation like molten quicksand engulfs him. The heat of the midday sun blasts against his face, chest, and arms. He leans forward and places one foot in front of the other, but his skin turns black as coal as all of his nerves continue to fry and fizzle.

Diablo's determination falters. 

_I can't... can't get any closer. My whole body is numb. I need to retreat. I'll die if I keep going..._

The Emperor of Annihilation battles with himself. Only twenty feet separates him from Michael, but his body refuses to listen to his commands.

_I can't give up. Lucy is dead! I have to avenge her. I have to kill Michael!!_

Diablo takes another step, causing the intensity of Michael's holy aura to increase further.

Hercules watches the Emperor slowly advancing toward him. For a moment, his cold demeanor falters.

_Hmph. Diablo and Lucifer. These two are mates, much like I was with Megara. In a way, I understand the emotions he's experiencing right now. Watching the woman I loved perish destroyed me. Diablo's heart must be in tatters._

Hercules sighs.

_Ah, well. Such is life. We lose those we love to those we hate. To be born is to suffer._

A glint appears in Michael's eyes. Hercules sends a command to the Archangel's body. A moment later, Michael rushes at Diablo.

_SLASH!_

Before Diablo can react, the Archangel's blade sweeps across his neck. Diablo's thoughts falter as the world spins around him.

_What? M-Michael... cut... me...?_

Like a rock landing in the mud, Diablo's head falls from his neck and slaps against the ground with a moist _splurt._ His headless body teeters for a moment, before falling over.

Michael's eyes sweep toward the two remaining Emperors.

"Well. It's time to kill the other two. Come, sister."

Hercules' mind travels to a new pawn, one he acquired only in the last several minutes.

Satan, still blinded by the Archangel's radiant energy, forces his eyes open. Through the narrowest crack possible, he peers into the blinding star standing fifty feet away until he can faintly make out the remains of Diablo's corpse.

"Sh-shit! Deebs? No... no!!"

Satan grinds his teeth together. He turns his attention inward, to the Archangel within his mind. "C'mon, Sammy-boy! Tell me we've got something that can kill this worthless..."

Samael remains impassive. _Thy demon body has its limits. Using magic will be nearly impossible with my brother's aura suppressing thee. If we're lucky, we might be able- ah! SATAN! Look out!_

A series of massive stomping noises rushes toward Satan. He jerks his head to the right, but with his terrible vision, he doesn't see anything coming.

"What's that noise?"

_Thump, thump, thump!_

Heavy footsteps, like trees crashing into the dirt, travel toward Satan.

A moment later, an invisible foot bigger than Satan's entire body falls from the sky.

**SPLURT!**

Satan perishes before he can react.

A Titan, much taller than Hercules, and nearly as tall as Zeus, crushes the Devil into pulp. After a moment, a shroud of invisibility wears off from the giant, revealing a Titaness with braided brown hair and glowing eyes. Her body stinks of the blood of a thousand dead demons.

The woman nods. "Haha. Oh, my beloved little brother. I cannot thank you enough for this opportunity."

Hercules' thoughts transfer into his newest minion's mind. _No, I must thank you, Chantis. Remember the plan. When Satan revives, crush him again and again. I will deal with the Succubus Emperor myself._

Chantis, a Titan female who once lost several of her sisters to Nerissa's ice blades, nods. "Nothing would please me more."

Belial reels in pain. She hears the sounds of the other three Emperors' last moments, but she does not witness their fall. Unlike the others, her control of healing magic only cripples her under Michael's holy aura. Every time she tries to heal herself, her magic backfires, causing more damage to her internal organs.

The succubus curls up in the fetal position and shivers uncontrollably as Michael slowly walks toward her. Each step he takes causes his aura to press against her more intensely than before. Soon, her spasming becomes uncontrollable. Belial's thoughts careen helplessly as she begs heaven and hell for a savior.

_Satan... Satan... Satan... save me... it hurts... hurts..._

Tears roll down Belial's face. She chews her tongue into pulp and gurgles blood as the holy aura kills her in the slowest, most agonizing way possible.

Hercules sighs. "You were a worthy opponent. I am neither heartless nor cruel. I will kill you now. A swift end is the best your kind can hope for."

Michael raises his sword. He aims it at Belial's neck, but before he swings the blade down, a strange thought enters the back of his mind.

"Hm. Strange. It smells like something is burning."

A faint odor reaches Michael's nose. Hercules puts his assassination of the succubus on hold to turn and gaze at Lucifer and Diablo's fallen bodies.

What he sees next shakes him to his core.

"What... what is... this demonic mana...?"

Diablo's beheaded body lies motionless in the dirt. However, blood-red energy begins dripping from his skin like juice through a sieve. As the liquid lands on the grass, Heaven's Soil loses its luster. The nature energy emanating from Uzziel turns inert, making the grass turn black as night.

The ghastly demonic mana sends a shiver up Michael's spine.

"My god... oh my god..."

Hercules watches in disbelief as Diablo's arm twitches. His fingers flex slightly and begin tip-tapping across the grass to reach for Lucifer's corpse.

"Lu... cy..."

Diablo's head, separated from his body, breathes out a single word. He stares at his wife, while an ungodly fury blooms in his eyes.

"Kill him... I have to kill him. Now!" Hercules yells. Michael lunges toward Diablo and swings his sword a thousand times. Instantly, he dices the Emperor of Annihilation into a pile of gore. Diablo's blood sprays across Heaven's Soil, and wherever it strikes, the grass fizzles and dies.

"Lu... cy..."

Diablo's voice speaks again. This time, it hangs in the air like a ghost on the wind.

Hercules turns pale.

"No... no... what is this? What's going on?!"

As Hercules focuses all of his attention on Diablo's blood-red mana, he stops paying attention to the minds of his other servants.

...

Hercules' main body trembles in terror. His teeth chatter, and his mind becomes disturbed as he senses the immense power building up where Diablo, the Emperor of Annihilation, fell.

"What do I do? What do I do?!"

Distracted by the condensing blood-red mana, Hercules fails to notice a shadow silently shimmying underneath rocks and behind trees toward his main body.

Berith, the Emperor of Backstabbing, peeks out from the boot of one of Hercules' guardian angels.

_Hehehe... Once I eliminate this thorn in his side, Master Valac will reward me greatly. Truly, his vision defies Fate itself._
