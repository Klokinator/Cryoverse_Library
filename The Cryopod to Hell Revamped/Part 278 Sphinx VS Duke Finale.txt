Part 278: Sphinx VS Duke Finale

**[Recommended Listening](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1M_QLC8-mc8)**

_Hero City's Eastern Front._

Zamiel, the Duke of Weaponry, howls like a madman. He summons a rain of blunted and bladed weapons, crashing them upon his adversary, the King of All Monsters, Kar. Still in his Sphinx form, Kar shrugs off Zamiel's attacks as if he were a metal-plated tank being pelted by snowballs. A two-ton hammer crashes into his head, only to richochet away and flop to the ground.

"Hurgh! You will have to do better than that!" Kar bellows.

The half-lion, half-eagle monster rears up on his hind legs. He swings one of his massive paws horizontally, summoning the weight of a planet upon Zamiel's figure.

_THUMP!_

Kar slaps Zamiel to the east, where he careens helplessly into the canyon filled with demons and Kar's summoned Earth Golems. Zamiel flies for half a mile before finally crashing into the leg of a Diamond Construct, knocking it off-balance until it tumbles to its side.

"Argh!" Zamiel cries, after finally grinding to a halt. His vision blurs for a moment before he forces himself to his feet. With so many of Kar's constructs slaying demons, plenty of blood lingers in the area, allowing Zamiel to draw the plasma toward himself. Within seconds, he heals his injuries and rises to his feet.

_Damn! My body might possess the strength of an Emperor, but my magic is still that of a Duke's! My summoned weapons can barely slow the Sphinx down, let alone allow me to kill the bastard! If only little bro were still here..._

Remembering the pain of watching his brother's corpse explode into a bloody mist, Zamiel's eyes turn red with emotion. He squeezes his hands into fists and punches his chest.

_Little bro! I won't let you die in vain! I WILL avenge you! No matter what it takes! No matter what price I have to pay!_

Zamiel raises his head. He spots the Sphinx leaping into the sky and flapping his wings, then dive-bombing the Duke of Weaponry.

"You want to kill me, reptile? Try! Just TRY!!"

Off the the west, the terrible screechings of a hydra continue to bellow into the surrounding world, but Zamiel pays them no attention.

_I'll bet Mephisto must have summoned that Hydra from the body of the previous one. Heh. If that's the case, then I have no reason to worry. I'll focus all my attention on killing the Monster King!_

Kar hurtles downward. He opens his mouth to bare his lion-teeth, aiming to bite off Zamiel's head. 

Just before Kar can land the killing blow, Zamiel flickers to the side, evading death. The Monster King's jaws snap onto his after-image, but this doesn't seem to bother Kar in the slightest.

"Hurgh! You demonsh are all the shame! You talk big, but run away when shomeone shtronger than you showsh up!"

With his mouth no longer in its usual form, Kar's words drawl a bit. Even so, the sheer timber of his voice proves menacing enough to freeze the blood of those facing him.

"I don't fear you..." Zamiel replies. "I'm merely suffering a little disadvantage. Once I find your weak spot, I'll end you in an instant!"

"You may try!" Kar roars.

A terrifying battle unfolds. For the next twenty minutes, Kar and Zamiel trade blows like twin thunderstorms competing over who can best devastate the land. Deafening sonic booms erupt whenever their fists and claws meet. 

Time after time, Kar smacks or eviscerates Zamiel, leaving him at death's door. However, with all of Kar's golems repeatedly killing demons in the valley, Zamiel manages to regenerate his body back to full.

By contrast, while Zamiel's tenacity proves immovable, Kar begins to show signs of slowing down. Despite the power afforded by his Sphinx form, its limited duration ticks silently in the back of his mind. Unlike his opponent, who can drain the blood on the battlefield to heal himself, Kar possesses no such means. Light injuries accumulate on his body, causing his blood to spew forth.

Unsurprisingly, Zamiel notices this fact. Every time Zamiel inflicts even the smallest cut on Kar's body, he forces the wound to bleed, then sucks out the blood to nourish and empower himself.

Twenty minutes become thirty. Finally, Kar pulls back, panting heavily.

"Hah... hah... you are quite the hardy cockroach."

Hatred glimmers in Zamiel's eyes. "Feeling sleepy, reptile? Don't worry. I'll soon put you to bed forever."

Zamiel summons another hundred hammers and swords. This time, instead of hurling them at Kar, he pauses for a split second before firing them outward.

_Boom! Boom! Boom!_

A series of explosions resound in the valley as Zamiel's weapons meet their marks. All of Kar's golems collapse under the sheer might of his duke-level attacks.

"Shit!" Kar curses. "You shneaky little..."

In an instant, the demon army moves as one. They rush the humans' frontlines once again, clearing the distance with frightening speed.

"Don't worry about them, Monster King," Zamiel hisses. "Those squishy little fleshbags are the least of your concerns."

He lunges forward while summoning another hailstorm of maces and spears. They fly at Kar, forcing him to defend himself from the two-pronged attack. This time, when the demons and humans collide at the western side of the valley, Kar proves unable to run to their aid.

So furious is Zamiel's onslaught that Kar quickly finds himself in a passive position, forced back one step after another. The Sphinx pants wildly, drawing forth all his strength to forcefully maintain his Sphinx transformation.

Eventually, during a momentary lull in the battle, Kar leaps backward, only to trip over a rock and land on his side. Immediately, he loses his battle momentum, causing his body to detransform. Within seconds, he morphs back into his crocodilian form, sapping him of the enormous strength he possessed as a Sphinx, yet also returning him to a much more sustainable power level.

"Hurgh... I underestimated you, Ugly," Kar says as he jumps to his feet. His breath no longer comes in ragged gasps. Unlike before, where maintaining his Sphinx form could be likened to a mountain climber desperately trying to clutch a rock with the tips of their fingers, Kar now finds his feet back on solid ground. Despite possessing somewhat weaker strength, the drop in mana requirements alone prove capable of reinvigorating him.

"Cocky as ever, Sphinx..." Zamiel murmurs. "Your words seem to imply that if you'd just fought me more seriously, you'd have killed me by now. Don't lie to yourself. We both know I'm far too much demon for you to handle."

Kar nods slowly. "We shall see."

The Monster King suddenly launches forward, tearing across the plains to clear the few-hundred-feet gap separating him from his most hated enemy. Zamiel's heart skips a beat as Kar charges at him.

_Damned croc! I thought he was sapped dry, yet somehow, he gained a second wind!_

Another vicious melee ensues as Kar rushes forward, using his far less bulky crocodilian body to bob and weave, ducking and deflecting Zamiel's ethereal weapons each time he summons and flings them. With their weight ranging from half a ton to five tons, each one sends rocks and mud spraying whenever they strike the ground, yet not once does Zamiel manage to land a blow.

As their battle picks up in intensity, Zamiel starts retreating two and three steps at a time, furiously blocking Kar's claw and tail swipes. Like an enlightened martial artist, the crocodile continuously strikes at Zamiel's weak points, sometimes knocking Zamiel backward, while other times forcing him to evade.

_Damn!_ Zamiel thinks. _The croc might be weaker when he isn't in his Sphinx form, but he's also nimbler and still hits like Bael's big brother! What the HELL?! How can the universe be so unfair as to give him all that power?!_

Zamiel tries to telekinetically yank a patch of nearby blood off the ground, but during that moment of inattention, Kar rushes over and violently slaps his face, sending him crashing into a boulder.

"Hurgh! Ah-ah-ah! I don't think so, punk! You aren't healing anymore. Not with me around."

Hearing his opponent's declaration, Zamiel's thoughts turn grim.

_I can't keep this up! The Sphinx is deliberately limiting his strength. He's not trying to kill me anymore, but to beat and break my bones. He wants to cripple me so he can drag me somewhere without any blood. If he succeeds, I'll be a goner! I have to find a way to beat him while I still can!_

Gritting his teeth, Zamiel jumps to his feet and starts focusing on defense. He retreats toward the main demon army, hoping to stumble across blood-soaked patches of the battlefield. However, to his horror, no matter where he looks, he doesn't find even a single corpse.

"Looking for something?" Kar asks, grinning viciously. "Oops. I must have accidentally sucked all those juicy, bloody bodies into the ground. Shame you don't have the powers of earth manipulation, eh?"

Zamiel pales in fear.

"You bastard! Do you think you can fuck with me?! I'm the strongest Duke in all of Hell!"

"But in the end... you are _only_ a Duke..." Kar chuckles. He jumps into the air and dive-bombs toward Zamiel, intending to land a grievous blow.

Zamiel launches another barrage of weapons at the falling Monster King. However, even as he does, the sensation of death wraps itself around his heart.

_I won't win. I can't win..._ Zamiel thinks.

_The Monster King is too strong. No matter how many weapons I hit him with he never goes down._

_They're all too weak to pierce his hide. Even if they could hurt him, he'd just dodge them._

_If only I could land one critical hit..._

As the crocodile descends upon him, a flash of insight sparks in the Duke's eyes.

_What am I thinking? How could I be so stupid? Of course! All this time, I've focused on quantity, when I should have poured my magic into quality!_

Seeing Kar on a direct collision course, Zamiel stomps his foot against the dirt and dives to the side, causing the crocodile to smash into his previous position. Zamiel dives into a roll, then leaps to his feet.

"Tch. I'm sorry, little bro. I almost gave up. But don't you worry... this reptile won't live ten minutes longer!"

Once again, Zamiel taps into his magic. He conjures another summoning portal, but this time, instead of summoning a hundred random weapons, he pours all of his magic into one single, unbelievably powerful greatsword.

Harnessing a magical energy equal to half of Zamiel's mana reserves, the weapon pulsates with rage, fueled by his emotions. The menacing curved blade, like a dragon's tooth, seems to demand energy-rich blood. Zamiel jumps into the air and wraps his fingers around the sword's hilt, then drops down and plants his feet on Tarus II's soil. His entire demeanor shifts, changing him from a fleeing red rat into an unfathomable expert.

Zamiel inhales deeply, allowing the smell of death permeating the valley to flow into his nostrils. His pupils enlarge as he gazes at his opponent without a trace of emotion on his face.

"...I see. To think I've been chucking swords and clubs at the Monster King like rocks this whole time. Truly, my approach to combat has been nothing short of amateur. That changes now."

Magical energy wraps around the weapon's blade. It carries a sensation of bloodlust, causing any who look at the sword to feel a sensation of despair. Kar takes a step back and examines his opponent's changed posture warily. As a long-lived predator of the swamp, he has faced countless vicious enemies. 

"Hurgh. Have you been holding back the whole time, Ugly? Come, then. Show me your 'true' strength."

Zamiel nods. 

"Gladly."

The Duke of Weaponry slowly slashes his sword upward, aiming to chop the Monster King in half. However, with more than fifty feet separating them, his sword seemingly won't hit Kar at all.

The instant his weapon starts to curve upward, the malevolent mana enveloping its blade transforms into a wave of ultra-sharp Sword Qi. It lashes forward, flying toward Kar at the speed of sound. In that split-second, Kar dashes to the side, his eyes following the Sword Qi as it rockets forward.

_Slash-slash-slash!_

The blade's energy chops through ten boulders and half a dozen demons in its path, instantly severing all of them in half. It continues flying until it plunges into a cliff at the north, where it finally sinks into the mountainside and loses its momentum.

Kar's eyes widen in alarm. "What the-?!"

Without hesitation, Zamiel lunges forward. His movements, no longer as slow as during his first slash, speed up tremendously. He fires off wave after wave of Sword Qi, forcing Kar to dodge, lest that deadly energy behead him in a single move.

"Hurgh!! You... when did you learn such a terrifying attack?!" Kar howls.

Zamiel executes a horizontal slash. Kar jumps to avoid it, but the blade still flies out in a huge arc and slaughters several hundred demons, chopping them in half at their waists. Some of them cry out in agony, while the rest perish in the blink of an eye, never even seeing their death coming.

Kar grits his teeth. "You are much more dangerous than I thought!"

Seeing Zamiel killing his fellow demons without a shred of conscience, Kar explodes in anger. Even if he couldn't give half a damn about their fates, seeing them killed by friendly fire stirs up his rage toward the dishonorable.

"You complete lowlife! How dare you throw away the lives of comrades who depend on you?!"

Kar charges toward Zamiel, clearing the gap at lightning speeds. He evades one last slash from Zamiel's sword, then narrows his claws into a spear of bone.

_SKRIIIIK!_

Kar plunges his claws into Zamiel's stomach and tears through his back. With a single move, he impales his entire arm through the Duke of Weaponry's body, spewing a gallon of blood out behind him.

"Ah! Puhak!" 

Zamiel cries out in pain. However, a vicious grim spreads across his lips.

_Got you._

With Kar's arm plunged deep within Zamiel's guts, the Monster King finds himself within a danger zone, one he didn't expect. Zamiel's sword, still infused with Sword Qi, flits around behind the Monster King and stabs into his back!

_RIIIP!_

Not expecting his badly injured opponent to use such a move, Kar gasps in pain. he quickly pulls his arm out of Zamiel's guts, allowing the Duke to stagger backward and flop to the ground. However, Zamiel's sword, possessing an animal-like sentinence, continues to pour razor sharp Qi into Kar's back. The Monster King tries to cry out in pain, only for the sword's bloodthirsty mana to eviscerate his lungs, making it impossible to breathe.

"Shuh! Shuhhh... kuhhh..."

Kar wheezes and gasps for breath. He grabs the front of the blade and tries to forcibly reverse it out of his body. Instead, the Sword Qi rips apart his hands like a garbage disposal, causing him even more horrifying wounds. With his claws mangled, Kar's eyes widen in horror. He staggers backward and tries to think of a way to pull the sword out, only for it to retract of its own volition. It retreats from his back, allowing Kar to flop to the ground, wheezing and coughing up blood while his useless lungs fail to do their job.

On the other hand, with Kar's blood soaking the nearby terrain, Zamiel quickly heals himself back up, then jumps to his feet, a look of glee playing upon his face.

"Hahaha! HAHAHAHAH!! I can't believe it! Oh, what luck."

Zamiel's smile disappears, replaced instead with the look of a bloodthirsty, homicidal maniac. 

"Monster King. I've been waiting for this moment all day."

The older Battle Brother slowly walks toward Kar, who barely manages to push himself away with his feet. Kar repeatedly vomits blood, while even more spills from his ruptured internal organs out of his back and stomach.

"Kuuk... krrrnn..."

Zamiel holds out his hand. His sword flies into his grasp, purring like a contented kitten. The Duke strokes its blade while smiling sweetly. "Yes. Good boy. You did well. Did you get a good taste of the scaley's blood? Mmm. Me too. But what say we have some more? There's plenty for both of us."

Without warning, Zamiel stabs the sword down, impaling Kar's knee. The Monster King opens his mouth to scream, but all he manages is to gasp out dozens of air-filled blood bubbles.

Zamiel greedily levitates Kar's gushing blood. He splashes some of it on his sword, enriching its spiritual energy. The rest, he pours into his mouth, smacking his lips while savoring the mana-rich iron-like texture.

"Mmm. Nothing like a few gallons of blood to wake me up in the morning. I think I'll have another!"

With that, he impales Kar's other knee, causing the Monster King to silently shriek in pain.

Over and over, Zamiel impales Kar's mangled claws, his elbows, and his shoulders. He takes his sweet time tearing the Sphinx apart, grinning sadistically the whole time.

"You should have accepted my offer from the beginning, Sphinx. If you had just turned against the humans, my little bro would still be alive, and you'd be secure in your power. Sadly, your arrogance blinded you. You never thought you could lose to a pair of Barons. That was your undoing."

Kar lays motionless in the grass, his body mangled and incapable of moving, his chest heaving, and his eyes glazed over.

"Well, dear Sphinx. I think you know what comes next," Zamiel says. "No more fun and games. I want your Sphinx powers for myself. What say you, hm? Shall we do this the easy way or the hard way?"

Zamiel pauses for a moment to smile. As he does, Kar lifts his head.

"Ssss... **BUTTERFLYYYYY!!**"

Kar bellows at the top of his lungs, startling the Duke of Weaponry.

"What the devil?! Your lungs healed already? Damned reptiles and your hardy- oh, fuck."

Zamiel only gets a split-second to react before a boulder-sized fist made of psionic energy materializes beside him and smashes into his body.

_Whump!_

The Duke goes flying. Blinker's summoned fist blasts him more than two miles into the distance, causing him to careen uncontrollably until he crashes into the planet's crust.

An instant later, Blinker materializes at Kar's side, her body still human sized.

"No, no, gator-man! You had that guy on the ropes! What happened? Are you okay?! Oh my god, you look horrible! Don't worry, I'll, uh, I'll get you to Belial! Just stay strong, okay!"

Blinker panics. In all the time she's been with Kar, not once has he ever suffered such horrific, gruesome injuries.

"Cough... Butterfly... you came..." Kar gasps.

"Duh! Now, stop talking. I'm gonna teleport you to Belial, okay? Lemme just lift you up-"

Blinker starts to levitate Kar's body with psionic energy, only for his figure to go limp. The crocodile starts shaking uncontrollably, forcing Blinker to set him back down.

"Kar? Kar?! Wh-wh-what's wrong? Oh my god, did I hurt you? I'm sorry!"

It takes several seconds before Kar manages to reopen his eyes.

"D-don't... Butter... fly... hurgh. Can't move. Barely... barely holding on..."

Blinker starts to say something, only to jump in alarm as a hail of ghastly specters falls upon her. Not content to let the fairy get away, Mephisto summons another rain of ghosts to attack her, but Blinker quickly conjures a barrier of psionic energy to keep them away.

"Shit! Shit! Okay, Kar? I need you to stay calm, alright? Stay calm, like me! I'm always calm, right? Right!"

Blinker nods several times, convincing nobody of the veracity of her statements. Not even herself.

"I can't heal you, Kar! I don't know healing magic. Only Belial does! Wait, don't you have gator healing juices? You can fix yourself, right? Do you need mana? I've got plenty of mana! I can- eek!"

Blinker shrieks in fright as Zamiel suddenly appears and starts whaling on her barrier with his sword.

_WHAM! WHAM! WHAM!_

"Open up, fairy! Give me that Sphinx! He's mine!!"

With ghosts attacking her psionic barrier on one side and Zamiel on the other, Blinker finds herself forced to send more mana upward, reinforcing her psionic shield further.

Unsure how to get Kar out of such a vicious encirclement, Blinker's head spins. "Uh, uh, don't worry, gator-man! I'll save us both! I'm strong you know! Almost as strong as you! Just gimme a second and-"

Before Blinker can finish, Kar coughs.

"Butterfly. No. There is... no saving me now. Out of mana. Too injured. Won't... won't last long."

Kar's eyes flutter.

"Butter... butterfly... listen to me. One... one question."

Blinker's heart stops. She bites her lip to try and quell her trembling.

"Y-yes? What is it, Kar? A question?"

Slowly, Kar nods.

"Who... who do... I love... the most?"

Uncomprehending, Blinker winces as Zamiel's furious assault intensifies, sending pangs of pain into her chest.

"I don't understand, Kar. What do you mean? Why are you asking me this?"

Kar grits his teeth. "Cough... damn you... woman... cough. Just answer... my question..."

Blinker lowers her head. "I... okay. Who do you love the most? It's obvious, right? Pyra. You love her the most."

Kar lolls his head to the side. "No... wrong... stupid woman... hurgh... answer again..."

Tears stream down Blinker's cheeks.

"_Me?_ Is it me?"

Finally, Kar's expression loosens a little, making him lose some of the pain in his eyes.

"Hurgh. Yes... that's right. It is you, Butterfly. You are... the one... I love most..."

Kar coughs one last time.

"Kuu... I always was... bad at... _riddles_."

"Kar?" Blinker whispers.

A moment later, Kar exhales one last time.

His body sags, losing the last of its strength.

As the light fades from his eyes, a faint smile creeps upon Kar's face.

In the end... he finally achieves the answer he sought his whole life.

"Kar! Kar, no! NOOO!"

Blinker wraps her arms around Kar's head. Even as the smashing of fists and weapons strikes her psionic barrier, all she comprehends is her husband's cold, lifeless body.

Unseen by those of the mortal coil, a congregation of spiritual energy levitates out of Kar's body. It hovers in the air, then dives down into Blinker's sobbing figure.

Shortly after it does, Blinker raises her head. 

A look of immense rage plays across her face.

"You... you killed... my HUSBAND!"
