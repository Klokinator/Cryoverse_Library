Part 100: A Beautiful Day

A cool breeze swept across the plains, as the sun shone down upon the soft, rolling meadow. Trees with flowers were blossoming, fruits were ripe, and birds chirped sweetly to one another.

This is _hell_.

The two armies faced off on opposite sides of the field. Humanity was led by the Slayer, a man of incredible powers who had united all the nearby populations together to battle the menace to their future.

Across from them lay a great army of demons of various shapes and sizes, along with other uncommon and rare specimens, such as werewolves and whitewalkers, along with orcs and goblins. They were led by Mephisto, the Duke of Mist. He was a creature of intangible characteristics, his body being mostly transparent and ghostly.

Mephisto smirked at the army in front of him. "Nothing you do will ever matter. You have no way to defeat us. If you should surrender now, I will promise you a swift, painless death."

"And join you as undead soldiers in your army of evil? Never!!" The man screamed a mighty battle cry, and his army charged forwards. Mephisto did the same, leading his minions into the fray as they licked their lips eagerly.

...................................................................

I screamed in rage, as one of my soldiers, Darius, was cut down by an orc right in front of me. "You BASTARD!", raising my personally forged mighty battle axe up, I swung it down with incredible power, cleaving the orc cleanly in two. The other demons and monsters nearby took a step back in fright as they witnessed my inhuman power. _Good._

Raising my axe again, I screamed unintelligible words of rage as I charged through their lines, slicing anything down that got in my way, shoving my men out of my way with brute strength as my gargantuan body plowed through foes towards my ultimate goal: Mephisto.

He stood with two orc guardians at his side, both of them massive monsters equal to my size. When they saw me charging, they squealed in fright and jumped out of the way. Mephisto smiled as I charged. "Come at me, 'Great' Leader. I'll test what you're made of."

He never even saw my attack coming. I swung my axe down suddenly and it passed right through his body as if it didn't exist. I grinned. "Now I've cut you down to size."

Then I gasped. He was perfectly fine. I had literally passed right through him. He appeared to have an incorporeal form.

He grinned even wider as he saw my confusion. "A good effort, but you'll have to do better than that."

Rushing the few feet that separated us, he closed the distance in a millisecond, shoving his palm forwards, and solidifying it the instant it hit me. In fact, he even made it pass through my arms I had put up to protect my face, impacting me square in the jaw.

I went flying, careening backwards, my massive body smashing down friend and foe. Anyone in the path of my flight was knocked over or crushed to death instantly.

I gasped as pain overtook my face. I don't need to see anything so my eyes are a moot point, but my jaw hurt like hell. This transparent ghost demon is no slouch.

" _Heal!_ " I spit the word out, the agony in my jaw almost making it impossible to speak. That was a close one.

I quickly healed my face. I didn't notice the slightest drop in my spiritual energy. At least there's that. But this guy...

I aimed my hand at him. " _Die!_ "

Nothing happened, of course. It wasn't working on any of the demons anymore, not even the lesser ones. I always tried it though, just to be sure. You never know when a proud demon might ignore the countermeasures and offer me a freebie on a silver platter.

Mephisto cackled with delight. "That's it?! Try again!"

I grimaced as I formulated a plan. If he wants to play this game, let's do it. I'll play ball. I aimed my hand at him again. " _Corporeal!_ "

Suddenly his body came clearly into view. He seemed confused for a second, then he realized what had happened. As I suspected, he didn't protect against that complicated word from my native language. "Smart move human, but what if I do this?"

With a quick movement of his hands, he became immaterial again, then he did another movement after that one, and nothing seemed to happen. I yelled again.

" _Corporeal!_ " And I quickly charged before he could cast his spell again, but- ah! He had protected himself from my word! He was still transparent, trying to come to a quick halt as I realized my mistake, I stumbled for a single second as he flew at me, passed through my body, appeared on the other side, and punched me in the back, sending me smashing face first onto the ground. He used a lot more force this time compared to the last.

I wheezed as the wind was knocked out of me. I tried to heal myself but I couldn't get the word out.

"Having trouble speaking, you scant little rodent? It's hard to use your spells if you can't speak, isn't it?" He cackled with delight once more.

These demons... over the last 5 years, they've gotten so much more powerful, or perhaps they are simply becoming more used to my manner of fighting. Now they're even targeting my mouth, throat, and jaw. But, I always have a trump card waiting, just in case that happens.

I gasped as I jumped to my feet. I quickly cast another spell. This was one I hadn't used before, at least not in front of the enemy.

" _Slow!_ " Time crawled to a halt around me, as that familiar feeling of deja vu overtook me. This is just like what happened the first time Hoarhiim and I... no... best not to think about that.

Time was slowed, and my mental speed was still incredibly fast, but my body was slowed significantly as well. Time to fix that.

" **SSSSSPPPPEEEEEDDDDD!** "

My mouth slowly formed the words as I put a ton of energy into it, directing the spell at myself.

Suddenly, my body moved normally. I quickly glanced all around me. Mephisto's eyes very slowly and poorly tracked onto my position. I've got lots of time now. I raced over to one of my men who was a second away from being brained by a particularly big orc with a spiked club. I decapitated the orc in one smooth motion and kicked the club out of the way as it fell, making it smash on the ground beside the man. He probably had no idea what happened, the lucky bastard.

I quickly and efficiently decapitated several other monsters in the area, as I felt my energy noticeably starting to decrease. The 'slow' spell is a cost over time ability, and moving around while time is slowed causes my energy to drop rapidly. I'll have to wrap this up quickly.

I quickly ran up behind Mephisto, who was slowly turning to try and follow my movement from earlier. " _Flesh!_ "

The spell failed. Damn. " _Material! Physical!_ " Neither of those worked either. This son of a- wait! One more word, he probably doesn't know this one!

" _Tangibility!_ "

He slowly became flesh and blood in front of me, as he tried to track onto the position he heard my words coming from. I grinned evilly. _You're finished_.

In the blink of an eye, I decapitated him, chopping up other parts of his body into bits, and finally smashed the rest of the pieces down with the flat of my massive axe, literally crushing him into pulp before he could respond.

My energy is running low.

I quickly jumped up to a vantage point on a nearby rock, smashing a skeleton archer out of the way with a single blow. Poor boney bastard, he's lucky he can't feel pain.

Aiming my mind simultaneously at every one of my troops on the battle field I screamed at the top of my lungs.

**"RECALL!!!"**

Instantly, all my men vanished and returned to the designated point I had set before the battle. The enemies still slowly swung down at opponents that were no longer there. I grinned as my breath came in ragged gasps. Time is of the essence now. I'm down to critical levels of energy. I spared a quick glance back at the clone I had created before the battle. Good, he's in position. He'd goddamn well better be. They seem to be getting more and more independent these days.

I raised both hands and aimed them in the sky, as I formed a mental image of the biggest most solid and dense object I possibly could.

**"METEORRRRR!"**

From the heavens, the meteor that I had seen in one of my dreams appeared, but far bigger than even I imagined. As the last bit of energy drained from my body, I gasped two words when I saw the size of the object I had summoned.

"Oh... fuck..."

Darkness took me.
