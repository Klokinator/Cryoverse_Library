Part 072: Battle Bowl

What the hell am I watching. I can't follow this with my eyes. I can barely follow it at all.

Beelzebub was the first to move. At a speed so fast I almost missed it, he appeared behind the traveler, he stabbed his hand forwards into the traveler's heart.

The traveler instantly died. In fact, he didn't just die, he faded _into dust_.

"Hm?" Beelzebub didn't look happy that he'd won, he looked perplexed, and his eyes started darting around. "Where did you run off to?"

And suddenly, the traveler, no, the _hero_ was behind him. With a single slap, he smashed Beelzebub into the wall. "GAH!" Beelzebub yelped as the attack caught him by surprise, but he stood back up a second later. Despite the impact damage to the earthen wall, Beelzebub somehow seemed unharmed.

Beelzebub stood back up and stared warily at the hero again. "Filthy insect. I knew you wouldn't fight me fair and square. Always resorting to simple tricks and deceit." He glanced down at his pants, which were now slightly tattered and very dirty. "Ah... this fills me with such.... anger!"

He lunged again at the hero, and punched the hero right in the chest, or at least he tried to. The hero deflected it with a single hand, the other one reaching around and slapping Beelzebub across the face once more. Curiously, I noted amongst the speed of the battle that the hero never once looked at Beelzebub. He always had his head aimed at the ground. Was he perhaps... blind?

Beelzebub snapped when the second slap hit him. "You insolent little-!" Jumping backwards he unhinged his mouth and I jerked back involuntarily as he suddenly breathed a massive wall of fire. It hadn't reached me yet as I was a hundred feet away, but it blasted down the corridor and would be here in a second.

The Hero reached his hand out. " **Absorb!** "

The fire instantly disappeared. It seemed to suck inside his hand instead of passing all around him. Beelzebub took a step back. "You've changed. I should have realized when my pieces went missing but... I didn't think you had grown to this extent."

Hero nodded. "And you haven't changed. Always underestimating humans. I've heard about what you've done. It all ends today."

Then, something unexpected happened. To be honest, everything here was unexpected, but what happened next blew my mind.

The hero simply said " **Invisibility!** "... and he vanished.

"Clever." Beelzebub said simply.

Moving his hands quickly, he cast a spell, what the effect was, I'm not sure. "Unfortunately, as a filthy human, your blood runs hot. You can't hide from a predator's vision."

"Let's change that." A voice came out of thin air. " **Reptile**."

Suddenly Beelzebub was actually visibly confused. "What? You can make your blood cool?! How is that possible?!"

"It really is unfair, isn't it, _Beelzy_?" Said a voice from somewhere in the room. Beelzebub jumped and swung at its source wildly, but only hit the wall. "Always thinking you're so smart and above it all. Your pawns fell, just as you will. You think you're a predator, but I am the real predator here."

Beelzebub reeled as something smashed into his back, then as he flew forward, just before he hit the wall, another impact smashed into his face, sending him flying backwards. But this happened 4 more times, in the space of 4 seconds, he was smashed back and forth by the invisible Hero, who could not be seen or heard as he continued his relentless assault.

"You simply don't understand. I haven't even started getting serious yet. You're finished." Hero spoke again, this time his voice seeming to come from within the walls themselves. I was wrong, this man was the real monster here.

Beelzebub staggered to his knees as he coughed up blood and spit some teeth on the ground. His face, no longer in perfect condition, was hideously beaten and torn up. "Hah... how d-did you?!"

Beelzebub paused as a storm of thoughts crossed over his face. "Ah... now I remember... three archangels..."

"Wrong!" said a voice cheerfully from a nearby invisible spot. "I've had the ability since I first appeared in this hell. Hoarhiim and the archangels only increased my available magic output. But over the last year... I've gained a lot more power than that. I'm probably equal to, oh, say, a Duke class demon? Probably higher to be honest!"

Beelzebub seemed stunned. "Im...possible... you haven't even been here two years... yet... so fast...?"

"Yeah. I really am a walking cheat code." He chuckled to himself at some joke. I didn't know what a cheat code was, but it must have been a fearsome monster.

"By the way, Beelzy, old buddy old pal... you do realize that this was over from the start, right? I only turned invisible to play around a little." Pausing for a second, I suddenly heard his voice right in front of me. " **Visibility.** "

He appeared in front of me, his body now covered in lizard-like scales. Hero yawned and stretched a little. "Well... it was a good run. But unfortunately, you were outmatched from the start. Even if you were incredibly powerful... it would be too easy. I'm sorry, friend."

Beelzebub grimaced as he staggered back to his feet and pointed at the Hero's chest. "This... isn't over. I will return, and when I do-"

The hero raised his hand and his grin disappeared as he stared coldly at the demon. "No, Beelz. You won't return. I already sealed your teleportation before you got here....Farewell." Hero's voice was icy cold.

Then he said a single word, as his hand aimed at Beelzebub.

" **Die.** "
