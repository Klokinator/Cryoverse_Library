Part 092 - Chapter 1 End

As I returned to my body, I gazed around, looking for Hero. "Wow! Great job! You must have really destroyed him! I can't even see a trace of his body anywhere!"

Blaarjiim was unusually silent. Usually he'd be saying something like _It was but a trifle, my queen_, or _I need no praise from you, only to stay with you forever_. He's usually so romantic like that!

But... he's silent right now.

I looked around and spotted Beelzy standing all alone. How... odd.

"Oh Beeelzzzyyyy!!" I dashed over to him and waved in his face, but he was mute. Oh right, that guy, he's just a puppet now. Sigh.

There was no sign of Hero anywhere. I even used search magic, but of all the places I had traveled on the planet, he was in none of them.

" **My... queen...** "

Finally, Blaarjiim spoke up.

"Oh Blaarjiim, wow you must have done a heck of a job here! I can't find him anywhere!" I was giddy with excitement. Now me and Blaarjiim would be able to hang out and play in the grass and have all kinds of fun times! It kinda sucks we can't re-enter the labyrinth but it's fine, I love my minion to death!

" **My lady... something happened, while I was in control...** "

Blaarjiim sounded... sad? I've never heard him sound sad before. My smile disappeared as I looked at his orb with concern.

"Blaarjiim... are you okay? What happened while I was under?"

A long, uncomfortable pause. I didn't like this. I don't like it one bit.

" **Hoarhiim appeared... during the final battle... and he won decisively.** "

I frowned. "He won? But, we're still here. Wouldn't a decisive win mean that he killed us?"

" **No my lady... he has escaped into the labyrinth along with the boy. We are trapped here.** "

I stared at him silently. We're trapped? That's unfortunate. Maybe we can return to the labyrinth somehow. Magic is powerful. "Come now, don't be too hard on yourself. You guys have always stalemated when you fought. Even if he's back in the labyrinth and you destroyed the door to get back..." I gritted my teeth. This imbecile. What was he thinking, doing that without my permission? There's no more snacks here!

"Well, even if that is the case, don't worry about it!" I smiled cheerfully. Blaarjiim seemed to have taken this really hard. "We'll escape here eventually! Nothing can stop me if I put my mind to it!"

I sat down on the ground and kicked my feet up, and stared up at the sun. It was a beautiful white color. Strange though, I could have sworn this was a yellow sun before.

" **My queen... we won't be returning to the labyrinth. We are in our last minutes together, if we even have that much time left. Hoarhiim did something incredible before he left... and we're going to die.** "

I stopped smiling and stared at Blaarjiim. He wasn't joking. Whatever happened, it must have been horrible. "Blaarjiim, what happened? Just tell me already."

He paused again, as a mental image of him wincing entered my mind. " **He used his power to create what he called a 'supernova'. It caused the sun to explode. We only had 4 minutes left before the blast hit us when he told me this... it has been three now.** "

I quickly looked back up at the sun. Oh my god... it's even whiter than it was a minute ago, and noticeably dimmer.

"You're... not joking... are you?" I gaped at the sun that was dimming more and more every second. It was also getting smaller at a frightening speed.

" **My queen... Amelia. Before we die. I wanted to say something that has been on my mind for thousands of years.** "

I looked back into the pitch black orb. Blaarjiim's eyes mentally gazed into mine.

"You don't have to say it..." I whispered as tears started to cloud my vision. I could feel my chest tightening and breathing sharpen. "I... love you too, Blaarjiim."

In the blink of an eye, everything went white.
