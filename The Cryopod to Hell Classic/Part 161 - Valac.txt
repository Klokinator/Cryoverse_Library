Part 161 - Valac

As Lucifer spoke with Valac, a mental image of his old friend's face began to form. He could at first see only a vague outline, but as the minutes crawled by and Valac's mind seemed to gain understanding of his current situation, he became less fuzzy and more pronounced. Soon, the face that spoke in Lucifer's mind was identical to the demon who had lost his life in the Great War some years earlier.

"I see. So we're losing this war now?" Valac lowered his gaze, seeming to look at the ground. "I had thought that eventually, Lord Satan would begin to prevail against the weak and simple-minded humans. I feel shame, knowing that I died during such an easy time compared to what you face now."

Lucifer nodded grimly. "Times are desperate. But... there is more. I found the secret to Satan's power, and because of what I discovered, in order to atone for my sins, Satan sent me to Mount Sinai, the location of the angels' home base, in order to retrieve this black orb."

Valac stared ahead blankly. His youthful expression belied his battle-scarred young body and all the years he had fought, not just as a demon who had evolved, but his fighting for survival as an imp. His special ability had been one that allowed him to find special treasure, the kind of ability that would normally lead someone to finding a rare artifact, such as the black orb he now resided in. _It is a cruel irony that he is now sealed inside an orb that he might have been able to find with his powers before he died._ Lucifer shook his head as he waved away the thought.

Lucifer hastily explained to Valac about Leviathan, how Satan had spared him, and his mission to find the black orb. Valac chuckled. "Hehehe... for a demon, you are remarkably pure hearted... and naive. You believe Satan did this for you to redeem yourself? He expected you to die, to be killed by the angels when you invaded their sacred grounds. This was anything but a chance for redemption, unless he expected you to redeem yourself by _dying_."

Lucifer stared at the orb and the face in his mind grimly, as thoughts swirled inside his head. "So... Satan wanted me to die? Then... I will likely never be trusted, ever again... even if I should return."

Valac grimaced for a second. _Sheesh, this guy is way too nice to be a demon. I should let him off easy._ "Look, kid, let me be frank. I don't know what my abilities are as an orb, and I can't predict the future, but anyone with half a brain knows that you will be betrayed by Satan at some point if you return. You simply _know too much_. If you go back there, you might as well sign your name in blood on a suicide contract, you get me?"

Lucifer nodded. "Then... if I cannot return, what can I do?"

Valac pondered it for a moment. "I'm not sure, but the first thing that springs to mind should be simple. Don't! Don't go back. Instead, strike out on your own. Together, you and I can gain our own power. We can help our people in the shadows, where we belong. Satan probably thinks you're dead anyway, so he'll do some sappy sad ceremony, yadda yadda, and then replace you. But you'll go on living your own life. Given time, anything could happen. What if Satan dies? Maybe you could even replace him as leader!"

Lucifer jerked his head up in alarm. "R-replace Satan?! No... no! I couldn't do that! Diablo would crush me easily!"

Valac mentally smacked himself in the face. "Boy, you're a real dim one, ain't ya? Look, you said yourself that Leviathan boosted your evolutionary abilities all the way to Satan's level right? That makes you practically _equal_ with him! And Satan is WAY above all the other demons, so that means _you are too_! Who better to lead the demons if Satan passes, than one who is his equal, who is a literal agent of the darkness, and who possesses some fancy dark orb?"

Lucifer turned from the orb and stared ahead blankly at a tree. "Your words... they have truth to them. Now that you mention it... perhaps Satan sent me away to get killed because he feared my power... if I'm as strong as you say, then it could result in a humiliating defeat for him."

Valac nodded. "Yes, now you get the point. So you would be perfect to take over for him if he dies!"

Lucifer grinned. "No... no... don't you see? I was his equal before I left. But now, I have _you_, and if I can just learn to harness your energy, I can easily crush Satan and take over as the Demon King. Then we will move on to other things, like erasing the angels and the gods from existence!"

Valac's face turned deathly white. "Uh, no, that's a bad idea. _Very bad_. I like you, okay? But I still respect Satan. He may have gotten his power through illicit means and he may have lied to our people, but I understand why he did it. Satan is a brilliant leader, and we need him to lead us through this war, until we have crushed the angels, and beyond."

Valac paused, as a sneer appeared on his face. "Besides, no offense, but as strong as you are, even if you used my power, I don't think you _can_ beat Satan. I fought him in a mock battle once. He didn't even use brute force, he outwitted me a hundred times over, it was a crippling embarrassment. You may be a red-skinned demon, but you're too _green_, kid. You'd never beat him as you are now."

Lucifer stared back at the face in his mind. "You think so, hm? I think... you are wrong. I can beat him. I can defeat him."

Valac grimaced. "Cheh! Fine, go ahead and try. But don't ask me for help. I won't give you any. I refuse to give treasonous aid to one who would harm my king."

Lucifer ground his teeth together. "I... don't think you understand the _position_ you're in. I'm not _asking_ you to help me. I'm going to _take_ your power, whether you want to give it to me or not. I am the master, and you are my servant."

Valac stared up numbly at Lucifer, as his eyes filled with greed. He bit his lip as he looked away. "...Yes. I thought so. You're not half the demon Satan is. Do what you must then, I wash my hands of it."

Lucifer quickly clamped both hands on the orb. "Shut up! **Give!** "

He concentrated intensely, as he felt dark power flowing from the orb into his hands and through those, into his body. He quietly started laughing, and the volume increased as more and more power flowed into his body.

" **Yes! With this power, I will RULE over the demons! Ahhh hahahaha!!!** "
