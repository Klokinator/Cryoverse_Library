Part 257a:

"Damn! Damn damn damn!" Harold cursed his bad luck as he eyed the display. Jason's life signs had vanished during a critical moment when Harold was keeping an eye on a clone, and now his body was being ruined by the prototype AI they had installed as a last resort.

"You moron! Why didja turn 'im loose?! He ain't ready yet!" Harold pounded an imaginative desk as he turned his advanced mental functions towards the inside of the suit. The AI was making millions of calculations per second as it processed multiple levels of data at once. "Centurion, turn yerself off 'n return to the mothership right this instant!"

"NEGATIVE. SAFETY PROTOCOLS WERE DISENGAGED. ONLY PILOT JASON MAY RE-ENABLE THEM. DISCONTINUING CONVERSATION TO FOCUS 100% OF PROCESSING POWER ON THE BATTLE. CENTURION OUT."

Without a single extra word, Harold felt his 'feed' go dead. "Fuckin' hunk o' junk! Yer rippin' the boy ta shreds, dumbass!"

With intense irritation, he turned his mind towards the clone in Hong Kong. "Jason 'Four', we gots an emergency! What are ya doin' right now?"

The mental connection faltered for a second as the clone moved into Harold's mind. "Ah! Sorry Harold, I'm cleaning up some rubble! There are survivors trapped under this collapsed building!"

"LEAVE 'EM! They'll be there in an hour when ye go back! The Original Jason just got whoppered by that damn demon! Even worse, he flipped the switch on Centurion, it ain't ready yet!"

"What? Centurion?! Why would he do that?! That thing's more dangerous than Umi!" The clone spoke with irritation, as he had all the same memories that Jason did. "Jesus, if that suit goes crazy, it could wipe out all of New York City!"

"EXACTLY! Git over there and turn it off! I can't do a ding-damn thing up here!"

"Alright, I'm on it!" Jason Four broke the mental link as Harold used the ship's teleporters to send him over to the battle occurring in Central Park. If he was lucky, this might not end on a catastrophic note.

...................................................................

Satan's eyes lit up as he activated the powers of the 63rd Hero, Alexander. Dashing towards the suit on his four new arms, he dragged along his shattered legs as his left eye pinned the suit in place for a single second. Using his two free hands, he conjured a cloud of smoke to obscure his movement.

The suit halted its speech as it detected his charge, leaping into a defensive position. Satan jumped through the smoke and grabbed hold of the suit as he wrapped all six of his limbs around the suit, his ten other arms jerking downwards and pounding on the suit with all their might.

"ALERT, CENTURION UNDER ATTACK! DAMAGE INSIGNIFICANT. DAMAGE INSIGNIFICANT. ADAPTING TO ATTACK PATTERN. PATTERN PROCESSED."

Satan screamed as he pounded the suit with all his might. "Mocking me?! That will be your last mista-!"

The suit suddenly increased its outer temperature, racing upwards at a rate of hundreds of degrees per second. Satan yelped as, within seconds, it grew to thousands of degrees. Jumping off, he healed his arms, then focus healed his legs. "You've got a lot of tricks."

"HOSTILE RECLASSIFIED: THREAT LEVEL ZETA PLUS. DISREGARD ALLIED FORCES. EXTERMINATION OF HOSTILE PRIORITIZED OVER CIVILIAN LIFE. FULL FORCE AUTHORIZED."

Satan raced towards the suit, but hesitated just before he was within striking distance as he sensed a massive spiritual presence suddenly appear. _Wait, is that another one?!_

Jason Four arrived, just in the nick of time. "Wait, Centurion! you need to deac-!"

Centurion rooted its feet into the ground as electricity was sucked in from the New York power grid, bolts of power shooting from lamp-posts into its mainframe. "YAMATO CANNON CHARGING. CANNON CHARGED. FIRING."

Satan gasped as he sensed a massive buildup of power inside the suit. _That will kill me!_ Every instinct in his body screamed to flee. Using the power of a demon he had once defeated in combat a thousand years ago, he buried himself into the ground in the blink of an eye as Centurion fired with all of its power.

BOOOOOOM!

A blast of energy shot out of Centurion's chestplate, starting small but widening in an instant to the size of twenty city blocks, the blast surging forwards over fifteen miles to the east as it erased hundreds of skyscrapers and other buildings from existence.

Three seconds later, Centurion's massive blast stopped as it cut the power.

"HOSTILE PRESENCE NOT DETECTED. SCANNING......... HOSTILE NOT FOUND. CONFIDENCE OF HOSTILE ELIMINATION: 85%."

"C-Centurion... disable all auto-pilot routines... Re-engage safety... protocols..." Jason Four stared numbly at the devastation in front of him. His own suit, securely in pilot mode, performed a quick sweep. 100% of all life forms caught in the blast had been eliminated. Billions, if not trillions of dollars of infrastructure were erased as if they had never existed...

"VOICE MATCH CONFIRMED. PILOT JASON HIRO MATCH CONFIRMED. ERROR. JASON HIRO CONFIRMED DECEASED. HYPOTHESIS. OTHER JASON HIRO IS AN IMPOSTER."

"I'm not an imposter! Code Cassiel-Blue-Theta, disable your auto-pilot functionality and re-engage your safety systems at once!"

"...OVERRIDE CODE CONFIRMED. CENTURION ENABLING SAFETY PROTOCOLS. HELLO, PILOT JASON HIRO." As the autopilot disengaged, Jason's body slumped to the ground as Jason Four ran over to him. " **Heal!** " Jason Four warily swept his senses over the area. He had not detected Satan's death. It would be too convenient if he died that easily.

He yelled in surprise as the corpse in front of him began to slowly repair itself. It had only been a few minutes since 'death', and recovering brain functions should be doable. " **Storage!** " Jason Four reached into their shared storage, pulling out Excalibur and attaching it to the side of Jason Original. "Good, that should... patch you up..."

Jason Four hesitated as he held the body of the Original in his arms. Was this really what he wanted to do? The original was just going to reintegrate him, like he'd done to Hope. Jason Four didn't want to die...

...No. He understood now what it meant for his clones to be sentient. The Original would not do that to his clones. They deserved to live. Jason Four felt this in his gut. If he wouldn't reintegrate himself, neither would the original.

After a few minutes, he sensed warmth returning to the body after he healed it a few more times. Jason Original finally stirred, moaning in pain as he groggily opened his eyes.

"A...alive... I'm alive?" Jason glanced up at his own face in surprise. "Clone...? You're a clone...?"

"Yes. I revived you. You were torn to pieces in the battle with Satan. You're lucky to have survived."

"Ah, yeah. Satan... wait, did he-?!" Jason hesitated as he glanced around, searching with his spatial vision.

"No, he's gone. Or at least, I can't sense him anymore. Centurion... might have killed him."

"What? Centurion? No... wait, that can't be... I activated the auto-pilot?"

Jason Four nodded soberly. "Yes. I don't know why, but you did... and the results were..." Four raised his gaze to the carnage in the East. Jason Original followed his gaze.

"...Oh my god... Centurion did this?"

"No. _You_ did this. A... lot of people died... _original_."

Jason nodded soberly as he felt tears well up. "This was my fault... I... I didn't mean to... How many d-died?"

Jason Four shook his head. "Millions."

Harold broke in to the conversation quietly. "Hey, kid. Glad ta see yer back on yer feet... but we need ta talk."

"Yeah we do... to say the least."

...................................................................

From a nearby skyscraper rooftop, Satan peered over the lip, keeping his body's energy levels down as low as possible while muffling his heat signature and as many other things as he could think of that might let him be detected. Staring at the two white-suited men on the grass, he grimaced in disgust.

"One of them, I could deal with. But two? Better to wait for another chance. It will present itself. What an enigma, that mortal is."

Shaking his head, Satan summoned a portal and crawled into it, closing it behind him.
