Part 081 - Darkness

Amelia and Blaarjiim were suddenly encased in darkness, as a massive cloud appeared over the horizon, blocking out the light of the sun. The sun's light disappeared and suddenly the day quickly became dark and dreary as a rain cloud formed over us.

Amelia and Blaarjiim began speaking together in unison.

_The power of darkness gives us our strength_

_The terror of death gives us our might_

_While separate we stay at arm's length_

_But when together, darkness our power unites!_

A dark cloud surrounded Amelia, as she and Blaarjiim finished their magic chant. It completely shrouded her from view. Inside, the red dot that I sensed vanished, and slowly became a black one as it reappeared.

The cloud withered away, and in front of me stood... something unnerving. It was still Amelia, but she appeared much older. Blaarjiim was no longer in her hands, they were both free. I could not tell what she was wearing due to my lack of eyesight, but Beelzebub described it for me. A black leather suit that encased her entire body, and a dark cowl that hid her hair, except that which hung out from behind the bottom of it. Amelia appeared to have gained more than a foot in height, and was now two inches taller than me.

But her eyes... her eyes now were pitch black. I did not need Beelzebub to inform me of this. I sensed two 'hollow' spots in my vision sphere, at the exact spot where her eyes would be. Pure black eyes that devoured the light around them, and the energy as well.

Her energy level was staggering. It raced out of her body at an explosive rate.

" **You must have angered my queen greatly for her to have assumed this form.** " Blaarjiim's voice spoke from out of Amelia's mouth. It almost seemed like...

"Did... the two of you...?"

" **Yes.** " The demonic voice was not as loud as I would have heard it in my mind, but it was still impossibly deep and gravelly. It reminded me of someone putting the bass on max with a subwoofer.

"Where is Amelia, then?" I stared 'her' body up and down with my mind. The presence had changed color. Was she simply dormant? Was she erased?

" **She's... inside me. I have taken over for now. But enough questions. She hates doing this, and I would not wish to anger my queen more than necessary.** "

He disappeared. Pain. I felt monstrous pain in the side of my head as he appeared next to me, smashing me in my right ear. The power was incredible, a wave of force erupted from the spot he hit and blasted the falling rain away and my body was sent flying helplessly across the ground. Before he could attack again, I managed to jab my hand out and stab into the ground, stopping my movement as I swirled my body around to face the attacker.

"Fuck." I cursed aloud, as blood trickled from my ear. He wasn't playing around. I dashed towards him and punched at him, teleporting behind him as I swung at the back of his neck. He ducked down in an instant, sweeping his foot under mine and tripping me, before I could even hit the ground, he punched me in the head, smashing me into the ground with incredible force.

He kept his fist pressed against my head. " **What were you saying, insect? Something about giving up? Don't make me laugh.** " He grabbed me by my right leg, and hurled me a great distance. Probably a half a mile or so. He hurled me away from the castle. The world spun and I struggled to regain my mental balance.

Before I hit the ground, I aimed my hand at my body.

"Halt!"

With a single word, my momentum stopped, though I began falling straight down I was able to land feetfirst.

Limping around badly, I quickly healed myself. The pain hurt but I just had to deal with it. I was getting a good sense of his movements now. He was definitely faster and stronger than me, two very bad combinations. But there is always a way out when you can make words reality.

"Invisibility!" I yelled, making myself go invisible. "Reptile!"

Now he couldn't track me with his eyes, or any sort of heat vision.

" **Heh heh, isn't that cute? You learned some tricks. I guess now that I can't see you, it's all over, eh?** "

And suddenly he was at my right side.

" **Wrong.** "

Another punch sent me flying.
