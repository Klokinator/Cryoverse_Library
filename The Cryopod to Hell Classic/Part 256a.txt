Part 256a

Satan stared at the body that he now watched being crushed by the Hand of Theseus. He sighed as the mortal had now lost all ability to move and was having the life squeezed out of him.

"Hahh... and here I thought this one might be some fun." Satan groaned as he slumped down, sitting on the corpse of a recently killed human who had been slain less than an hour before in this very park. "In the end, they're all the same. They simply cannot defeat one like myself with millennia of battle experience."

Satan stared emptily at the corpse as he finally retracted the Hand of Theseus. He was able to sense Jason's body heat lowering as his life signs went cold, there was no need to fight anymore. "That suit might prove useful. I'll have to take it back to my human scientist slaves and see if they can discover how it works. An army equipped with it could do some real dama-"

As he spoke, suddenly he noticed something odd, causing him to freeze mid-sentence. Jason's hands... they were moving? "Impossible. That attack should have killed him instantly."

As he had that thought, all at once, the entire body began twitching, as the mortal stood up once more. "How did you survive that? Wait... no..." He narrowed his eyes as he stared at the man. His body was still growing colder. He had to be dead, yet...

"AUTOPILOT MODE ENGAGED. ASSESSING DAMAGE TO SYSTEMS......... DAMAGE LEVEL INSIGNIFICANT. SUIT CONFIRMED AT 99.9% INTEGRITY AND HOLDING. BIOREACTOR STATUS CHECK... 99.9% CONFIRMED."

Satan paused with bewilderment as the man's mouth was not moving, but instead a strange, inhuman voice was speaking. Was this the 'robotic' voice he had heard about? Not being one to keep up with current technology, Satan left most of that boring hocus pocus to the younger demons, but he recalled how one particular demon had mentioned a concept of automated 'robots' that moved on their own... could this be...

"ASSESSING BATTLE READINESS. READINESS CONFIRMED. ALL SYSTEMS NOMINAL. PROTOTYPE AI 'CENTURION' ONLINE. ASSESSING LANDSCAPE STATUS."

The head of the mortal stiffly began to pivot back and forth, as if he were examining the horizon carefully.

"LANDSCAPE STATUS CONFIRMED. CASUALTY LIST: SEVEN THOUSAND, SIX HUNDRED AND FORTY FOUR DEAD. ONE ALIVE. ALERT! HOSTILE DETECTED. PROCESSING HOSTILE THREAT LEVEL."

Satan gaped openly at the strange way this monotone voice was speaking. What the absolute devil was a 'Centurion'? How was the human still moving, despite being completely dead? What was going on?!

"HOSTILE THREAT LEVEL ASSESSED. **ZETA THREAT LEVEL CONFIRMED**. DISABLING SAFETY PROTOCOLS. ENABLING PROTOTYPE PRIMARY AND SECONDARY FUNCTIONS. MISSION STATEMENT: ELIMINATE ALL HOSTILES USING ANY FORCE NECESSARY. OVERRIDING ALL SAFETY BUFFERS. PILOT UNABLE TO RESPOND, BUFFERS OVERRIDDEN."

At the exact instant the strange voice stopped speaking, with a blast of motion so fast he barely had time to even process it, Satan felt himself battered from ten directions at once.

Moving at twelve times the speed of sound, the suit bypassed safety protocols that might damage the pilot, as the pilot was dead. Using insane feats of contortion and bending, it systematically began snapping the limbs of its pilot as it wholly ignored Jason's physical status. Before Satan had a moment to think, the suit delivered colossal blows that could shatter tanks into every section of his legs, blasting thousands of micro and macro-fractures throughout every bone.

Satan screamed in pain as white hot agony surged in his mind. Before he could gather where the suit had moved to, it reappeared above him, aiming both palms at the devil below it. Wordlessly, it ejected a highly focused blast of fire straight down, the temperature exceeding the core of the sun for the flash of a second. Satan felt the world melt away as the blast pierced through his brain and down into his body, coming out of his bottom and into the ground ten miles before it dissipated into the asthenosphere below.

The suit halted all movement as it came to a grinding halt a few feet away, its head jerking robotically towards the falling body of the devil.

"ASSESSING HOSTILE THREAT STATUS. CHANCE OF TERMINATION SUCCESS: 25%. ASSESSING... ASSESSING..."

Satan flopped to the ground for a few moments, as his regeneration took over. In a flash, his mind returned as he blinked his eyes open, spots dancing all over the place. "Ah... ahhh... wha...?" Groggily, he stumbled back to his feet as he cast the highest level heal spell he could think of. "By the seven devils, that was... close..." He warily eyeballed the mortal in front of him. This one had changed so rapidly... perhaps it was a hidden personality, much like Hades within Satan's body, it could only come out if he were to die?

Quickly putting himself on guard he gnashed his teeth. "That hurt. No more jokes. You're dangerous."

"ASSESSMENT COMPLETE. TERMINATION FAILED. ADAPTING STRATEG-"

Before that _thing_ could finish its _assessment_, Satan activated his own special _mode_. High Devil was a special battle transformation he hadn't used in centuries, not since his battle with the forty-fifth hero.

Growing another four arms, two on each side of his body, and putting all of his ten invisible limbs on automated defense, Satan sneered as he charged at the 'Centurion' before him. _There may be a day when I die, but that will not be on this day._
