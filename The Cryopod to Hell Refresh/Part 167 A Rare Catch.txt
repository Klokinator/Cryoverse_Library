Davook flicks his eyes up and down her body, from head to toe. "What a catch. I thought this day couldn't get any better, but I was wrong! Alright, how about this? If you ain't gonna tell us your name, then we'll call you... Red! Yeah, how do you like that? Red's such a cute name!"

Davook's second personality immediately counters his own words. "I'd prefer Bloodhair. It's appropriate. The pigeons call us Bloodskins, so why not call a pigeon Bloodhair?"

"No way! A cute name for a cute girl."

"She ain't cute; you're just nasty."

Kirie curls herself into a ball. She shivers as Davook continues to talk about her as if she isn't there. "M-my name is Kirie! Not Red... not Bloodhair! Don't call me w-whatever you want... filthy demon..."

Davook whistles. "Hey! A name! She has one after all! Boy, we have to respect that fighting spirit. You haven't got any mana left, yet you're still acting like a big bad angel warrior. Hahaha! I love it when girls fight back."

A nasty look appears in Davoook's eyes.

"Yeah... that's right. It ain't no fun if a girl's meek and submissive..."

Davook's formerly lighthearted attitude disappears. All of Kirie's courage vanishes as her eyes meet those of the horrible monster planning to enslave her. 

"Please... please kill me..."

Her pitiful begging only makes Davook grin like a madman.

"Nah. I don't waste perfectly serviceable goods. I've got a better idea. How about you come along with me, and the two of us treat each other _real_ nice?"

Davook reaches toward Kirie. Shadows fire from his hand and wrap around her face, cutting off her oxygen.

Kirie gasps and tries to use her broken left arm to paw at her face, but her strength isn't enough to resist.

Eventually, her consciousness fades. She slumps backward and falls comatose, leaving Davook to rub his hands together with glee.

"Oooh! Oh, boy, oh, boy. These new shadow powers are awesome! We can do whatever we want, and nobody can stop us!"

Davook's grin vanishes. "Don't lump me in with the likes of you. I don't know what you're planning, and I don't _want_ to know. When will _I_ get what _I_ want, for once?"

"Pft. You're way too dull. What do you even want?"

"...I'd like to play some Sticks and Bones."

"BORING! Getting chicks is way better than playing stupid games with other demons. Now shut your face and help me pick her up."

Davook uses his shadows to levitate Kirie into his grasp. He wraps one arm around her and grunts as the unconscious cherub hangs limply over his shoulder.

"Damn. This girl's heavier than I expected. Or maybe we're just weak?"

With a shrug, Davook melts into his shadow and takes his prisoner along for the ride. The two of them disappear into the distance as he heads for Heaven's Gate.

"Hoo-hoo! What a great day! What a great day, indeed!"
