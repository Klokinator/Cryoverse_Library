Part 450: Sects Strike the Shadows

Mara, the Duke of Collection, and Xena, the Baron of Trading. Not long ago, they were roughly as powerful as one another. Both of them were Barons. While Mara was listed as the public face of the clan and often referred to as the 'leader' of the Tarot Sect, her actual demonic strength, like that of every other member aside from Yumagi himself, never exceeded the rank of Baron.

But that changed thanks to the Belial Booster.

Mara stepped into the next tier of demonic power, placing herself truly ahead of all her sect-mates, making them deeply envy her good fortune. Even the judgment by the Wordsmith that she would never be allowed to step into the rank of Emperor didn't temper their envy. After all, fewer than a percent of a percent of all demons would ever have the opportunity to earn enough souls to become a Duke.

But at this moment, as the two women charge toward the enemies below, Xena finds herself in that lofty position, temporarily strengthened to the rank of Emperor through Yumagi's mystical Tarot powers.

The Heroic Empowerment elevates one demon to a rank far above their station, while the Aspect of the Dual Dragons duplicates that effect to a second individual, allowing Xena to rise along with her sect-sister.

Together, the two of them form a terrifying duo... the first ever public battle between two Emperors capable of wielding the power of Tarot Cards!

Xena lunges toward Emperor Zamiel and his stolen crocodilian body. She motions with her hands to draw a Tarot card, but her heart jumps into her throat when instead of one card appearing in her grasp... three cards materialize!

_What?!_

Mara also experiences that same shock. Unlike her fellow disciple, she has become quite familiar with the elevation of her abilities after she reached the rank of Duke. Mara became capable of maintaining ten sustained Tarot abilities at once through their Imbue effects, but that still pales in comparison to the awesome ability granted by her Emperor level empowerment.

_Three cards at once?_ Mara mentally shrieks in delight. _How can any other Emperor possibly defeat me now?!_

Indeed. This simple boost does more than grant her a couple of extra options in battle. It essentially triples her strength, a boost to her demonic power almost never seen when demons jump from Duke to Emperor!

Not only can Mara and Xena summon three cards each time they 'draw' from their Mind Realms, but they can draw cards every five seconds in battle, rather than every ten.

Added together, this means they can activate up to six abilities every ten seconds, instead of only one.

A sixfold increase in combat strength!

Mara rushes toward the necromancer below her.

"Thorns of the Raven, **Imbue!** Eldritch Dragon, **Imbue!** Secret Effect, **Trap!**"

Mara fingers transform into bird-like claws. Leathery dragon-wings sprout from her back. At the same time, she invokes a trap effect, providing her a secret benefit as that card dematerializes.

**BOOM!**

Mara crashes into Mephisto's figure and smashes him into the dirt, stunning the necromancer's senses. Before she can deliver a follow-up blow, he dives underground and tries to run away, only to comically _splat_ against a magical force-field just thirty feet below him.

"What?!" Mephisto roars. "A magical barrier? We've been trapped! That Yumagi TRICKED usss!"

The necromancer whips out his dagger and starts desperately slashing at the invisible barrier barring his escape, but a yanking sensation grabs onto him and starts dragging him back toward the surface.

"No, no, no!" Mephisto howls in alarm. "Let go of me, you insssidiousss wench!"

Mara's latest attack comes from the effects of yet another three cards, all of which she imbues onto her body. One of them grants her a form of somewhat weak telekinesis, which she uses to yank Mephisto out of the dirt while also charging electricity onto her claw-hands.

"Come here Mephisto! I've got a present for you!" Mara laughs maniacally as she pulls Mephisto right into the path of her awaiting fist.

Mara's strength, empowered with the metaphorical might of a bear, allows her to launch a punch capable of destroying tanks directly at Mephisto's skull. The instant before her attack lands, Mephisto narrows his eyes and explodes into smoke, becoming totally invisible while dispersing himself over a large region. Mara's attack harmlessly whiffs through him, ultimately exploding in midair to discharge a burst of electric energy over the surrounding area.

"Coward! Try standing your ground and fighting for once!" Mara challenges. "Unless you admit you can't beat even one lousy Fake Emperor!"

At the same time as Mara and Mephisto's war, Xena and Zamiel also come to blows. However, unlike his boss, who does not perform well in physical engagements, Zamiel is a prolific combat veteran possessing the powerful body of the former Monster King, Kar. His strength goes well above what he would have possessed himself as an Emperor, and his scaled hide gives him quite the resilient defenses. The only downside is that Zamiel's soul-strength is only that of a Duke. This means his magical abilities are not nearly as strong as his physical body.

Xena opts to deal with the scale-thief by bombarding him from afar. She continuously imbues her body with magical effects, many of which come from Tarot Cards she has never seen before! Thanks to the mystical magic passed down to all of Yumagi's disciples, they possess the unique ability to instantly identify the effects of any Tarot Card they touch in all its activatable forms.

Thus, even when Xena finds herself continuously drawing ten and twenty cards she has never seen before in her life, she still instantly understands their effects and what will happen when she casts them.

"Mountain From the Sky! **Imbue**! Godsworn Hurricane, **Evoke**! Sun Guardian of Cairo, **Summon**!"

One after the other, Xena imbues her body with an array of mystical traits, becoming something of an eldritch monster with a horrifying array of abilities. While imbuement might provide the second weakest of all Tarot effects, it also has a 100% success rate in casting, doesn't damage the card, and lasts a full ten minutes until the effect runs out, making it one of the most reliable methods to use during combat. Only Enchanting specific objects, pieces of armor, or other things summoned by Tarot cards is more reliable, albeit a tad weaker.

Most shockingly, after ascending to the rank of Emperor, Xena becomes just as delighted as Mara when she finds out she can now maintain a whopping twenty active Tarot effects! This primarily relates to Imbuing and Enchanting, but also one of her strongest auxiliary Tarot abilities...

Summoning!

Xena finishes up her fifth round of card activations by conjuring the image of an ancient Egyptian Titan to reality. Thanks to the mystical methods behind Yumagi's Tarot creation powers, this card is somehow able to materialize the physical manifestation of a twenty-foot-tall Titan in the physical realm, though one that ultimately acts as nothing more than a soulless construct, rather than a summoning of the original figure.

Zamiel's eyes widen in shock as, while Xena continues attacking him with her imbued powers, a massive figure materializes behind her, the manifestation of the ancient Titan known as Sun God, Ra.

Shining with golden light, Ra's sculpted muscles make him truly resemble a deity towering above mere mortals. His falconic head peers into the distance, looking beyond the mortal realm into that of the immaterial. His armored gauntlets grant him incredible offensive and defensive potential in hand to hand combat, completed by the golden snake-staff held in his grasp.

The instant Ra's summoning completes, the ancient Titan turns his gaze toward the ten-foot-tall crocodilian who barely reaches his waist. He stomps toward Zamiel, forcing the Duke of Weaponry to retreat while also defending against Xena's onslaught.

"Two against one?!" Zamiel complains. "That's not fair!"

"Neither is stealing someone else's body!" Xena shouts.

Her body moves with the speed and power of a hurricane. She races around Zamiel, beating him from the front and back with gales of wind and bolts of thunder while her summoned construct, Ra, unleashes blasts of searing flame upon the Duke of Weaponry.

While Zamiel's Duke-level magic might not be comparable to that of his opponents, he still holds his own. He conjures Qi to form a thin shield of energy around his body. He strikes back at Xena, using his claws to try and shred her skin down to the bone.

Over the next several minutes, Xena continues activating dozens of Tarot cards. Sometimes she dispels different effects to bring forth new ones, but always she tries to maintain the maximum limit of twenty effects so as to maintain pressure on Zamiel.

At one point, Xena flings a Tarot-empowered spear at Zamiel's back during a moment of distraction. But this proves to be a mistake! Zamiel's mastery of weaponry has become so incredible that any attack launched against him through non-magical means has a good chance of being stolen by him. And he does exactly this! Zamiel spins on his heel, snatches the spear an instant before it impacts him, and transforms it into his own weapon.

"Stupid woman!" Zamiel cackles. "Thanks for the gift!"

"Shit!" Xena curses. She tries to undo the conjuration of this Tarot-artifact, but finds that Zamiel has enveloped it in Qi to temporarily make it his own! For the next ten minutes, he now possesses a weapon he can wield as an implement of destruction!

Zamiel launches an offensive. He charges and stabs at Xena to try and impale her on her own spear, but her summon frustratingly continues blasting him with fire and light, burning his back and royally pissing him off.

"If my little bro was still here, we'd have made mincemeat of you!" Zamiel shrieks.

"Too bad he died to the claws of that very same body you've possessed!" Xena taunts back. "It must feel wonderful, living in the skin of your brother's killer!"

"BITCH!"

Meanwhile.

As Mara and Xena beat the crap out of their opponents, Yama watches from the sidelines, anxiously wanting to jump in and help out. Unfortunately, due to the restrictions on his soul, placed by Satan the Devil... he can't hurt a woman without suffering terrible backlash to his soul!

And even if he did want to help, Yama simply can't, all due to Yumagi's presence!

"Damn!" Yama curses. "We cannot survive without Mephisto's protections. If he dies here, the Wordsmiths will be able to locate us! Then it will truly be over for us..."

Yama grits his amorphous teeth. He transmits a brief string of telepathic words to Mephisto.

_I'll try to break free of Yumagi's barrier! Once I've made an opening, you and Zamiel should escape through it!_

Without waiting for a reply, Yama darts away from the battle. He crashes against the barrier enveloping the battlefield, transforming his shadows into a series of battering rams. Over and over, he beats against the wall of invisible energy, trying desperately to make an opening so he can escape.

Before Yama can succeed, a figure materializes behind him.

"Going somewhere?" Yumagi asks.

Yama quickly spins to face his enemy. "You! We do not fear you! If you attempt to hinder us, we will fight back!"

Multiple dots of light glow on Yumagi's forehead, indicating the Tarot effects he continues to maintain. He stands with his arms crossed, looking down the bridge of his nose at the Emperor of Shadows.

"I'd be happy to fight you, Yama. But are you _sure_ you want to go that route? If I can handle a handful of Archangels, well... I fear the pain I might cause you to suffer if we come to blows. You and I are _such_ good friends, too."

Yama's shadowy heart turns chilly as he feels the Glyphmancer's stare beating down on him. He struggles to utter a comeback, but in his heart of hearts he knows he poses no threat to Yumagi.

_Even the Archangels could not resist his Tarot Magic until they transformed into the Cherubiim. What chance do we stand in a fight?_

With Yama unable to properly fight against two of his opponents and Zamiel's soul-strength significantly weaker than that of his direct opponent, the only demon on their side who seems to have an equal level of fighting strength compared to his enemy is Mephisto.

But Mephisto certainly does not seem to be much of a brawler! His greatest strengths lay in flooding the battlefield with cannon fodder to overrun weaker opponents. As for taking on enemies of equal or greater strength, Mara quickly realizes that he is far out of his league!

"You're pathetic, Mephisto!" Mara cackles. "To think I was afraid of you before... that's only because you were an Emperor and I was a Baron! But now I see that you're the weakest of your entire rank!"

Mephisto maintains his mist-body form, his strongest defensive ability that allows him to scatter apart and weave around Mara's attacks. Despite the inherent danger in her electrically imbued claws and fists, Mephisto manages to evade her attacks by virtue of becoming all but entirely incorporeal.

Mara might as well be trying to smother the morning fog by waving her hands around like an idiot!

As the minutes pass, Xena manages to gain ground against Zamiel, blasting him backward with her attacks, though not necessarily dealing a lot of direct damage due to his physical defenses. But as for Mara, she becomes frustrated at her inability to land even a glancing blow on Mephisto.

"Stand still and fight like a man!" Mara snarls, while pulling a card out of her Mind Realm. "Thunder Giant, **Evoke!**"

Mara's imbued abilities fail to deal any damage to her opponent, so she instead resorts to her strongest single-action type of attacks. Evocations! This causes her to instantly unleash the full power of a Tarot Card, albeit at the cost of making it unusable for the next seven hours.

While this might be a grossly inefficient way of using her abilities, the tradeoff is a massive burst of magic capable of overcoming her opponent's defenses!

A huge fist made of thunder roars out of Mara's soul, blasting the area around and directly in front of her with a thunderous BOOM!! Mephisto cries out in pain as he finally eats a portion of electrical damage directly to the face. His mist-body destabilizes, and he jolts back into reality, falling out of the air to return to his physical form.

The Emperor of Legions crashes to the dirt with a violent _thud_ and grinds to a stop. He weakly looks up as Mara races toward him.

"Gotcha!" Mara exclaims, while an evil grin crosses her face. "You've got nowhere to run now, Mephisto!"

She lifts another card, preparing to evoke it upon her helpless enemy. But at that moment, Mephisto's eyes gleam in a manner most sinister.

Having spent the last few minutes preparing a powerful series of spells, Mephisto presses his teeth together and focuses his mind and soul...

"Ssstupid woman... overconfidenccce isss a ssslow and insssidiousss killer... **DARK MISSST!**"

Mephisto abruptly levitates to his feet and raises his arms. An instant later, a thick and viscous cloud of darkness explodes from his body, the very same ability he used during Stormbringer! But this time, the ability is ten times more potent, traveling in all directions and flooding the area with a fog so dense that every combatant's vision becomes reduced to a fraction of their previous level!

Yumagi's barrier, created for the purpose of trapping his enemies in place, instead transforms the region into a giant glass jar, one which fully contains not only Mephisto's minions, but also his Dark Mist. The pitch-black fog completely smothers everyone's vision, all except for those most adept in fighting within its confines...

And who just so happens to fit that criteria? None other than Mephisto himself, his undead minions...

...and Yama.

Yama, previously appearing useless and frightened, suddenly grins evilly at Yumagi.

"**Heh heh heh... finally. Mephisto used it.**"

Without warning, he lunges at Yumagi, startling the Glyphmancer! The Emperor of Shadows achieves his highest level of power as he explodes his Shadow Realm outward, engulfing Yumagi in its confines and forcing him to go on the defensive.

At the same time, Xena finds her vision terribly diminished. While demons can indeed see quite well even in pitch blackness, along with their other sensory abilities, this still pales in comparison to Zamiel himself, who has spent thousands of years training under his 'boss' to fight in the Dark Mist. Additionally, as one of Mephisto's undead, he doesn't suffer the slightest loss of vision at all!

"It's time to turn the tables, woman!" Zamiel roars. "No more card games for you!"

With one move, Mephisto instantly reverses the momentum of battle. He begins racing around within the blackened fog, pulling out his Pain Dagger to strike at Mara's sides and back. Inside the Dark Mist, Mephisto's speed and agility quadruple, turning him into a terrifying wraith that Mara simply can't keep up with!

_Shik, shik, shik!_

"Aaaieee! Nnnnn!!"

Mara screams as Mephisto unleashes Pain Inducement upon her. While his attacks don't deal any physical damage, they cause her pain receptors to violently seize up, preventing her from activating Tarot Cards. The excruciating agony forces her teeth to grind together, as if she's been struck by a dozen lightning bolts. Mara tries to twist around and claw at the necromancer dogging her, but he deftly weaves beneath her palm strikes to stab her stomach, her thighs, and even her feet.

Meanwhile, the sole shining beacon of light on the battlefield is none other than Xena's summon, the Titan Sun God, Ra. Ra emits light brilliant enough to pierce a bit of the gloom, but even his brilliance pales compared to Mephisto's full-power Emperor strength. As a mere summon conjured by a Tarot Card, Ra does not have the true power of his original Titan body, nor does he have the soul strength or intelligence, either.

But still, Ra continues to supplement Xena's strength, allowing her to put up more of a fight inside the Dark Mist than her sect-sister can. Xena begins rapidly evoking cards to pummel and blast Zamiel, forcing him backward. But at the same time, Zamiel doesn't just take the beating passively. He weaves from side to side, evading her attacks wherever possible.

"You'll have to do better than that, woman!" Zamiel jeers after dodging a massive fireball. "I'm not gonna go down easy!"

Without warning, Zamiel dives toward the dirt. Xena experiences a terrible shock when he reveals a secret ability, the power of earth manipulation! Stolen from the soul remnants of none other than Monster King Kar himself, Zamiel dives under Xena's feet, then clamps his claws onto her ankles from below to yank her into the dirt. Xena shrieks in fright as she plunges waist-deep into the soil, only managing to save herself by lashing an imbued shadow-tendril out at the nearby cliff-face to hold on.

"Let go of me!" Xena yells.

A moment later, Zamiel swims underneath her, opens his crocodilian maw, and bites onto her legs. Xena screams as the Duke of Weaponry entangles her with his body and begins spinning underneath the soil.

He initiates a death roll!

Immediately, Xena loses her grip on the cliffs above. Her magically imbued body becomes worthless as the crocodile yanks her fully beneath the soil and begins spinning around as if he were in a lake of water rather than underground soil. Unable to fight back, Xena's face repeatedly smashes against rocks. Soil embeds in her nose. Blood pours from her legs. She tries to scream, but only ends up with huge mouthfuls of dirt that suffocate her.

In less than 30 seconds, Xena falls unconscious!

Unable to fight back, she becomes totally powerless under Zamiel's strength. The crocodilian suddenly releases her legs, then 'swims' up her body before rearing his claws back for a deadly strike.

_RIIIP!!_

Zamiel impales Xena's heart, killing her on the spot.

The beautiful blood-red-haired Baron of Trading perishes to Zamiel's combat prowess!

The moment she falls, Mara and Yumagi both gasp. They sense the disappearance of her life-force through their shared Tarot Link, leaving them in deep shock.

"Suh-sect-sister!" Mara gasps, in between Mephisto's attacks.

"BASTARD! You dare kill one of my disciples?!" Yumagi roars, still trapped inside Yama's Shadow Realm. "Wrath of Sol, **EVOKE!**"

At once, a stunning brilliance explodes inside Yama's domain of absolute darkness. His army of shadow-walkers and Shades screams in fright, scattering as quickly as they can while a counter-domain of absolute light erupts like a bomb, blasting apart the shadows.

An instant later, Yumagi bursts out of the shadows and back into the material realm, he aims his sight in the direction of Xena's fallen form.

"Ruler's Reclamation Right, **EVOKE!**"

Zamiel raises his guard, anticipating some form of brutal attack. However, to his mild surprise... nothing major seems to happen. A faint trail of magic departs Xena's soul and returns to Yumagi, but otherwise, her body remains untouched.

_Hm?_ Zamiel thinks. _What the hell did Yumagi do? I thought he was going to attack, but he didn't?_

Yumagi turns his attention to Mara.

"Disciple! We're leaving! Temporal Cricket, **Evoke!** Rising Star, **Evoke!**"

Yumagi heals Mara's wounds. She opens her mouth to argue that they can still defeat Mephisto and his allies, but before she can get a word out, she and Yumagi both transform into beams of light.

An instant later, they fire straight up into the sky! Like rockets launched instantly at ten times the speed of sound, they blast upward and disappear over the horizon, racing far too quickly for Mephisto and the others to stop them.

Moments later, the barrier enveloping the three remaining demons fades away. Xena's Titan construct dissipates to ash, leaving nothing behind by Mephisto's rapidly-dispersing Dark Mist.

A minute later, the three demons regroup. Mephisto holds his conciliatory 'prize,' the body of Xena, and frowns deeply.

"We won?" Zamiel asks. "Hahaha! We beat Yumagi's ass, boss! And we even got this ugly broad for your uses!"

Yama creeps toward Mephisto and Zamiel. His expression turns ugly.

"We do not understand. The Glyphmancer still had strength to spare. He has many more Barons he could summon and empower to fight us. Why would he flee so suddenly?"

Mephisto continues gazing at Xena's defeated body. After a moment, he procures her soul and places it inside, raising her as an undead thrall.

Xena jerks back to life. Despite the hole where her heart used to be, she remains awake in undeath, a look of horror stretched across her face.

"You!" Xena shouts at Zamiel. "You KILLED me!"

"Quiet." Mephisto hisses, his powers instantly forcing her to comply. "Yumagi fled after your death. Tell usss why he left in sssuch a hurry."

Xena struggles against Mephisto's soul bindings. However, her body has already lost its Emperor-level strength, returning her to the rank of Baron. She lacks any ability to stand against him.

"I... I do not know..." Xena says hesitantly. "I cannot imagine why my master would abandon me..."

"We turned the odds in our favor." Zamiel says confidently. "That Yumagi seems impressive, but he's nothing much at all, boss. Once we fixed the numbers in our favor, he didn't stand a chance."

Mephisto slowly shakes his head. "I would not be ssso cccertain. You do not know Yumagi like I do. During the Energy Warsss, hisss ssstrength was unparalleled. In the last 100,000 yearsss, he hasss become even more terrifying. Creating a sssect of disssciplesss with the sssame powersss asss himssself isss no mean feat."

As Mephisto thinks out loud, Xena suddenly gasps. She quickly falls silent, but Mephisto still gazes at her coldly, suspecting the worst.

"You've thought of sssomething!" Mephisto accuses. "What isss it? Ssspill the beansss!"

Xena tries not to speak, but against Mephisto's commands, she is powerless.

"My... my Tarot Cards..." Xena hisses. "They... they are... gone..."

"Gone?!" Yama exclaims. "We knew it! That Yumagi, that bastard! He must have taken her abilities before he left! He stole them so we would not have access to them!"

Mephisto's expression turns ugly. "Do you have any powersss beyond the onesss Yumagi gave you?"

Xena lowers her eyes.

"No. All of my master's recruits must join him when they are mere grunts or imps. I don't know the exact reasons, but he either can't or won't empower anyone above the rank of Lord. As such, we all develop the same Tarot abilities as our master."

For several minutes, Mephisto and the others remain quiet. Mephisto ruminates on this revelation, ultimately realizing an important truth.

"Hehehe... ssstupid woman. You accepted a deal with a devil. Hehehe..."

Yama nods. "Truly clever, that Yumagi. He binds hapless grunts to his power. If they try to leave him, or if they are captured, he revokes their powers. To him, his disciples are little more than disposable pawns."

"DISPOSABLE?!" Xena shouts. "Lies! My master will not forgive you! He will come to save me! I have served him faithfully since the Energy Wars! He will make you regret your actions!"

Zamiel crosses his arms. He looks at the lovely red-haired demoness with a mixture of pity and disgust.

"Take it from me, lady. You don't mean shit to Yumagi. He used you and threw you aside the moment you lost your value. Even took back his stupid cards so you wouldn't expose his secrets. Hehehe! You were truly nothing to him!"

"No..." Xena whispers. "That can't be right..."

For a brief moment, the air between the four becomes a little awkward.

Yama licks his shadowy lips. "It seems this female won't be of much use to you, Mephisto. Not without her powers, in any case... what say you give her to _us_? We will make good use of her body..."

"Pisss off!" Mephisto barks. "You wretched rapissst! Thisss female ssstill has ussse to me! Devilsss, you are sssuch a disssgusssting wretch!"

Yama shrugs. "It was worth a try."

The group of four returns to the Labyrinth, unaware that on the world of Tarus II, a powerful expedition force has begun assembling... one that will arrive at their doorstep.

Perhaps sooner than anyone realizes...
