Part 736: Two-Faced

_Far-Future Era. Day 21, AJR. The Garden of Eden._

Ose was in a good mood. An _exceptional_ mood, even. She had a spring in her step and couldn't stop snapping her fingers in rhythm to some unknown tune in her head. After she left the Soul Transference room, she couldn't help but skip along giddily to her next destination with a silly grin on her face.

"Hee-hee, eyes to see with, hands to grasp the world around me, and feet capable of touching the ground. What's not to love about having a body? Even if it's a little weak, I'll be back to my peak condition in no time."

Ose momentarily thought that her attitude seemed a little too sunshine-and-rainbows compared to how she usually acted, but then again, who could blame her for being so happy? Only a day earlier she had thought she would be trapped listening to Bael run his mouth for the rest of eternity, but now she had regained life, along with a decently attractive female body instead of a disgusting, overweight male one.

Naturally, possessing Bael's body back in the day had amazing upsides. She obtained access to all of her original powers, plus Bael's nearly invincible physique, which made her obscenely powerful. But even so, a woman as proud of her finely maintained appearance as Ose was could never be happy if she was trapped in the body of a slovenly fat bastard like Bael. Had she continued to exist in it without dying, she undoubtedly would have started a rigorous weight loss and training regimen to slim down. Within a year or two, she would have become unrecognizable!

But that was all in the past. She possessed Abby's body now, which was not as amazing as her original body, but it was a fine alternative. Maybe someday Ose would be able to figure out how to trade souls with Bael to reacquire her original self. She would definitely laugh at his misfortune if he ended up being the one stuck in Abby's body instead of her. It would serve him right.

Ose paused her skipping. She waved her hand, then frowned. Her happy mood deflated visibly.

"Hmm. Come on, why isn't it working?" Ose muttered.

She tapped into her magical core, her inner power, and tried to conjure lightning, electricity, even a single spark of energy. But after making all sorts of motions with her hands and concentrating her mind for a full minute, she failed to produce any results.

Ose's mood turned ugly.

"...I see. It seems I truly have been crippled." Ose said, her expression dismal. "I cannot produce any electricity. I may have completely lost all of my original powers!"

A thought sprang to Ose's mind. She changed her course and trotted over to an out-of-the-way part of the Garden of Eden. It was an isolated area where 'prisoners' were held, though these prisoners didn't actually know they had been imprisoned.

Only Ose knew the true location of the Garden of Eden. Not even the robots inside the facility knew. She had taken every precaution to keep its location a secret. Abby might not have been the brightest, but she understood the Garden of Eden was buried deep beneath the surface of an unknown planet somewhere in the Milky Way. What neither she nor Bael knew was that this underground facility was far, far bigger than it seemed, and they already thought it was massive.

Ose stepped inside a special region known as Inner Zone 2. There were three of these zones, and each of them formed artificial terrariums where clueless monsters had been carefully relocated, their memories wiped, the creatures completely oblivious to the fact they were now living in an isolated world very different from where they had been born and raised.

Inner Zone 2 was designated the Goblin Terrarium. Here, obviously, Goblins lived and thrived, with an entire ecosystem of food ready for them to consume, dirt for them to dig, and burrows for them to sleep and live in. Goblins typically lived underground, digging burrows beneath the soil to raise their families, but they were perfectly capable of constructing homes and other abodes on the surface as well. Different tribes had different customs.

Ose stood atop a catwalk high in the sky, one that was cleverly hidden from view so that the Goblins a mile below wouldn't be able to see her. With her keen demonic vision, Ose was able to peer down and make out the individual hairs on each Goblin's head.

She watched as a small group of goblins carried around a stretcher made of bone and animal-skins. On this stretcher there was a dead animal's carcass, likely the feast they had just hunted and would soon bring home for their village to consume.

Ose, naturally, did not care about these Goblins in the slightest. She came here for one specific reason.

"I'm quite fortunate." Ose mused. "Abby didn't just eradicate her soul, she fed my soul sliver with hers, so I now possess the power of a Baron. It thus follows that if I don't have access to my powers anymore, I must possess hers!"

Ose knew plenty about Abby's abilities. They didn't seem too potent, but essentially Abby could influence the thoughts and emotions of other beings, feed them illusions, and deceive their minds. Personally, Ose thought Abby had always drastically undervalued her abilities and utilized them poorly. If those were Ose's new powers, then she was sure to make better use of them.

Ose stretched out her hands. She aimed at the group of Goblins below, and tapped into her magical core, frowning as she began to concentrate.

She concentrated...

And concentrated...

Then she concentrated some more...

Sweat built up on Ose's forehead. Invisible question marks popped up above her head.

"Strange. Very strange. Why can't I influence them? Am I misunderstanding how Abby's abilities work? No, I wouldn't make such an elementary mistake..."

For once in her life, Ose was truly baffled. She couldn't activate her abilities, and she couldn't activate Abby's. For thirty long minutes, she panted and groaned, made all sorts of weird movements with her hands, and even tapped into her inner self over and over. The most she succeeded in doing was stressing herself out and becoming fatigued... all while accomplishing none of her goals!

Eventually, she gave up.

Ose glowered at the Goblins down below before turning and storming out of the terrarium.

"Could it be? Have I become utterly powerless?" Ose wondered. "I'm still a Baron. I can sense magical energy within myself. But why can't I tap into it? Have my powers fused with Abby's? Have they rejected one another and now I need to do something else to activate them?"

The former Emperor of Infiltration pondered a great many possibilities. She thought of this and that, her mind wandering as she guessed randomly and not-so-randomly at times.

"Even if I have no powers, at least I'm alive. I'm still a genius among my peers, and not even a lack of powers will change that. But it is rather disappointing to think I've become somewhat _ordinary_."

Ose returned back to her original destination. She entered a secret room that Bael and Abby had never visited, one that was hidden behind a retractable wall somewhere on the northern end of the Garden of Eden's 59th level. Considering how massive the Garden of Eden was, and the fact that Abby and Bael weren't exactly the most inquisitive people, with Bael being more lazy than anything else, they had not explored even five percent of the Garden's interior.

Thus, not only did they not know about this location, but they did not know of all Ose's other hidden places, either. Ose had prepared this secret base over 100,000 years. Everything in the Garden of Eden served a purpose...

The wall slid open. A new room revealed itself, one that had a horde of monitors arranged in a giant circle, all of them attached to the walls, and a central control console to interface with these monitors. The room was not too dissimilar to Jason's Spynet Sphere in appearance or purpose...

Ose stepped inside, where ADAM was already waiting for her.

"**Master Ose.**" ADAM said, the robot's tone as emotionless as ever. "**I have brought the item.**"

ADAM held up its palm, and in its grasp, there was a black orb made of demonstone. Ose's eyebrows raised slightly, and a smile played upon her face.

"Hehe, ruling the First Hell for a while certainly had its perks." Ose chuckled to herself. "It's a good thing I always come prepared..."

She walked over and took the small demonstone orb out of ADAM's grasp. Ose held it for a moment and sighed nostalgically. Then she pressed her palm on the top of the orb and carefully rotated the top clockwise, and the bottom counter-clockwise.

Without much effort, the orb slowly opened up. Ose popped the top off to reveal that inside the orb, there were four black beads of different sizes. One was extremely small, tinier than a marble. Another was a bit bigger. A third one was even bigger than that, and the fourth was quite large, easily three times the size of an average demon's eyeball.

These were Soul Orbs. Each one contained a certain number of souls within them. They were part of Ose's long term revival plan in the event she died. She had pilfered them from Mephisto through a series of cleverly engineered distractions several years earlier, well before Operation Stormbringer.

"Hahaha..." Ose chuckled to herself. "Looks like I won't be needing the first two. Thanks to Abby, I already possess the power of a Baron. I might even be able to pick a useful Grunt and uplift them to Baron if they serve my purposes well. But for now, there's no reason to hesitate. I can become an Emperor again!"

Ose immediately took the third soul orb and popped it in her mouth. It was quite large, and she could only swallow it with great difficulty. The process of it rolling down her throat into her belly was anything but pleasant, but she still relented. If she still possessed her original electrical abilities, she could have easily deadened her gag reflex or turned off her pain centers to make the swallowing process easier, but now she could only grimace and forcibly bear the pain.

_BOOM!!!_

Ose's aura erupted with majesty! In a single instant, she burst with power and leaped across the chasm separating Barons and Dukes, becoming a powerhouse once again!

Ose had to take a minute to steady herself. Once she was completely sure she was ready, she picked up the biggest soul orb and stared at it with a hint of worry.

"Imagine... imagine if the great Ose died here, all alone, choking to death on a damned Soul Orb. I'd rather the whole facility burned to ash, along with me, so nobody would ever hear such a shameful tale!"

Her pride was part of her bones. Ose couldn't bear the thought of being humiliated, so she forced herself to shove the absolutely massive Soul Orb into her mouth, gagging and choking as she slowly, painfully, and carefully forced it down her throat.

Ose's eyes watered. Snot spewed from her nose. For ten long seconds, her face turned blue. She really thought this might be the end of her story!

But, by massaging her throat and carefully pinching certain areas, she painfully forced the Soul Orb down, down, down into the depths of her gullet. Once it reached a certain point, the orb rolled the rest of the way smoothly and entered her stomach. Ose coughed and gagged, tears pouring from her eyes as she pounded her chest. If any other demon were to walk in and see her, she might die of shame right then and there!

At once, her aura blasted into the heavens once again. Ose stood up and roared at the sky as she felt her body firmly tear down the barrier of Demon Emperor, all thanks to the power of a million condensed human souls.

She had done it!

She had not only returned to the living, but had become an Emperor again!

From this point on, Ose knew she would be able to continue writing the epic tale of her life! The universe's mysteries awaited her!

Ose almost wanted to cry tears of happiness! She wasn't the dumb blubbering baby type, but even so, her raw emotions were difficult to suppress. Strangely, she thought these were the sorts of emotions only Abby would possess, but given the circumstances, it was only fair Ose would be so overwhelmed with emotions. It wasn't as if it was impossible for her to let out a good cry or anything, she simply had never done it in her entire life.

Ose slumped to the floor. She breathed heavily, then chuckled darkly under her breath.

"An Emperor again. Hehehe... the universe is being too kind to me, truly. my fortune is rising all the time. I don't even have any damned Wordsmiths to get in my way anymore. They were the only ones who could oppose me."

Ose's eyes flashed with an ominous, bloody light. She swept her hair back with her hands, then shakily stood up with ADAM's assistance.

"Put the other two Soul Orbs back, and only come back after an hour or so." Ose ordered. "I need time to think."

"**Yes, Master Ose.**" ADAM said obediently. The robot took the demonstone orb away, sealing it up as the machine left.

The room fell silent. Ose walked over to the central control matrix, then stretched out her hands.

Several long seconds passed.

Ose frowned. She tried to activate the controls with her electrokinesis, as she had a million times before, but nothing happened.

"Still?" Ose muttered. "I thought maybe becoming an Emperor would return my powers. Don't tell me I've truly become powerless! That would be a laughable tragedy to say the least. A complete waste of my talents!"

Once again, she tried tapping into Abby's abilities. But this, too, proved a flop.

Ose gnashed her teeth.

"I have magic! I have powers! I sense them! Why can't I activate them? What the hell are my abilities? Don't tell me I have some new power I don't know about?"

When demons evolved from Imps to Grunts, their 'nature' became known. The title of 'Grunt' typically revealed the initial form of what powers a demon would later wield. Upon becoming a Lord, every demon would know for sure what their unique abilities entailed. Some were like Yardrat, who started as a Warper and rapidly became an Emperor and a Deity who could manipulate the most mind-blowing long-range portals ever, along with space itself.

Beelzebub had started off life as a Burner, which obviously foretold his future evolution into a master of flames.

But, at the same time, not all demonic evolutions made sense. Abby was a succubus, but also an Oracle. Then when she became a Lord and a Baron, she learned she could manipulate emotions... this seemed to be a mixture of her succubus physiology and her Oracle powers.

And what about demons like Orias? He was a unique sort of demon who could use gems and other rare rocks to empower his body and gain new abilities temporarily. But unlike many demons, he had skipped straight from Imp to Baron due to the effect of the ancient dragon, Leviathan. Similar things were true for Satan, Belial, and other members of the ancient demons who had been directly elevated by Leviathan.

Even then, there were strange cases. Ose herself had been born an imp, then evolved into a 'demon child'. She had no powers, and just looked like a little girl. Demons like her were quite rare, but not unheard of. Beelzebub's former servant, Rosalia, was also a 'normal' demon, even as a Grunt.

Now, as Ose pondered the strangeness of her powers, she became more and more perplexed.

She and Abby had very different evolutionary trajectories. Ose felt that her powers now must be a fusion of the two, or a side-branch, but no matter what she tried, she couldn't come up with anything to explain her situation.

"Even if I have no powers, I still have the strength and physique of an Emperor." Ose said to herself. "I can still fight human war machines with my body alone. I'm still a faster thinker than when I was a Baron. I barely need any sleep, don't need food or water, and I'm immortal. It's not like my becoming an Emperor was a total waste!"

But these words merely served to soothe her ego. In truth, she was quite pissed. It felt unconscionable that she, the legendary Ose, was powerless now. It felt like the universe had slapped her across the face. She had always been a genius among her peers. Falling down to the level of an 'ordinary' Emperor ruffled the feathers of her ego.

Ose paced back and forth for a while. She tried all sorts of things, but time after time, she failed to uncover any new abilities.

Steam metaphorically spewed from Ose's ears. She squeezed her eyes shut and gritted her teeth. She widened her stance and flexed her arms, balling her hands into fists and she concentrated all her thoughts.

_Come on! There must be something! There must be!!_ Ose thought.

Ose pressed her fingertips against the side of her head. She thought, she thought, and she thought some more. Her face turned even redder than its normal hue as she started to shake with desperation.

_I... I can feel something!_ Ose grunted. _It's almost like-_

At that moment, the door to her Observation Chamber opened and ADAM strolled inside. "**Master Ose. I have returned-**" ADAM said, only to pause mid-sentence. "**...Master Ose?**"

Ose groaned. She opened her eyes to look at ADAM, but when she did, she suddenly felt extremely dizzy. Furthermore, it felt as if she were seeing double! There appeared to be two ADAMs staring back at her.

"What is it?!" Ose snapped. "Why are you bothering me? Huh?"

Ose jumped slightly. When she spoke, it sounded as if she heard herself speaking beside herself, on the right and on the left, but only out of one ear at a time. It was an extremely confusing, and disorienting, sensation.

Ose turned to her left.

"Huh?"

It was the strangest thing. Ose saw Abby in front of her, but only the back of her body. Except it wasn't Abby, it was Ose!

Ose was looking at Ose!

But, at the same time, Ose could also see the wall directly in front of herself as if she were looking past 'herself'.

"Huh??" Ose asked again, her tone more bewildered than before.

Ose turned to look to her right. When she did, she saw 'herself' turning around, and now she was looking at the back of her head again, as well as the center of the Observation Chamber.

Ose staggered slightly. She felt dizzy and confused. Her eyes blinked several times.

"What... what's going on with me?" Ose muttered. "Why am I seeing two... why am I hearing two of me?"

"**Master Ose.**" ADAM said. "**There are... currently two versions of you in this room.**"

"WHAT?!" Ose exclaimed, turning to look at ADAM once again. This time, she carefully focused her mind. She turned to look at 'herself', but the 'other her' also turned to look at 'herself'. They both made eye contact, and Ose's delirium worsened by another level. If felt as if she were looking into a mirror, one which distorted reality and broke her mind.

It was extremely confusing, disorienting, and it made no sense.

Yet she did it!

"You're me?" Ose asked, as both of her 'selves' spoke simultaneously. "You're a clone? No. You're me. I'm me? What's going on?"

A brief instant passed. Then, an idea came to Ose's mind. She carefully turned to the control system, then activated it using manual controls. There were cameras inside the chamber, so Ose rewound one of the monitors to show the scene of just a few minutes before.

Before her, a miracle happened. While Ose was concentrating, a second version of herself slowly 'slid' out of her body and appeared behind her.  Both of the two Oses were holding the same pose. Both of them then proceeded to mirror each other once ADAM entered the chamber.

For a brief moment, Ose felt slightly lost.

"Cloning...? I can clone myself?" She mused. "But it's so disorienting. I can see through both of my eyes at once. When I think, we share the exact same thoughts. It's like looking right and left at the same time. It feels... impossible."

Several minutes passed. Ose slowly began to experiment. It felt as if she were looking through a kaleidoscope, the way the world bent and shifted when each of her bodies moved. But slowly, as the minutes passed, she rapidly grew more and more proficient with controlling each of her two selves.

"This is... unexpected." Ose mumbled. "But far from bad. I would rather still possess my original abilities, but is this ability not intriguing in and of itself? It's certainly better than nothing!"

After a bit of work, Ose started controlling both bodies independently. One of them started doing squats while the other did jumping jacks. One of them read a book while the other one started reciting the digits of Pi.

"Amazing. Amazing!" Ose exclaimed. "I see. How interesting! I can control the flow of 'data' between us. I can choose how much to pilot and control each of the bodies versus letting them move and think autonomously. I can share our senses equally or focus more on one over the other. There's more nuance to this ability than I expected. I think I've barely begun to scratch the surface!"

Soon, the two Oses faced one another. They started sending slow punches and kicks at one another, then their fighting sped up.

Faster and faster, they started moving and thinking independently, all while still sharing brainwaves. A flurry of kicks and punches started striking one another as the clone tried to overpower the original body, except Ose no longer knew which was the original and which was the clone. 

She didn't care, either!

This was a completely different situation from Jason and Hope Hiro. Unlike the foolish Wordsmith, whose other self was an independent entity, Ose had full control of both her selves. Even if she chose to split her perception directly in half, she could regain control instantly!

"Amazing. Truly incredible!" Ose praised after her fight session. "Even if someone tries to kill me again, I hypothesize they would need to kill both my bodies. It's like I've gained an additional life. And even more amazingly, neither of these two bodies is any weaker than when we were one. We're both Emperors, through and through!"

Soon, her thoughts dipped downward. Ose calmed herself and began to look at the big picture.

"If I can make two of me..."

"Then..."

"Could I create... _more_?"

An evil smile slowly stretched across her face...
