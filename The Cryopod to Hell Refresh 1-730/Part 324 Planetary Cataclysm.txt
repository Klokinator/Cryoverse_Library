Part 324: Planetary Cataclysm

Ose cackles maniacally while spinning the ball-and-chain Mjolnir around herself. Unlike Bael, who only ever used Camael's personal weapon as a simple smashing device, Ose wields it with a level of adeptness he could never achieve. She uses her new body's strength and her soul's speed to spin the flail around herself more than fifty times per second. Mjolnir spins so rapidly that it becomes a blur of movement on both her right and left, almost like a wildly oversized nunchaku.

While she might only wield the power of a Duke's body, which also constrains the power of her soul and magic to a Duke's level, that does not limit Ose in the way it would most other demons. Bael's body alone, an invincible war machine that no Demon Emperor would ever want to fight, becomes even more frightening when combined with Ose's lightning powers.

"All of you, stay out of my way!" Ose roars. "This Cherubiim is MINE!"

An instant later, Ose vanishes as she activates her signature Lightning Dash, moving herself in a straight line at the speed of light.

Only Michael, the Archangel with the fastest eyes, possesses the ability to barely follow her movements. He immediately seizes control of the Cherubiim's body, while his brother and sister resume controlling the primordial phantasms.

A battle of epic scale bursts into existence.

While the other Emperors stand and stare in dumbfounded silence, Ose runs laps around the battlefield. Her body appears here and there, flickering in and out of reality with such speed and short intervals that by the time any spectator manages to observe her in one position, she's already vanished and reappeared in three other places.

_BOOM-BOOM-BOOM!!_

Mjolnir rips apart dozens of primordial entities with singular blows. Much like when Ose used her Lightspeed Punch to instantly defeat Monster King Kar, she uses the strength in her newly acquired body and her superior speed to accelerate Mjolnir's attack speed, and therefore its first-strike power, to absurd levels.

A ten ton object, when accelerated to nearly the speed of light, becomes a railgun of unfathomable power.

In one second, Jörmungandr's spiritual body detonates like a bomb, along with a dozen other primordial lifeforms. Two seconds later, another fifteen of the Cherubiim's seemingly unstoppable juggernauts perish as well, dying off at alarming rates.

Thankfully, with the other Emperors hesitant to join the battle, both out of a need to preserve their own lives, as well as due to Ose's proclamation, the Cherubiim manages to devote its sole attention to battling Ose.

Michael's six arms once again wield swords. While wildly inferior to the power of Mjolnir, the spectral weapons do afford him a minor degree of protection. In between attacking the Cherubiim's summoned allies, Ose also revolves around the Cherubiim at dizzying speeds, flinging Big Bonk at the Archangels' flanks and forcing Michael to deflect her divine weapon to the best of his ability.

_Ting-ting-ting!_

Michael's blades break one after the other, forcing him to re-summon them while still frantically fighting Ose off with all his might.

Ose's body spirals around the Cherubiim, leaving dozens of afterimages. She not only runs circles on the ground, but in the air and over the Cherubiim's head as well, all while kicking, punching, and smiting the primordial Archangels with Camael's artifact hammer.

"Having fun?" Ose laughs, her voice coming from a thousand different positions around the Cherubiim at the same time. "I certainly am! I've never enjoyed such unfathomable power! Even while only a mere Duke, my powers are stronger than any Emperor, or even the so-called Demon Deity! I am the mightiest demon in existence!"

While Ose taunts her opponents, she also whittles down their allied summons, breaking their spectral bodies and returning them to the same unbreakable ghostly masks as before.

However, unbeknownst to her, this also reduces the cognitive load on Raphael and Uriel's minds. Once the number of Primordial phantasms drops below 500, Raphael sends his sister a telepathic message.

_Sister. Take control of all the remaining summons. Verily, I shalt assist Michael directly._

Uriel offers no protest. _Do as thou must, Raphael. Teach the fallen sinner Ose the meaning of an Archangel's power._

_Aye._

Ose continues striking the Cherubiim hundreds of times per minute, shattering the landscape and turning Hell Harbor's surface to rubble. Then, from out of nowhere, a blinding pain strikes her soul, causing her to lose focus, trip over her own feet, and go hurtling off into the distance.

**BOOM!**

Ose flies into a mountain at a speed exceeding Mach Ten, blasting tens of thousands of tons of rock and dirt into the horizon and up into space, where over the next several hours they will rain down upon the continents, reaping untold numbers of demonic lives.

"Aaaargh!"

Ose screams in pain as Raphael unleashes torture on her soul, ravaging her so badly that she becomes a convulsing husk of a demon, laying in her makeshift mountain burial site while experiencing endless agonies the world can only imagine.

With Ose temporarily incapacitated, Michael's stress levels plummet. The Cherubiim's body visibly sags as Michael feels a mountain fall from his shoulders. All three Archangels collapse into the dirt as their unified body experiences terrible exhaustion.

_Ugh... brother... I strained my Omni-Perception to its limits. Frankly... I cannot maintain it for much longer. My soul is too weak... if only I still had a body._

Raphael mentally nods. _I have struck Emperor Ose with a Soul Debilitation attack. Since her defenses weren't up, it dealt her severe damage... but it will not kill her. She will return soon. Before then, we must come to a decision._

Michael and Uriel both become more alert.

_A decision regarding what?_ Uriel asks.

_What **price** are we willing to pay to ensure more dead Emperors?_ Raphael asks, coyly glancing at the demons a few miles in the distance.

Surprisingly, neither Uriel nor Michael jump at the opportunity.

_We can always return to fight another day._ Uriel answers. _If we die here, our revenge shalt almost certainly go unfinished._

_The Wordsmith will be able to assist us, next time._ Michael adds. _We decided to attack today because of the element of surprise. We have already slain several Emperors. I do not believe continuing to fight while our Cherubiim powers are nearly exhausted is a wise idea._

Raphael frowns. However, despite his desire to continue killing his hated enemies, he realizes Michael has exhausted his soul, and Uriel will soon follow.

_Ose has become an anomaly among the demons._ Raphael states. _She might be a braggart, but she has reason to boast. We must continue testing the limits of her strength. Right now, we lack precious insight into her full capabilities. I doubt she's unleashed even a fraction of her power upon us. If she attacks humanity, the worst may come to pass!_

Uriel and Michael's emotions both turn sour at the thought of fighting any longer. In less than five minutes, Ose has beaten them a hundred times worse than all the other fights up to this point combined.

_...If we must, then so be it._ Uriel thinks. _Michael cannot fight Ose any longer. Raphael, continue to attack her soul. Slow Ose's speed down and force her to unleash her trump cards. I shalt fight defensively, acting only to sap her strength._

The Archangels discuss their strategy in only a few seconds, the speed of thought allowing them to communicate far more effectively than through any spoken word. Once they finish strategizing, Raphael turns his head toward Ose's makeshift tomb.

_She has awakened._

Ose explodes out of the mountain, a look of pure rage on her face.

"Raphael!! Hahaha, I should have KNOWN you'd try a soul attack, you rancid old wastrel! But you won't catch me off-guard so easily again!"

"**We acknowledge that thou art a deadly foe.**" Raphael says. "**But no matter how mighty thy powers, dost thou thinketh thyself capable of defeating ancient monsters from the primordial era? We have a million times thy battle experience. I have conquered Titan Kings so frightening thy soul would evaporate were they to breathe upon thee. If we fail to slay thee, it will only be due to our decline of power over the eons, not because thou hath become some unstoppable _demigod._**"

"Were those your final words?" Ose hisses. "How SAD! I hoped you'd have something better than excuses to give me, but it seems you already know DEATH is upon you!"

Ose accelerates toward the Cherubiim, but at that moment, all of the phantasmal bodies of the primordial monsters fade away. The last remaining five hundred vanish and revert to their base 'mask' forms, then jump back to the Cherubiim and latch onto its core.

An instant later, they detach, spread apart, and glow with power. They conjure a massive barrier of holy energy that engulfs the Cherubiim, protecting it from all directions. This barrier resonates with all three Archangels' souls, becoming a barrier so powerful that when Ose crashes against it, not even Mjolnir manages to scratch its surface. Hundreds of hexagonal shapes interlock together, strengthening the barrier well beyond anything the current universe has seen in hundreds of thousands of years.

_Ding-ding-ding!_

Ose races around the barrier once again. She smashes Mjolnir against it repeatedly, only to find that each time she does, the kinetic energy of her strikes magically transfers across the whole barrier, wildly diluting her maximum possible damage.

A minute later, after unleashing more than one thousand strikes with her flail, Ose pauses to stare at the barrier with dismay.

"Oh? What's this? You can't beat me, so you've opted to hide inside your barrier like a little turtle?! You're pathetic!"

Her words don't anger the Archangels. Instead, Raphael's head slowly shakes as he offers her a kindly, reprimanding smile.

"**Silly child. Dost thou truly thinketh the Archangel of Wisdom a fool? This barrier is currently a necessity while we recharge our power. Once we have finished, we will emerge stronger than ever. Ah, and should I mention one other thing? We are currently amassing enough primordial energy to obliterate this entire world. The elites of thy species shalt fall here, today. The Wordsmith shalt eliminate the remainder.**"

Ose's greedy smile falters. She stares at the Cherubiim with a look of disbelief.

"You're recharging your batteries? Bullshit. Don't lie to me! You're on your last legs! I'll bet you haven't much time left in that superform!"

"**So confident, thou art.**" Raphael blandly remarks. "**Consider this my form of recognition for thy moderate talents. Thou shalt certainly survive our last attack... but what of the other Emperors, hmm? The Legions of Hell won't inspire much terror if thou art the only Emperor left alive... heh heh heh...**"

To emphasize his words, Raphael lifts a palm to show a slowly growing orb of energy spiraling and amassing power.

"**We never did get to unleash the Primordial Sphere of Holy Devastation. T'would be a waste to... leave it be.**"

The other Emperors still bold enough to watch the Cherubiim's battle turn pale with fright. They scurry toward the portal left by Yardrat, one that has remained open for him to observe the battle, and begin piling through.

"Aw, HELL no! No way am I stickin' around here!" Bael yells.

"I'd rather not die unless necessary." Crow comments.

"My Hell has already lost three Emperors. Let's not make myself the fourth," Kristoff adds.

Mephisto and his summons take their leave, as do all the other Emperors, Dukes, and Barons present. Mara even manages to grab Emperors Guura and Melody and drag them through the portal before Yardrat seals it shut.

With all the other Emperors gone, Ose appears a bit more mollified.

"Hehe... so this is your plan, Raphael? You know damn well you can't kill me in Bael's body. Even if you blow up this entire world, the Volgrim Warpgate is made from Living Moldanium. I can still travel to it and leave. You won't even be able to scratch it. What's your real goal? Are you just trying to piss me off?"

"**Thou mayeth care little about the other demons, but no Empire can stand if it loses its soldiers.**" Raphael says, as the orb grows bigger in his palm. "**Think too of the symbolism of my actions. Art thou such a dullard, Ose? If I destroy Hell Harbor... what will demonkind think of thy rule? Will they respect and fear thy eminence? Or will they look at thee as the disgrace who allowed the obliteration of their capitol world? I cannot say for certain what will be the case, but I do know... the results shalt certainly prove _interesting._**"

The more Raphael talks, the darker Ose's expression turns. She curls her lips into a snarl as her body begins to tremble with hatred.

"...Fine. You want to piss me off, old man? You think you can handle my full power? Let's test that hypothesis. I'll show you what I can _really_ do when I take off the kid gloves."

Ose closes her eyes. Her body begins to shift and morph and blood-red demonic light coalesces within her chest.

"Grr... GRR! GRAAAH! ME CRUSH! ME SMASH!"

Ose's figure balloons into a familiar specter of death, that of the Balrog. Once Bael's mightiest transformation, a weapon that could cow any Emperor into silence with the mere mention of it, it now belongs to Ose as her personal plaything.

Her arms and legs double in thickness. Her horns bend forward and enlarge, and her head turns into a hideous, flame-spewing weapon of mass destruction.

"SMASH! **SMAAAASH!!**"

Ose screams to the heavens, turning the Archangel's expressions ugly when they confirm she does, indeed, possess all of Bael's signature abilities in addition to her own.

"**How fearsome. Luckily, in that form, thou art truly a simpleton imbecile. This old man played thee for-**"

Before Raphael can finish his sentence, the Balrog snaps her head in his direction and smiles. "GRAAH! ME SMASH! ME... me... hahaha. Who are you calling an imbecile, Raphael? Did you truly think I lost my mind when I mutated? No. I have full control of the Balrog. And that's not _all_, either."

Ose stretches to her full height of nearly 20 feet tall. Despite looming over other demons, she still appears utterly insignificant before the mighty Cherubiim.

Thunder crackles around her figure. Ose inhales deeply and performs a series of complicated hand gestures, the awkwardness of her huge Balrog claws posing no barrier to her abilities. Half a minute later, she conjures a second layer of power onto her body, startling Uriel.

_That form! Brother! Ose has somehow called down her Aspect of the Thunder God to empower the Balrog!_

Lightning crackles off Ose's body, lashing out at the wasteland around her with angry intent. She revels in this world-shaking power and laughs.

"Hahaha! As strong as I am now, Raphael, how frightened must you be when you realize I still only possess the power of a Duke? Can you imagine this fearsome might if I possessed the baseline power of an Emperor? Worry not. After I crush you, I will take all the souls necessary for my ascension from the River Styx. I will complete my evolution into a monster capable of singlehandedly killing the old angel civilization. Were you to put me as I am now back in the Ancient Era, the War of Heaven would have ended in less than an hour."

Raphael gazes at the thunder-attuned Balrog with a deep sense of unease. No longer do any smart quips come to mind as he truly comprehends the power his opponent possesses.

_Ose is only a Duke. Already, she possesses a 'low Primordial' level of power. How terrifying. I thought the primordial energy had vanished from this universe, but it seems our enemy hath uncovered a method to mimic the old powers._

Ose cracks her neck, then begins spinning Mjolnir around her body at hurricane-level speeds.

_Swish-swish-swish!_

As the weapon accelerates, Ose leaps skyward, using her lightning power to fly into the upper atmosphere without using the Balrog's wings.

"Raphael! When you greet your brothers and sisters in the afterlife, TELL THEM WHO SENT YOU!"

A sense of fatal danger swallows the Archangel's minds.

Raphael swallows heavily, despite his phantasmal body's lack of a true digestive system.

"**Oh, no.**"

From the sky above, a bolt of lightning formed from pure rage and demonic power descends like the Hammer of God.

Ose hurtles herself and Mjolnir downward at relativistic speeds. She rams into the primordial barrier protecting the Archangels, shatters it, and continues crashing into the Cherubiim's main body.

An atomic blast wave explodes from the impact point, leveling whatever mountains might have survived all the earlier bombardments, or whichever ones may have been created by rubble falling from the atmosphere.

Her impact sends a tsunami of rock and dirt in all directions for hundreds of miles, a tsunami that ultimately will kill off millions of demons and rock the entire world of Hell Harbor, scarring it beyond belief for millennia to come.

Ose smashes into the Archangels, driving every last bit of her power into the impact to ensure their swift and immediate death.

The aftershocks of her attack not only blow away all the clouds on the horizon, but also get picked up on every Volgrim listening post in the system. Multiple Class III emergency signals and energy detonation warnings travel as far as Volgarius, where the Founders will certainly hear of the battle and its aftereffects directly.

Moments later, as the shockwave continues to radiate outward, the Balrog bursts out of the impact zone and sweeps her eyes around, looking for the Cherubiim.

A minute passes. A second minute. A third.

Ose tears apart the debris, but no matter where she looks, she fails to find her prey. 

The Balrog roars with rage. "Raphael!! Where are you?! Where are you hiding?! Show yourself!"

Still, nobody answers her call. As she tries to figure out if her opponent has disappeared or if she obliterated the Cherubiim's entire existence, a portal opens up not far away, and Yardrat steps through.

"The Archangel fled." Yardrat says. "Raphael tricked you."

Ose whirls to face him. "What? NO! That's not possible! I crushed him into dust! How could he have escaped?!"

Yardrat remains unmoved, despite her intimidating presence and the lightning-element Balrog power she wields.

"Isn't it obvious? Raphael swapped out the Cherubiim's body with an illusion shortly before impact. He teleported himself and the others somewhere safe. But... I doubt they will return."

Ose breathes heavily. She gasps for breath, so overpowering is her rage. Despite her claims to the contrary, she doesn't possess full control of the Balrog, and its predatory instincts muddle her mind.

"He... he tricked me?! No, that can't be! You're lying!"

Yardrat starts to say something, but pauses. He and Ose both lift their heads skyward, where a tiny speck levitates downward from the void.

Confessor Vulpanix, having witnessed the entire battle psionically, crosses her arms behind her back and drops down into Hell Harbor's upper atmosphere.

[The Archangels escaped.] She says plainly, her usual anger nowhere to be seen. [Ose. You have much to explain about what has transpired here, today. No longer is this a simple incident of negligence. You did not inform the Overlords of these Archangels' existences. The appearance of a Founder-Class Threat has changed the balance of power.]

Ose inhales and exhales fire and lightning through her nostrils.

"_Confessor Vulpanix._ You picked a BAD time to show up. I might have feared you before, but NO longer! Don't you start demanding anything from me, or I'll rip you and all your little friends to SHREDS!"

Vulpanix cocks her head slightly. Her six eyes narrow as she examines Ose's monstrous figure.

[That would be inadvisable. Perhaps you no longer have any reason to fear me... but what of the Five Executors? Or perhaps even... Founder Dosena herself? I trust you will cool that attitude of yours.]

Ose's rage deflates like a balloon. For once, even the Balrog's overpowering instincts don't manage to strangle the chill that descends down her spine.

She grits her teeth and lowers her voice.

"...Yes. Perhaps I do owe you an... explanation."

[Good. Then get on with it.]
