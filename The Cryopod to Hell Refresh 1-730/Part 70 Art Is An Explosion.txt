Beelzebub closes his eyes and inhales. Suddenly, the oxygen in the room sucks toward him. The change in air pressure makes my ears pop. I stand firm as the wind enters his body.

Then, all goes still.

He sucked the room bone dry.

I glance up and meet the bloodskin's gaze. Beelzebub's body trembles as he holds in the air.

I can last a while; he can't. The fool is still new to using his powers.

Finally, Beelzebub exhales. A violent maelstrom of oxygen bursts from every pore on his skin, knocking me back a foot or two. I stabilize myself with magnetic control over my metal boots and gauntlets, only to see a wall of flame rushing toward me.

Unit Two jumps in front of Beelzebub's attack. She holds up her hand to summon a wall of light, blocking the flames with ease.

Beelzebub narrows his eyes. "Light Manipulation. Since you're a wielder of Holy Energy, I should have expected as much. I'm surprised you can use it while possessing Barbatos's body."

I stand up straight. "In a way, being devoured by this bloodskin was the best thing that ever happened to me. I possess all of Barbatos's magic, all of mine, and also..."

I smile. _Beelzebub doesn't have to know about Artorias._

The bloodskin trapped in my mind appears as a mental image. **What's the matter, Uriel? Scared that if he knows too much, this little Duke will beat you? You are a coward, after all.**

"Shut up," I murmur. "Beelzebub, thy manipulation of fire is quite impressive. I witnessed thy battle against the Black Queen. Thou must feel confident about thy abilities if ye think defeating me will be a simple task."

Beelzebub smiles. In his hand, another fireball forms. "I'm only feeling you out. I've yet to get serious."

He shoots the ball at me. The superheated flame rushes at my face. I hold my hand up, freezing it in midair.

"Thou are far from being the only one who can control the power of fire, little bloodskin."

"What?!" Beelzebub's smile dissipates. "You, too? But you're an angel! Fire belongs to demonkind!" 

I smirk. "When I was an Archangel, my official title was the Archangel of Protection. However, I also had a nickname: _The Flame of God._ Beautiful, isn't it?"

I clamp my hand shut, and the burning, red ball dissipates. Beelzebub glares at me. "You must think neutralizing my primary power will end me."

"Not especially, but _this_ will." I raise my hand, and a hundred knives tear from the demon bodies lying around us. They sail at Beelzebub and-

He disappears. 

The knives crash together and clatter to the ground uselessly.

I quickly scan my surroundings. 

_Where did he go?_

Beelzebub reappears a foot away with a fireball lit in his hand. I jerk my head toward him just in time for the demon to fling it against my chest, exploding it with such power that it sends me flying back. Before I can hit the wall, Unit Two catches me and thumps me onto my feet.

I blink in surprise. "Teleportation? I've never seen thou wield such a power."

The fires from Beelzebub's initial attack earlier continue to burn the bodies of his deceased comrades. He smiles. "Demons gain new abilities when we evolve, stupid Archangel. There's a lot about me you have yet to discover."

The moment he finishes speaking, a light as bright as the sun erupts from his body.

My brain screams in alarm. "GAAAAUGH!" 

Every nerve and receptor howls, spins, and tumbles around. Stars dance in my vision. I claw at my eyes futilely, but no matter what I do, I can't see a thing!

Beelzebub gasps. "Confound you, damn winged freak! How can you still see?!"

Despite the burning agony in my eyes, I smile. _Unit Two has no eyes._

A scuffle breaks out. Something heavy hits the ground. A wave of heat washes over my legs. Beelzebub laughs. "Hahaha! This little false angel is no match for me!"

A moment later, my connection to Unit Two severs. She dissipates into the ether.

My vision returns, though it's blurry like glass during a snowstorm. I can barely make anything out. 

A hazy figure stands amidst a field of flames. "Your toy fell without you to protect it. Now you only have the one left."

"Oh, Beelzebub. How little ye know." 

I make a motion with my hands and Unit Two reforms beside me. "Be more careful this time. He's a crafty one."

She nods, and Beelzebub takes a step back. "You rebuilt it?!"

"My Constructs are creations of magic. So long as I possess spiritual energy, I can rebuild them over and over." I gesture to Unit One. "Hand me the Warper. Both of thee, work together to take out this trash. After my vision returns, so will I."

Artorias speaks in my mind. **You will fall. I've seen this raw strength only once before in Satan. You underestimate Beelzebub's power. He will overwhelm you.**

"Unlikely. One Duke cannot hold a candle to the might of an Archangel. After I slaughter him, I will continue to Hell Harbor and finish off the rest."

Artorias purses his lips. **We shall see.**
