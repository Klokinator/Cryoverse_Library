Part 257: Daisy's Fate

Belial finds herself alone in the operating room with all three Shades. She keeps her back to the exit, not allowing them to pass. Even so, she knows that if they really wanted to snatch Phoebe's daughter, they could leave through the window and zip around the hospital's outer wall to attack from three different directions. With Rosalia having taken Daisy away, Belial tries to comfort herself knowing she can fight unhindered now, but the Shade's lack of interest in leaving immediately raises her suspicions. Rather than pursuing Daisy at all costs, they simply watch her leave, then eyeball Belial with sadistic intentions.

_What's going on?_ Belial thinks. _I thought Daisy was their target. Could it be? Was all of this a ruse to get me alone? Is that blasted Shadow Emperor trying to turn me into one of his Shades again? Was I his true target after all?_

Despite Belial's worries, her mouth curls into a confident smirk, and she straightens her posture. "There. Now that I've lessened my burden, the rest of you will be child's play. What's the matter? Won't you attack? Or are Yama's whores afraid that this sad, weakened Emperor will be too much for them to handle?"

"Taunt us all you like," The leader, Malice, sneers. "It makes no difference. We'll get that brat one way or another. But just think of how greatly Master Yama will reward us if we give you to him, all bound and gagged for his... _pleasure._"

Greed claps her shadowy hands together and laughs wickedly. "That's right! All those times you rebuffed his generous advances, insulted and mocked him... Master Yama will get his revenge! Once he has you in his clutches, he'll perform the 'Master's Rites' and turn you into part of his harem! And then, stupid bitch, you'll know what it means to feel true, undeserved pleasure! Master Yama is so generous that even in spite of your lies about him, he'll still turn you into a real woman!"

Belial's expression doesn't change in the slightest. "There was only one demon I ever loved. He's dead, now. I have no interest in Yama's lascivious desires. As a fellow Emperor... if he tries to sully my mind, I'll teach him a lesson he won't ever forget!"

The Second Emperor doesn't hold back. Immediately after speaking, she charges at the Shades, her bloodlust igniting like never before. Normally, she wouldn't feel such an intense desire to fight, but the attack on Daisy touched her reverse scale, pissing her off in a way that few have previously achieved.

She closes the gap instantly, and three of her four arms transform into hammers, looking to club the Shades into shadow-pulp. At the same time, Belial uses her fourth arm to cast a basic mana wave, momentarily trapping Malice in place.

"Shit!" Malice curses, as she tries to block the hammer. Greed and Demise both scatter into smoke, easily evading the Second Emperor's onslaught, but due to her multi-pronged assault, they can't determine her true target until it's too late.

_Boom!_

Belial smashes her hammer-fist into Malice's head, sending the Shade flying backward, where she crashes against the wall and explodes into smoke. She reforms herself a moment later, taking the vague form of a long-haired human woman. She lays on the ground while clutching her head and moaning.

"S-sneaky little... aaaoo..."

Greed spots her injured leader. "Ah! Belial, you scummy cunt! So she was your target!"

Belial lunges at Malice a second time, once again conjuring another mana field around the Shade to hold her in place. "Hahaha! Revenge is best served cold!"

Just before Belial can batter Malice's already badly-injured body, Demise rushes at her from behind. The huge shadow-orc roars in rage and raises her fists overhead.

"**DIE!**"

Belial, sensing the enraged orc, cancels her attack and dives to the side, narrowly evading Demise's downward-smash. The strongest Shade misses by mere inches, mostly due to Belial's speed and dexterity. However, thanks to her shadow-bending abilities, Demise doesn't even stagger for a moment before changing her trajectory and lunging toward Belial again.

Five times, Belial and Demise trade blows. Each one results in Demise's loss, frustrating the shadow-orc more and more. She howls at Belial and enters a berserk rage, then lunges forward. When Belial dodges, the orc punches the wall behind her, blasting it to smithereens and scattering the concrete wall as if it were made of fiberboard. The concrete sprays outward, revealing the adjacent hospital room to Belial and the other Shades.

"Damn!" Belial grunts, glancing at Demise for a split-second before looking into the room she just revealed. The succubus's worst fears, that these blasted Shades might draw innocent bystanders into their assault, finally comes true.

The Second Emperor instantly surveys the revealed room, expecting to see a few startled soldiers meeting her gaze with curiosity and alarm. Instead, her pupils shrink as she instead observes a bloodbath. Inside the adjacent room, two dead soldiers lie in their beds while blood coats the walls. Their ripped-apart chests tell a tale of a sudden, violent attack, as if they had died without knowing what hit them.

"D-devils!" Belial shouts. Her heart lurches into her throat as a horrifying realization strikes her.

_Why did I assume only three Shades came here?! There must have been others! While I've been fighting Malice and her cronies, more Shades attacked the hospital! That means..._

The succubus's expression turns to one of icy rage.

_...Daisy is in danger!_

On the other side of the room, Malice shakily climbs to her feet, aided by Greed. The Shade's leader grins evilly. "Haha. Look at her face. It seems little-miss-Belial figured something out."

Belial turns her head toward the doorway. In the blink of an eye, she dashes toward it, while the Shades spring into action.

"Haha, oh no, you don't!" Greed cackles. "You're not going anywhere! That little brat is ours!"

Six shadow-arms zip out of the darkness, their intent clear: slow down Belial and stop her escape at all costs. Belial barely makes it to the hallway before the Shades grab the back of her neck, her shirt, and all four of her arms. Even so, she manages to glimpse the blood-filled corridor, where bodies of countless dead soldiers and hospital employees lay in pieces, all of them felled by one or more skilled assassins. Not a single person managed to sound the alarm, resulting in their collective extinction.

Belial turns livid. For the past several days, she's worked her fingers to the bone healing the injured, taking care of their needs, and providing them the assistance they need. Now, their cold, vacant eyes stare at the ceiling. Their unwillingness to go out quietly, even in the face of absolute power, burns Belial's blood to the limit.

"Get your fucking hands off me!" Belial screams, whipping around to face the Shades. Her hammer-fists transform into swords, while her eyes turn bright red. She wildly slashes at the Shades, turning their fingers and wrists into smoke-sashimi. Even so, their limbs simply explode into smoke and reform an instant later, while the Shades rush at Belial with greater determination.

"You can't flee!" Malice laughs.

"**The child is ours!**" Demise roars.

A furious melee ensues. Belial struggles with all her strength, only managing to take a few steps down the hall every few seconds, all while hacking the shadow-hands to pieces whenever they grab her. Each advancement comes at the cost of her stamina and endurance. She continuously uses her already-overdrawn mana, tapping into her life force to shove the Shades back. 

It takes more than two minutes before Belial suddenly obtains a lucky opportunity! During one unified attack, she staggers all three Shades with a mana-blast. At that moment, the succubus stomps her foot against the floor and bolts away.

"Don't let her escape!" Malice yells, roaring in rage. "Grab Belial! Stop her!"

The Shades chase the Second Emperor, their superior speed in the darkness allowing them to close the gap. Still, as a Demon Emperor, Belial isn't some nameless chump. She makes it to the central hallway, where the southern side opens into the hospital's entrance, while the west, north, and east hallways all branch off into different wings for specialized treatment.

Belial grits her teeth. She can't help but feel despair engulfing her heart as she spots even more bodies piled atop one another, the lifeless heaps of death and slaughter echoing the atmosphere of ancient-Earth mass graves.

As soon as Belial reaches the central corridor, she hears a woman's scream in the distance, down the eastern corridor. Her heart stops.

"Rosalia?! No, no, no!!"

Before she can reach the eastern branch's entrance, the Shades catch up. They grab Belial again, frustrating her efforts to reach Daisy.

"Get off me! I'll fucking kill you all!"

Belial whirls to face her pursuers. Her eyes turn bloodshot. Without hesitation, she burns even more of her life force. Her head shifts its form as she transforms herself into a Banshee. Mimicking that monster's biology, her throat's structure changes. She sucks in a massive breath of air, then screams at the Shades.

"**SCREEEEEE!!**"

A sound-wave with the power of a ten-ton cannonball erupts toward the western corridor, catching all of the Shades off-guard. Knowing that everyone in the west wing has already perished, Belial doesn't see any reason to hold back. She ruthlessly turns that entire side of the hospital to rubble, blasting it apart at its foundations and flinging the Shades backward with the ruthlessness of a true Demon Emperor.

"Aaaaargh!" Malice, Greed, and Demise all scream in unison. The lose their grip on Belial and fly backward, while hundreds of tons of concrete and steel crash onto them from every direction. The hospital's western wing collapses into a pile of rubble, momentarily burying the Shades alive.

Once again, another scream of pain reaches Belial's ears from the eastern wing. She quickly turns to face it, then dashes forward, reverting her body to its original form.

"Daisy, Rosalia! Hold on! I'm coming!!"

Belial crashes through the eastern entry-doors. She rushes down the hallway, toward the furthest end, where Kar's room sits tucked off in a distant corner. As another scream reaches her ears, Belial's heart shakes.

_I'll kill them all! Every last Shade! No mercy!!_

Suddenly, while Belial is only a few hundred feet from the final room, a shadowy figure bolts out of the room. It hits the ground and rolls, then rushes toward Belial. Its figure resembles a hellhound wreathed in shadow, one with a mouth full of razor-sharp fangs.

Belial gnashes her teeth. "Get out of my way, shadow-bitch! You won't stop me-"

Before the succubus can finish her sentence, something incredible happens. The hellhound-Shade freezes mid-movement, as if held back by an invisible chain. 

Before Belial can register what just happened, a second bizarre event occurs. The Hellhound, frozen in mid-jump, falls toward the ground heavily, as if pushed down by a giant, invisible hand.

Once again, a scream of pain erupts in the corridor. This time, Belial pauses and blinks in surprise. The scream she heard earlier came not from Rosalia... but from the hellhound-Shade before her.

"Eeeyah! Help... help me! Please! Please!! She's- eeyahh!!"

The Hellhound shade looks up at Belial, terror in its eyes. The monster digs its claws into the tile, while an invisible force begins to pull it backward against its will. The monster's claws rip up the hospital's white tiling, smearing the blood of dead soldiers across the floor, mixing their essence with the powder used to create the hospital's flooring.

"No! No-no-noooo!!"

The hellhound-Shade screams in fright, trying desperately to hold onto the floor and pull itself forward.

Its efforts prove futile. 

Belial's blood runs cold. She stops and watches as something unseen drags the Shade back into Kar's room. The Shade grabs onto the door's sides for dear life, only for the pulling force to strengthen further, ripping her claws off the side-panels with violent force.

A little girl laughs from within the room. "Haha! You nasty, bad person! Where do you think you're going?! Daddy says bad people need to be punished!"

Stupefied, uncertain, and confused, Belial gazes blankly at the entrance to Kar's room. Inside, the sound of several crisp slaps resound. She finally shakes the fog from her head, runs to the doorway, and peers into the room's interior.

There, she sees the shadow-hellhound pinned to the floor, its limbs splayed out in an X-shape. Daisy towers over the monster and raises her hand, summoning magical power into her palm.

_WHAP! WHAP!_

Daisy spanks the monster's ass, beating it with such savage brutality that Belial can even hear the unmistakable sound of bones breaking.

"Eeeyaah! No, stop, it hurts! I don't like it! I DON'T LIKE IT!!"

The hellhound pitifully screams, begs, and cries, but Daisy doesn't stop her assault. She continues spanking the Shade's rump with enthusiastic gusto, clearly enjoying herself.

"This one is for all the people you've killed! This one is for trying to hurt the fat lady! And this one is for trying to run away from your spanking! You are a very, very bad person!"

Belial gazes vacantly at Daisy. Her thoughts swirl like a typhoon, but nothing she imagined in the minutes following up to this moment could have prepared her for such a drastic shift in circumstances.

"D... Daisy? You're okay?"

The little girl pauses her spanking to glance at Belial. "Oh, Aunt S'matha! You won't believe it! Me and fat lady were coming in here, right? And then this scary shadow-monster tried to eat us! But when I got scared, I pushed out my hands... and boom! It felt really good- the 'boom,' I mean- and then I was like, _whap, whap_! It turns out going 'boom' is lots of fun!"

Belial nods dumbly.

"Going... going 'boom.' Yeah. I guess so."

Belial's vision falls on Rosalia, now standing beside Kar's bed. She, too, gazes at Daisy with vacant eyes.

Rosalia mutters to herself. "This kid... she's a monster. An absolute monster. Didn't know human spawnlings could be so scary... so scary..."

Belial shakes her head helplessly and forces a smile.

_Looks like in the end, she's still daddy's little girl. I used to think the children of Heroes couldn't gain magical powers... but clearly I was wrong. If we make it through this war, I'll have to talk to Phoebe about investigating Daisy's abilities more thoroughly._

With the crisp sounds of a magically-imbued palm meeting a screaming shadow-hellhound's hindquarter echoing through the air, Belial almost doesn't notice the rapid approach of the other three Shades. Luckily, just before they arrive at the entrance, Belial senses their demonic energies and whirls to face them.

Malice, Greed, and Demise stop outside the doorway and look inside, expecting the pain-filled screams to be coming from Belial, Rosalia, or even Daisy. Instead, the glee-filled expressions on their faces freeze as they spot their comrade getting spanked by a child one-third her size.

"What... what the hell?! Vicious! What are you- what's going on?!" Malice shrieks.

"Help me! Please!" Vicious cries, shadowy tears dripping from her eyes. "It hurts so bad, I can't stand it, eeeyah!!"

_Whap! Whap!_

Daisy spanks Vicious two more times, then pauses to look at the other three Shades.

"Ah! The other bad people!"

As the child turns to look at them, all three Shades grow mortified, realizing they greatly underestimated the difficulty of this mission. Malice's jaw drops as she senses a buildup of mana forming behind the child.

"D-drop everything! Scatter! That little brat is a Hero- GUWAAK!"

Malice's words catch in her throat as Daisy stretches out her hand, sending a pulse of telekinetic energy rushing toward her. Before the Shade's leader can react, a powerful force grabs hold of her body and yanks her into the room, its might too great for her to resist.

"No, no, nooo!"

Like an ant trapped in a tornado, Malice can't offer the slightest resistance to Daisy's magic. The child laughs innocently as she throws Malice across Vicious's back, leaving her butt exposed for an equally brutal round of spanking.

Demise, unafraid, raises her fist and charges into the room. "**You can't stop us all!**"

What follows is a pitiful display by the strongest Shade. She, too, ends up thrown on the same pile as Malice and Vicious. Not even the might of the orc-Shade can resist Daisy's unmatched telekinesis.

Daisy turns to grab the last Shade, only to frown.

"...Aww. She got away."

Greed, having seen more than enough of this terrifying child, stays true to her name and flees the scene, abandoning the other three Shades to save her own skin. By the time Daisy realizes, she's too late to capture her.

The child shrugs, then gets to work violently spanking her two newest victims, making them scream and beg for mercy. Each strike contains not only the concentrated anima of telekinesis, but some unknown magical element that ravages the Shades, tearing apart their bodies at the molecular level.

After several minutes, all three Shades faint from the torment, falling comatose as they prove unable to resist Daisy's might.

Already, Belial finds herself growing numb to Daisy's shocking displays of power.

"...Good work, Daisy. Good work. Your uh... your mommy will be proud of you when she finds out what happened."

Daisy plants her foot on Demise's back while striking the pose of a conqueror. She beams a smile filled with sunshine at her aunt and nods.

"Yup! I'm super cool!"

...

Once the euphoria of a victory snatched from the jaws of defeat fades away, Belial, Rosalia, and Daisy all glance outside, at the hallway filled with bodies and blood.

Their high-spirits dampen. A somberness takes over, making them sigh in defeat.

"We won..." Belial mutters. "But can we call this a victory? Everyone is... they're all dead."

Rosalia lowers her eyes in shame. "I was powerless. I couldn't do a thing to help."

"It wasn't your fault," Belial says, sighing. "Many of these soldiers had exosuits that could have protected them. Even so, they died like lambs, ripped apart by wolves."

A sense of bitterness engulfs Belial's heart. The succubus turns her head slightly to look back inside Kar's room at the three comatose Shades.

Her expression hardens.

"...There can only be one punishment for those who commit such an atrocity."

The succubus's right arm transforms into a long, slender bone-blade.

"Daisy. Look away."

The Second Emperor strides toward the fallen Shades. Without any fanfare, she swings her arm thrice.

The sounds of three heads hitting the floor startles Rosalia. She quickly covers Daisy's eyes.

"S-Second Emperor... you..."

Belial gazes at the dead Shades. Her eyes don't contain a hint of regret, nor a wisp of indecisiveness.

"I hate killing. I hate death. I hate violence. But if it means protecting those I love, then even I have a limit to what I'll endure."

"These Shades _crossed that line._"

Belial lifts her head. She gazes at the ceiling as countless thoughts tumble inside her mind.

_Sister. I'm sorry. I have to break our promise._
