Part 48: The Ultimate Battle; The Archdemon Awakens!

[Recommended Listening](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2DFYvwn_xLM)

Arthur and the Balrog cease their battle and turn to gape as a massive, monstrous claw erupts from Polaris's bowels. A hand, the size of a small office building, grabs the ground and lifts.

Awe gives way to horror. Arthur, having never seen such a monstrosity in all his years, begins to shake. Despite his soulless vessel, terror floods his mind.

_Impossible... impossible... what ungodly fiend could ever reach such a height...?!_

Another limb bursts from the ground, and another, and another. Four huge, clawed arms spread out. Fire and thunder arc from every pore of the Archdemon's body. Diablo flings himself out of the ground with the force of a volcano and thrusts himself into the sky. 

His legs, now a mass of one hundred writhing tentacles, beat and batter the landscape around him. Each whip-like tentacle ends in a mouth of razor-sharp teeth, ready to devour beings smaller than the Archdemon in one gulp, transforming them into a puddle of flesh. The Archdemon finishes rising and towers over the specks below him, standing well over five-hundred-feet tall.

Diablo's head, an eyeless aberration, twists and tilts around, as if trying to perceive something unseen. Two massive, curved horns, each over fifty-feet tall, sprout from his skull, as do thousands of spines that cover his body.

"**BAAAEEELLL!**"

The Archdemon bellows with a volume that rattles the eardrums of everyone nearby.

The Balrog, a tiny, insignificant demon compared to his master, howls in delight. "BIG BOSS! YOU ALIVE! ME WORRIED! ME THINK-"

"**SILEEEEENCE!**" The supermassive demon lunges one of its clawed palms down and snatches the tiny Balrog up, hoisting the Duke of Pain high into the sky. "**That human tore me apart, and all you did was watch!! WORTHLESS BASTAAAARD!**"

"BOSS! NO HURT! ME SORRY! ME-"

The Archdemon inhales for a moment, then begins to vomit a fire ten times hotter than what the Balrog spit before, blasting Bael's body with the heat of the sun. Bael writhes and squirms in his master's grip before the temperature makes him pass out and go limp.

Diablo pauses his fiery assault to lift the Duke into the sky. Then, he slams Bael's tiny body into the ground with enough force to shatter the continental plates. Somewhere, on the other side of the planet, an earthquake rips apart several mountains.

Again and again, the Archdemon pounds the Balrog into the dirt. Finally, he flings Bael into the horizon like a baseball. Bael flies over a hundred miles, his body careening helplessly as he sails toward the planet's surface below.

When Bael lands, he crashes into Polaris's crust with the force of a small tactical nuke, blowing away everything in his path and eradicating all life for several miles around.

...

Diablo turns his unbridled rage to the other specks below him. One glowing white form catches his attention.

"**ARTHUUUR!! You won't live to see another day!!**"

The Archdemon's tentacles lash out, spin around, and pound the ground where the Hero-King stands. Arthur instantly raises two fingers to his temples and begins transmitting left and right, up and down, all in an effort to evade the Archdemon's attacks. Diablo's transformation more than rivals that of Gabriel, the legendary Archangel whose ultimate ability would only allow him to fight on par with the Archdemon.

Arthur transmits to the Archdemon's chest and adheres to Diablo's body with the power of Holy Magic, then quickly modifies his Warhammer into a ten-foot spear of light. Arthur uses all of his strength to jam the spear deep into the goliath's chest. Then, he activates a pulse of Holy Energy, surging his mana deep into the demon's body.

"**You have no power here, HERO!! I'm immune to your so-called Holy Magic!**" Three tentacles pounce from the ground and lunge at Arthur, but he increases his Crusader Aura to its maximum level and deflects them without turning back.

Somewhere nearby, Satan trembles in fear. He rushes away from the pit where his mate and the Hero-boy prepare to battle the Black Queen. 

_Damn you, Blaarjiim! I have to fight Diablo for the second time in my life, only now, I have no backup at all! This time, I'm going to die for good!_

Satan comes to a stop and clutches his head. Despite his protests, Blaarjiim's orders force him to obey. Still, he has autonomy over _how_ he will fight back. His eyes follow the Hero-King dashing all around the Archdemon, stabbing and jabbing here and there. 

It's at this moment that a face bubbles up from the ground. "Massster Sssatan! Diablo... he'sss... changed! What manner of devilry isss thisss?"

Satan glances down at the aethereal Duke. "Mephisto! Diablo's Archdemon form means the end-times have arrived. He's unstoppable. It took the combined power of the Archangels, myself, and every demon and angel in Heaven to suppress his power long enough for him to deplete his energy and transform into his base form. After he did, though, he went comatose for a hundred years. If we're lucky, the same thing will happen again."

The Devil swallows. "If."

Mephisto hisses. "Isss he truly that powerful? If ssso, then... we mussst flee! Let him rampage and dessstroy the land! We care little for thisss world!"

"If only it were that simple," Satan snarls. He glances back at Blaarjiim's crater. "The Black Queen has ordered me to fight Diablo. I have no choice!"

A thunderous _boom_ blows away every cloud in the sky. Diablo discharges a concentrated stream of fire at Arthur. It misses and instead lasers into the distance, where it obliterates a mountain and sends millions of tons of debris careening into the atmosphere.

Mephisto glances at Satan, then whirls to face the Archdemon. "Ssslow down Diablo asss long asss you can. I have a ssspecial tool for jussst thisss sssituation. Leave it to me."

Satan eyes the King of the Undead for several moments. "I don't care how many servants you have. He'll erase them all. Far be it for me to order a demon to flee, tail tucked between his legs, but... leave. Take Belial and Bael, and go. There's nothing you can do here."

Mephisto raps his fingers together. "I've been sssaving thisss for agesss, massster Sssatan. Asss your greatessst pupil, I will impresss you. I will earn the posssition of Firssst Emperor with thisss feat. At the leassst, I pray you will ressspect me for defeating Diablo."

Satan eyes the young demon for a few seconds before shaking his head. "I've always regarded you with high esteem, Mephisto. If it weren't for you, we'd never have defeated the humans and angels, all those millennia ago." 

The Devil turns to stare at Diablo's Archdemon form. "Alright. If you think you can defeat him, don't hold back. Unleash everything you have. Show me how you've grown."

He turns his back on the necromancer and runs toward the Archdemon, leaving the Duke of Mist by himself.

Mephisto's gaze hardens. "Fear not, massster Sssatan. After thisss, nobody will quessstion my powersss ever again."

"My bag! I dropped it!" Phoebe panics and turns away from the Archdemon toward the crater where Jason faces off against the corrupted Amelia. "We have to go back! Oh my god! I'm an idiot! I shouldn't have-"

Samantha pats Phoebe's back gently. "Calm down, Phoebe. A bag hardly matters. Look at me. Will you be okay? If anything happens to you-"

"I'm fine, Sammy! But my bag, it's-" Phoebe is interrupted when the firey Archdemon lights up the night like a miniature sun. His dreadful _roar_ of rage tugs at her attention for a moment. "Never mind. You have to help Jason. He's weak. He can't fight the Black Witch, not as he is now!"

"I know. I'm going back. Stay hidden. The battles currently unfolding are beyond your abilities." 

Samantha pauses for a moment, then kisses Phoebe's forehead. Phoebe stares at her in surprise, but the succubus dashes back to the crater without another word.

...

Inside the crater, Blaarjiim licks his lips. "Make your move, Hero."

Jason nods. "If you and Amelia chose the path of evil, then I'll do what I must. You can't have my ability." Jason aims his palm at the shadowy woman, pausing for a second as a rush of emotions floods his mind.

_Amelia... I'm sorry._

"**FIRE!!**"

A wave of holy magic erupts from his palm and races toward the Black Queen. However, it misses at the last moment. Blaarjiim leaps into the air, and the energy surge smashes against the side of the crater behind him. 

Blaarjiim sends a wave of darkness from himself down into the ground, and a second later, hundreds of shadow-lances leap up, stabbing Achilles' armor all over, as well as Jason's arms and legs. The sudden attack stuns the hero as they pierce not bone and flesh, but his mana instead.

Sucking the power from Jason's body, Blaarjiim lands right next to the youth and grabs him by the neck, slamming him against the side of the crater.

"How foolish! You sent away your two allies, and now you haven't the strength to resist!"

Jason tries to raise his arm, but slumps in Blaarjiim's grasp. His energy drains into Blaarjiim's shadow-lances.

Blaarjiim begins punching and pounding Jason, battering the divine armor with all of his strength. Eventually, Achilles's armor and Orion's Belt explode into light, leaving only the Boots of Hermes. Blaarjiim's dark magic tears those off, leaving the Hero defenseless. All Jason wears is his tattered underwear and a torn green T-shirt.

"And now, Hero, you become mine." Blaarjiim presses his hand against Jason's face.

Samantha's voice echoes from the top of the crater. "Not so fast, Witch! I'm still here!"

"Oh?" Blaarjiim immediately diverts his attention, ignores the boy, and drops Jason's limp body to the ground. "So you've returned already? A succubus against the King of Darkness. You should know, I have no qualms about beating a woman senseless. Making the Second Emperor my servant in addition to the First should prove useful."

"I'm nobody's servant," Samantha mutters. She hops into the pit and begins to morph, unleashing her True Succubus form for the second time in an hour.

Blaarjiim crosses his arms and watches as Samantha's body shifts and twists. She grows a spiky tail, leathery wings, and replaces her soft hands with razor-sharp claws. 

"You're foolish to transform in front of me. Luckily for you, I am a principled fighter. Beating you in your weakest state would be unsatisfying. The power flooding my veins aches for a challenge."

Samantha finishes morphing and immediately jumps forward, swinging her clawed hand at Blaarjiim. The Dark God leaps to the side and laughs. "You're so slow that I could dodge you in my sleep! You can't even-"

He stops mid-sentence as Belial's talons pound the side of the crater, shake the ground, and sending a flood of rock flying thanks to her Herculean strength.

Immediately, Blaarjiim's attitude changes. _Belial is slower than me, true, but her power is fearsome. I will end this before she lands a hit on me._

Spears of darkness bolt out from Blaarjiim's aperture and race toward the succubus. Belial dodges them with ease. She darts between the gaps in the spear's attacks and leaps backward, retreating from the dark god's range.

Blaarjiim retracts his dark energy. "You're like a snake, slithering between the gaps in my attacks. I commend your battle prowess, but it won't be enough to save you."

Belial flicks her eyes to Jason, who lies on the ground next to Phoebe's bag, barely conscious. "Yeah, yeah, save it. I've heard more than my fair share of big, bad, evil speeches from goons like you."

Belial rushes toward Blaarjiim. The two of them continue trading blows, and the Second Emperor's succubus form transforms her from a decent opponent to one with lethal power and precision. While Satan and Arthur unleash a hail of attacks on their Archdemon enemy in the distance, Belial's enhanced speed and power drive Blaarjiim back again and again.

Belial swings her claws toward Blaarjiim's head, and even when the dark god dodges, she still draws blood. One of Belial's claws cuts Blaarjiim's chest, spraying blood all over the dirt.

"Gurk! I'll finish you off! As soon as one of my tendrils hits, you'll-!"

"Too bad they wooon't~!" Samantha cackles, cutting him off. She pushes Blaarjiim back against a wall, then slaps him, sending Amelia's corrupted form flying to the side.

"Uwah!" Blaarjiim slams against the crater with a resounding _thud_ and coughs up blood. One of his ribs jams through his chest. "C-curses..."

"I don't like killing," Belial hisses, rage building in her eyes. "But you took my husband from me. A thousand deaths would be too good for you!"

The succubus raises her claws to land the killing blow, but Blaarjiim raises his voice.

"You fool! If you love Satan, then you should be careful! If you kill me, he will die as well!"

Belial hesitates. Her arm lingers in the air for a split-second. "Y-you're lying."

"Try me."

"RAAAH!" She swings her talons at the dark god, but out of nowhere, a blast of fire slams into the side of her head, catching her off-guard.

Belial flicks her gaze around. "Who did that?!"

"I did," Beelzebub replies from atop the crater. 

Belial sweeps her gaze toward him, only to freeze in place.

Beelzebub's right arm wraps around Phoebe's neck. She slumps listlessly in his arms, unable to fight back. "You wouldn't believe who I spotted while I was flying around! Why, you dumped this poor, defenseless human off behind a rock and left her there! How wonderful it is that I showed up to save this insect from all the scary _monsters_ running around!"

The Baron of Flames cackles gleefully. "Hahahaha! I can hardly believe it! The Hero who humiliated me, his fiance, the woman who pounded me to a pulp in Hero City, and the woman who enslaved my master as well! I owe every one of you a blood debt!"

Belial turns white in the face. "Leave Phoebe out of this! She's innocent!"

Blaarjiim sneers. _Foolish woman. Belial forgot about me._

With a quick motion of Amelia's hands, Blaarjiim unleashes the full power of his tendrils, spiking fifty energy-sapping spears of darkness through every point in Belial's body.

_Shik-shik!_

Belial gasps. "Ahh!" 

The succubus stumbles forward, unable to retaliate. "Eugh... you... you..." 

She flops to the ground and writhes for a moment, then goes still as Blaarjiim's magic does its work. Belial's eyes slowly turn toward Blaarjiim. "C-cow... coward..."

"Only a fool turns her back on an enemy," The Dark God replies.

He lashes out and kicks her limp body with brutal force, sending her sprawling over to the other side of the crater, where she lands near Jason. Her succubus form reverts, causing her wings and claws to retract as she falls unconscious.

Beelzebub smirks. He drops Phoebe off the ledge. Her body spins and rolls, bouncing off fifty feet of hardened dirt until she hits the ground with a sickening _crunch_. She lands only a short distance away from Belial, but her badly broken arms and legs reveal her arrival at death's door.

"Three down, one to go," The Baron of Flames says.

Blaarjiim cracks his neck. "Oh, do you wish to fight me as well? I drained all the energy in the Second Emperor's body. I'm ready for action again. I suppose a pipsqueak like you will do for a weak servant. Maybe you could fetch the water for my queen's bath, or serve as a footstool when she's tired."

Fire erupts from every pore on Beelzebub's body. "The Hell I will!!"

"What chance do you have, imbecile? I'm immune to fire. In fact, I'd go so far as to say that I'm your worst opponent!"

Beelzebub raises his palm toward Blaarjiim. "We will see."

---

_I'm pathetic. I'm weak._

_How long have I hovered at the edge of life and death, already? I couldn't beat Satan. I didn't stand a chance against Amelia. Some Hero I turned out to be._

_I thought when Phoebe showed up, everything was going to work out. Seeing her face filled me with life._

My eyes vacantly follow her body falling off a cliff. The woman who cared for me during my paralysis slams into the ground and falls silent.

_The succubus demon girl... she's down too._

_We've lost._

_Amelia said she would reap my soul and turn me into a servant, just as she did with Satan. Maybe I deserve that._

_I failed in every conceivable way one could. I never had a chance in the first place._

_Today is the greatest failure of my life._

My eyes drift to the side, away from Phoebe. It hurts to see her lying in that position. Her arms and legs are bent unnaturally. _Why, Phoebe? Why did you have to come? I didn't want you here. This place... it's awful. You're too kind for the perils of war._

Eventually, my gaze settles on a bag. Ah, it's Phoebe's shoulder-purse. The one she always carries. It seems brighter than usual. Almost as if it's glowing...

_All alone._

Somewhere, in the back of my mind, a voice whispers.

_No, Jason. You are not alone. I have always been here for you. You merely had to listen._

My eyes widen.

_H-Hoarhiim?_
