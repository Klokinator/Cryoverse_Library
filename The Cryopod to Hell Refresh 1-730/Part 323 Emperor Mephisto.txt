Part 323: Emperor Mephisto

With Mephisto's arrival, demonkind's inevitable defeat suddenly reverses, sparking a glimmer of hope in the eyes of every Emperor present. Crow, having lost one of her arms, Yumagi, having long since stopped treating the battle as a game, and Kristoff, having reluctantly accepted his imminent death, all stare in shock at the newcomers.

Mephisto's gigantic Brute roars at the Cherubiim before charging toward it with stiff, jerky motions. As an amalgamation of thousands of lesser human, demon, and monster bodies and souls, the mashed-together undead goliath lacks any sort of concentrated brain matter, but it makes up for the deficit with a body sturdy enough to even withstand the Cherubiim's holy magic.

Michael's head gazes at the incoming attacker with a hint of scorn. "**How pathetic. One last hurrah for the bloodskins. I will end this.**"

All six of the Cherubiim's arms lift into the air and summon building-sized greatswords in their palms. The Cherubiim and its primordial monsters charge into the incoming Brute and engage it in yet another planet-shattering battle, one that sends frightening shockwaves in all directions, constantly battering the Hidden Emperors as they try to withstand the storm.

Not content to merely sit back and watch, Fae joins the fray, flying into the sky to hurl explosive mana-bombs at any primordial summon that gets in her way. At the same time, Mephisto's revived Emperor minions follow along to his tune, running along the ground or flying into the air to assist the Brute as it dull-headedly hammers against the Archangel's defenses.

"Kekeke!" Mephisto cackles. "Behold! The rissse of the next great Necromancer! The future Firssst Emperor of the Ssseven Hellsss!"

Mephisto does not join in on the battle. He hangs back, which only makes sense, given his poor fighting abilities. Unlike the ancient Emperor, Valac, who possessed a soul-reaping scythe and a surprisingly powerful body, Mephisto's talents lay in the finer aspects of the necromantic arts.

Michael's six swords spin like buzzsaws, whirling around the Cherubiim's body at five times the speed of sound. Unlike a physical body, with its limited joint movements, the Cherubiim's 'core' and its summoned limbs act as almost disconnected weapons, able to move freely around the Cherubiim's 'main body' as they please. Without the tendons, bones, and other intricacies of a fleshly body to slow him down, Michael's blindingly fast attacking and reaction speed completely dwarfs that of his opponents.

The Brute tanks all of Michael's attacks directly, making no movement to avoid them. Despite the incredible sharpness of Michael's phantasmal blades, his swords don't manage to cut deeply into Mephisto's summon's flesh, but instead glance off its chest, neck, and head, sometimes chipping away boulder-sized flakes of hardened skin.

_This beast is quite sturdy..._ Raphael comments internally, as he keeps an eye on the battlefield, allowing Michael to focus on the Brute. While Michael controls the Cherubiim's six limbs, his brother and sister each take direct control of half their summoned monsters, sending them to attack and blast back the demons wherever possible.

Before long, Mephisto's triumphant appearance loses some of its spark. He stops laughing to gaze coldly at the scenes taking place.

"Kss... the Cherubiim cannot ssslay my Brute, but neither can my Brute land a killing blow... how troublesssome..."

Emperor Yumagi takes to the air. Having spent a few minutes catching his breath, he heals his worst injuries and hovers into the sky while withdrawing a few cards from the Spiritual Deck attached to his soul.

"I apologize, everyone. I did not take this battle seriously, before. Now, we have paid a heavy price. We will have to work together to launch one last-ditch attack on the Cherubiim if we hope to survive this day with our lives intact."

Crow nods. She breathes heavily while sending a mental transmission back to the demons on her homeworld.

"...I will begin a Ritual of Healing. It will take me a few minutes, but I should be able to regrow my arm and patch up some of my worst wounds. Until I take a trip to the Blood Pits, I'll be hurting quite badly for the foreseeable future. Ca-caw!"

Kristoff groans. "I've broken a few bones. My powers can't heal them... only skin and organ-injuries. I've probably lost half of my strength... but I will try to help."

He shoots a nasty glance at Mephisto.

"But do not think for a _moment_ that I am doing this for _you_, body-stealer. If we survive today, you will pay for stealing my wife away from me!"

Mephisto shoots a casual, utterly dismissive glance at Kristoff. Having reached the rank of Emperor, his arrogance reaches new heights, making him no longer fear the mightiest Demon Emperors as he previously did.

"Kukuku... I am putting her body to good ussse, Krissstoff. Perhapsss you ssshould thank me for sssaving you."

Kristoff doesn't reply. He looks away from Mephisto, mostly to cool his boiling blood pressure.

_...Fenrir, dearest. We had so many plans together. We were going to rule the Seven Hells, with me as your number one. What can I do, now? I detest politics. You, Lupus, and Wolfram have all fallen. I am the Hell of Blood's last living Emperor..._

As Kristoff and Crow take time to patch themselves up, the Cherubiim continues its brutal onslaught. Michael's swords prove less and less effective against the Brute, to the point where Raphael makes a startling observation.

_Brother, beware! This undead behemoth is not so simple! Nay, 'tis learning to adapt to thy attacks. Already, its skin has grown substantially more resilient to slicing and slashing attacks. Switch thy swords out for hammers!_

Michael grimaces. His fighting style, heavily reliant on rapid attacks with light weaponry, does not allow him to nearly as effectively wield such clumsy and oafish weapons as clubs and hammers.

_If my swords art not up to the task, then sister, thou shoulds't taketh over. Thou art a generalist when it comes to weaponry. In the way of the blade, thy skills cannot match up to mine, but with other weapons, thou art far and away my superior._

Uriel nods. _So be it._

While the demons manage to work together to kill off a few dozen of the Cherubiim's unstoppable army, they fail to notice the rapid switching of its six swords to two spiked clubs, two pointed hammers, and two warhammers.

The instant Uriel takes over the Cherubiim's main body, she drastically changes up its fighting style, using Holy Barriers to cut off the Brute's movements, tripping it with awkwardly placed barriers by its feet, and hampering its movements so she can land clean blows.

The Brute swings its fists and roars, but Uriel bats them aside and drives her weapons into its head and chest, fracturing its bones and continuously shaking the planet.

During a brief reprieve after knocking the Brute backward, Uriel gathers her strength into the Cherubiim's arms. Her eyes glow with power as she raises all six of her weapons overhead, aiming to smash its skull into bloody pulp.

At that moment, a Demon Emperor's voice rings out.

"Sneaky Sea Salamander, **Evoke!**"

Emperor Yumagi evokes one of his Golden Aspects. A beam of light rockets out of the card held in his hand, expands in size, and envelops the Brute. Just as Uriel swings her weapons at its head, the Brute teleports from its location and arrives directly behind her.

Uriel's attack misses the mark and instead crashes into the continent, sending earthquakes deep into the crust, where they will become tsunamis on Hell Harbor's opposite side later that day.

At the same time, the Brute spins around and swings its fists at the Cherubiim's exposed flank.

_Boom! BOOM!_

Two powerful strikes finally land on an unprotected area, causing all three Archangels to cry out in pain. Their primordial phantasms flicker and fall silent for a few seconds before resuming their previous actions.

By the time the Brute has launched its second attack, Uriel has already caught up to its new position and resumed her stalwart defense.

She shoots a hateful glance at Emperor Yumagi.

"**BLOODSKIN BASTARD.**"

Yumagi shudders as the bone-drenching coldness of the Cherubiim's full attention focuses on him. Uriel takes command of Jörmungandr and fifty other Primordial beasts. She sends them rocketing toward Yumagi, hemming him in from all directions.

For the first time since Mephisto's arrival, the Glyphmancer finds himself in dire straits. He hurriedly evokes two Silver Aspects and one Bronze, but the dragons and Titans blast him with terrifying attacks far beyond his level of a mere Demon Emperor.

"Damn! Someone help me!" Yumagi calls out. "These phantoms-!"

Before he can finish his sentence, a blast of holy energy fires at his back, released from one of the Cherubiim's palms.

Yumagi turns to look at it, but after evoking a card just seconds earlier, he has no defensive options left.

"Oh."

_BOOM!!_

The holy energy irradiates his entire body, frying him at the molecular level. Several of Yumagi's hidden trap cards start to activate, only for all of them to shatter and burst into pieces, offering him no protection due to the overwhelming gap between him and his opponent's power.

Moments later, the Glyphmancer lets out only one last silent scream before his body evaporates, leaving behind nothing but ash scattered in the wind.

The other Emperors gasp in horror. Even Fae, who had no love for Yumagi, shudders when she sees the formidable Demon Emperor, someone who previously toyed with the three Archangels in their base forms, shatter into particles of cosmic dust.

Mephisto growls.

"U-Uriel! You bassstardly wench! You left behind neither a body, nor a sssoul! How am I sssupposssed to reussse that Yumagi if you've utterly annihilated hisss exissstence?!"

The Cherubiim ignores him. With Yumagi gone, the morale of demonkind's elites falls even further, making their situation feel truly hopeless.

Even Bael, ever the wisecracker, can only huddle in a hole and pray none of the scary dragons and Titans notice him.

"Shit, shit, shit! I can't believe it! They done offed, uh... that guy! Whoever he was! Wait, didn't his cards remind me of somethin'?"

Bael's eyes slowly lower to the other demons beside him, chief among them Mara, the Baron of Games.

Bael stares at Mara for a few seconds while his brain struggles to fire more than three barely functioning neurons. Eventually he shakes his head.

"Uh, nope. Doesn't ring a bell. Oh well!"

While Bael returns his attention to the battle, Crow and Kristoff finish their patchwork healing sessions. Crow flexes a newly regrown arm, one that cost her the lives of more than five thousand virgin male and female humans from her homeworld.

"We have to help out Mephisto. So long as his Brute still stands-"

Hardly has Crow gotten the words out of her mouth before Mephisto's Brute suddenly roars in pain. She turns to look at it, just in time to see its back explode into a tidal-wave of meat as one of Uriel's battering-clubs pounds against its chest.

"**Hahaha!**" Uriel laughs. "**This is the mightiest mutt the demons can unleash? How pitiful! Brothers, rejoice, for our victory is at hand!!**"

Clouds form in the sky above as the Chrubiim's power surges violently. Uriel lunges all six of her arms at the monster, grabs its limbs and forces them apart, then stabs two of her hands into the hole in its chest.

Seconds later, the Brute roars in pain, while a terrible _ripping_ sound causes everyone within ten miles of the titanic battle to grit their teeth. Uriel tears the Brute in half, then flings the two sides of its body to the Cherubiim's right and left.

_Thoom! Thoom!_

The Brute's severed halves crash against the planet's surface, making the nearby landscape rumble.

After she kills the Brute, Uriel laughs uproariously. "**Oh, what great fun! Long have I awaited a chance to exact my revenge, but never did I think it could taste so delectable!**"

All of the Emperors turn pale with fright. Mephisto's Brute was their one chance at killing the Cherubiim, but despite weakening the Cherubiim's oppressive aura a little bit, even if all the Emperors united their power, they still wouldn't have a chance at killing it.

Crow, once ready to charge into battle, instead sinks to the ground.

"We're... doomed... we can't win."

In all her years, never has Crow felt so utterly helpless. Even if the Hidden Emperors were to gather all the demons from all the Hells, the best she and the other Emperors could do is stymie the Cherubiim's movements for a few minutes at best. 

Even then, it would destroy their tens of millennia of accumulation. All their legacies, built up with the understanding of galactic power levels formed by the battles they've fought since well before the Energy Wars, mean nothing compared to the power of the primordial Cherubiim before them.

Mephisto visibly falters. He slowly levitates backward while forcing his undead Emperor slaves to go on the defense, not wanting to risk their existences.

"Kss... impossssible! My Brute wasss... it wasss sssupposssed to be unkillable! It ssshould have been asss durable asss the Archdemon! How could you defeat it ssso easssily?!"

The Cherubiim slowly marches toward him, proud expressions of conquerors on all the Archangel's faces.

"**As sturdy as the Archdemon? Foolish bastard.**" Raphael says mockingly. "**The Archdemon's power came not from its body's defensive properties, but from its regeneration factor. That lumbering behemoth of thine was slow, weak, and utterly incapable of threatening Our existence. 'Tis no wonder thou hath once again failed to slay us Archangels, three.**"

The Cherubiim's power surges, forcing all of the Emperors to kneel. Without Wolfram around to counterbalance its unchecked aura, the Emperors truly begin to feel as small as ants and as weak as brittle glass.

"**Whether on thy feet or thy knees,**" The Cherubiim counters, "**thou shalt all die a final death, right here and right now.**"

The Cherubiim raises one of its hands. It conjures a ball of supercharged holy energy that begins to spiral in its palm like the center of a galaxy.

A powerful vacuuming sensation grabs at the souls and bodies of every spectator present. As the Cherubiim's orb spins faster and faster, not only does that suction effect increase, but so does the orb's size and cosmic might.

"**In truth, had we wished, we could have destroyed this planet from the start,**" The Cherubiim calmly states. "**But when faced with our most hated enemies, the ones who brought our species to ruin... forgive us if we felt a need to indulge in our baser desires. The terror on thy faces have truly lifted the hearts of our fallen brothers and sisters.**"

The Cherubiim slowly levitates into the air, but even as it pulls away from the Emperors, its soul-suppressing aura only grows stronger. It rises higher and higher, lifting up toward the roiling clouds in the sky.

"**The Creator giveth, and the Creator taketh away,**" The Cherubiim says. "**And much like our Father, so too shalt we giveth and taketh as we please.**"

Emperor Crow's heart palpitates in her chest. She struggles to lift a finger, but the sensation of her soul trying to tear from her body leaves her ten times more exhausted than before. Likewise, the other Emperors suffer similar troubles, with none of them able to move an inch.

"We're doomed... doomed..." Fae coughs. "And all... because... Yumagi couldn't just... finish his stupid fucking game!"

_Foop._

A faint whooshing of air displaces an area not far from Fae. Reflexively, she and the other Emperors flick their heads toward a portal formed in the center of all their positions.

From the portal, a voice speaks.

"Well, what are you waiting for? An invitation?! Hurry up and get inside before it's too late!"

Emperor Yardrat, having fled earlier, opens a gateway for the other Emperors to escape!

The Cherubiim flicks its eyes toward the portal.

"**Dost thou thinketh I shalt stand idly and let my prey escape? Nay.**"

Using one of its other five free hands, the Cherubiim points at the portal and activates Raphael's temporal abilities.

However, during that moment of distraction, a figure flickers into the sky above the Cherubiim, moving at the speed of light.

This figure, tiny compared to the Cherubiim's oversized body, and a blur of motion too fast for anyone but Michael to observe, assumes a humanoid shape.

Michael's eyes flick toward the newcomer.

"**Broth-**"

Michael doesn't get to speak even a single word before that figure instantly accelerates to the speed of light once more, but this time, its trajectory aims directly at the Cherubiim's body.

**BOOOOOM!!**

A planet-shaking shockwave explodes with violent power, smashing the Cherubiim out of the sky. In less than a second, it plummets to the ground and slams into the tectonic plates, sending tens of earthquakes in all directions. Every Emperor gets flung into the air and crashes back to the dirt, only to repeat this motion several times as the planet's soil practically liquefies.

Five full minutes later, the Cherubiim's body flickers. It jumps to its feet, badly injured, while staring in shock at the levitating figure that nearly killed it in one blow.

"**No! Impossible! Bael, the Duke of Pain?!**"

The three Archangels gaze in stupefied horror as a seemingly insignificant Duke hovers in the sky above them, levitating through the use of some unknown and previously unseen elemental ability.

While the Cherubiim struggles to reconcile the appearance of 'Bael' with his inexplicable powers, 'Bael' cracks a huge grin and laughs wildly.

"Me? _Bael_? HAHAHAHA! I think not, Raphael, Uriel, and Michael! No, I'm MUCH scarier than that fat oaf ever was! I am Ose, the mightiest demon to ever exist! I am perfection, superiority, and the embodiment of demonic excellence all in one! I am your worst nightmare!"

Ose clutches Bael's treasured weapon, 'Big Bonk', in her hands. She spins Mjolnir around in its flail-form, casually wielding the ten-ton weapon as if it were a child's toy.

"**Ose?**" Raphael repeats. "**How can that be? We saw Ose already. She is right there!**"

The Archangel of Wisdom points at Bael, who merely scratches his chin awkwardly.

"Uhh... yeah, you kinda never let me get a word in, so... that one's on you, pal."

Ose, likewise, laughs even harder. "Hahahaha! Did you really think you could just teleport me to the other side of the planet and take care of me later? Think again! I returned here not even five seconds after you sent me away! I just decided to sit on the sidelines and see how powerful my 'fellow Emperors' were, but what a pathetic lot they turned out to be!"

Ose casts a disparaging gaze at Emperors Crow, Kristoff, and all the others before returning her attention to the Cherubiim.

"Hear me well, Archangels..."

She continues.

"During the Energy Wars, I alone brought humanity to its knees, despite being a mere Baron."

"When Uriel attacked Hell Harbor, I alone defeated her, despite being a lone Emperor."

"And now, I alone will crush this so-called 'Cherubiim,' despite having fallen to the rank of Duke."

She concludes her speech with one final declaration while spinning Mjolnir over her head like a windmill.

"I am Ose! I am perfection! And most importantly, I am the one who will CRUSH YOU without any **MERCY!!**"
